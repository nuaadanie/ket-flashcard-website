# Journey to the West · Chapter Fifty-Eight: The Meditation Contest

## ①

  To the West, chapter fifty-eight: the Meditation Contest.

> "I challenge you to a Meditation Contest," said the tiger immortal. "Whoever can meditate the longest without moving is the winner."

  Wukong pondered. *I used to meditate a lot,* he thought. *But I haven't done it lately. I'm terrible at sitting still.*

  The Tang Monk stepped forward. "I accept your challenge."

> "Master, are you sure?" asked Wujing.

  The Tang Monk nodded. "I've spent many years meditating. This contest will be easy for me."

  The tiger immortal grinned. "This won't be an ordinary meditation contest. We will float in the air and meditate. Can you do that?"

  Now the Tang Monk looked nervous.

## ②

  But before the monk could answer, Wukong clapped him on the back.

> "Of course the Tang Monk can float in the air and meditate," he replied.

  Then he whispered into the monk's ear: "Don't worry, Master. You just meditate. I'll handle everything else."

> "What are you babbling about?" snapped the tiger immortal. "Let's start the contest!"

## ③

  The tiger immortal and the Tang Monk went in front of the crowd. They sat down with their legs crossed and their palms facing up. They closed their eyes and started meditating. After a moment the tiger immortal slowly floated up into the air.

> "Ah," said Bajie. "Master can't do that. He'll lose the contest."

  "Don't worry," said Wukong. "He just needs some help from me."

## ④

  Wukong recited a spell and then blew toward the Tang Monk. The monk lifted off the ground. His eyes remained closed. Soon he was floating next to the tiger immortal.

> "I think Master will win this contest," said Wujing.

  "I'm pretty good at meditating too," said Bajie. "I could have competed in this contest."

  "Wukong's smart. It would take a lot of magic to lift you, Bajie."

  "The pig's mortified."

## ⑤

> "Master's space is twitching!" said Wujing suddenly.

  "I'll check," said Bajie. "I wonder what's wrong."

> "Maybe he has a flea," said Wujing. "This isn't good. If the monk moves at all, he will lose the contest."

  Wukong looked up at the Tang Monk. "I think he has an itch," said the monkey. "Wait here."

  Wukong became invisible and flew up to the monk. A flea was crawling on the monk's head.

  *Hmm,* thought Wukong. *Why would a flea land on the Tang Monk? He has no hair.* The monkey bit his lip. *The tiger immortal must have used magic to put this flea here. That rotten cheater.*

  Wukong brushed the flea away and scratched the Tang Monk's head. The monk's face stopped twitching.

## ⑥

  *Now I'll teach the tiger immortal a lesson,* thought Wukong.

  The monkey plucked one of his hairs and turned it into a centipede. He dropped the centipede onto the tiger immortal's head. The tiger immortal's ear twitched as the centipede crawled over it. Then the centipede crawled along his mouth. The tiger immortal's lip curled slightly. His eyes wrinkled.

  Next the centipede crawled onto the tiger immortal's nose. The tiger immortal sniffed. And then—

> "Ow!" cried the tiger immortal. "Something bit me!"

  He lost his concentration and fell, hitting the ground with a puff of smoke. When the smoke cleared, a dead tiger lay on the ground. The crowd gasped.

## ⑦

  Wukong and the Tang Monk came back down. The crowd was silent. Daoists and Buddhists all stared at one another. The monkey cleared his throat.

> "The tiger immortal was just an evil tiger spirit. Now that he's gone, I want you Daoists to treat the Buddhists kindly. Everybody should live together peacefully."

# 🇨🇳 第五十八章：打坐比试


## ①

  西游记第五十八集：打坐比赛。

> "我挑战和你们比打坐，"虎力大仙说。"谁打坐不动的时间最长，谁就赢。"

  悟空想了起来。*我以前经常打坐，*他想。*但最近没坐了。我最不擅长安静地坐着了。*

  唐僧走上前。"我接受你的挑战。"

> "师父，您确定吗？"悟净问。

  唐僧点了点头。"我打坐多年，这个比赛对我来说轻而易举。"

  虎力大仙咧嘴一笑。"这可不是普通的打坐比赛。我们要在空中悬浮着打坐。你们能做到吗？"

  这下唐僧看起来紧张了。

## ②

  但没等唐僧回答，悟空拍了拍他的肩膀。

> "唐僧当然能在空中悬浮打坐，"他回答说。

  然后他在唐僧耳边低语："别担心，师父。您只管打坐，其余的交给我。"

> "你们在嘀咕什么？"虎力大仙厉声说。"开始比赛吧！"

## ③

  虎力大仙和唐僧走到人群前面。他们盘腿坐下，掌心向上，闭上眼睛开始打坐。过了一会儿，虎力大仙缓缓飘向了空中。

> "啊，"八戒说。"师父可做不到这个。他会输掉比赛的。"

  "别担心，"悟空说。"他需要我帮点忙。"

## ④

  悟空念了个咒语，然后朝唐僧吹了一口气。唐僧离开地面飘了起来，眼睛依然闭着。很快他就和虎力大仙一样悬浮在空中了。

> "我觉得师父能赢这场比赛，"悟净说。

  "我打坐也挺厉害的，"八戒说。"我也可以参加这场比赛。"

  "悟空聪明得很。八戒，要浮起来你可得费不少法力。"

  八戒满脸窘态。

## ⑤

> "师父的脸在抽动！"悟净突然说。

  "我去看看，"八戒说。"不知道怎么回事。"

> "也许他长了跳蚤，"悟净说。"这可不好。只要师父动一下，他就输了。"

  悟空抬头看着唐僧。"我想他有点痒，"悟空说。"等一下。"

  悟空隐身飞到唐僧身边。一只跳蚤正爬在唐僧的光头上。

  *嗯？*悟空想。*跳蚤怎么会落在唐僧头上？他又没有头发。*悟空咬了咬嘴唇。*一定是虎力大仙用法术把跳蚤放在这的。这个卑鄙的家伙。*

  悟空赶走跳蚤，给唐僧挠了挠头。唐僧的脸不抽动了。

## ⑥

  *现在该教训教训虎力大仙了，*悟空心想。

  他拔下一根毫毛，变成了一条蜈蚣，放到虎力大仙的头上。蜈蚣爬过他的耳朵，他又爬到了他的嘴边。虎力大仙的嘴唇微微扭动，眼睛也眨了眨。

  接着蜈蚣又爬上了他的鼻子。虎力大仙吸了吸鼻子。然后——

> "哎哟！"虎力大仙叫道。"什么东西咬我！"

  他失去了注意力，从空中跌落下来，"砰"的一声摔在地上，化作一团烟雾。烟雾散去后，地上一只死老虎。人群倒吸一口凉气。

## ⑦

  悟空和唐僧降了下来。人群一片寂静。道士和佛教徒面面相觑。悟空清了清嗓子。

> "虎力大仙不过是一只邪恶的虎精。现在他不在了，我希望你们道士善待佛教徒。大家应该和平共处。"
