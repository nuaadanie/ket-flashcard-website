# Journey to the West · Chapter Ninety-Four: Wind and Fart

## ①

> "I hope we reach the Western Paradise soon," said the Tang monk. "This journey has been very dangerous."

  Bajie nodded.

> "That was three demons who tried to pick on me!"

  Everybody laughed, even Bajie.

> "What happened to those demons?" asked Wujing.

> "Buddha took them away," said Wukong. "I'm sure they were punished."

## ②

> "Thank you again for saving us," said the Tang monk.

  Wukong waved his hand.

> "It was nothing, Master. Buddha did most of the work."

  The group felt a strong wind blowing down from the mountains up ahead. A dark fog filled the sky and then disappeared.

> "What caused that wind and fog?" the Tang monk asked nervously.

## ③

> "Relax, Master," said Wukong. "I'm sure it was something harmless. Maybe some small demons. I'll check."

  The monkey flew up the road and around a bend. He heard laughter and looked up. High above the road, a fat monster was sitting on the edge of a cliff. Several little demons sat next to the monster. Wukong flew up to get a closer look. The fat monster appeared to be concentrating.

## ④

  Suddenly a burp as loud as thunder blew down the mountain. The demons tittered and laughed.

> "That was your best burp yet!" cried one demon.

  The monster patted his belly.

> "Yes, I'm proud of that one."

> "Show us the fog too," said another demon.

> "Coming right up," said the monster.

## ⑤

  He covered one nostril with a finger, closed his eyes and blew a fart. Dark fog poured out of his other nostril. The demons clapped and cheered.

  The monster smiled and then -- more wind blew down the mountain. There was a roar of laughter. One demon nearly fell off the cliff.

## ⑥

  Wukong shook his head.

> "I'm not worried about these demons," he said to himself. "They're disgusting, but not dangerous."

  The monkey smiled.

  It does give me an idea, though.

  Wukong returned to his companions, who were waiting alongside the road.

## ⑦

> "Did you see any demons?" asked Wujing.

  Wukong shook his head.

> "The road is safe."

  Then the monkey pulled Bajie aside.

> "Bajie," he whispered. "There's a village up ahead. The people there are preparing a huge feast -- buns, noodles, tofu, steamed buns, and all sorts of delicious food. I thought maybe you'd like to go before us and start eating."

## ⑧

  Bajie licked his lips.

> "Thank you for thinking ahead! I am rather hungry, like that."

  The pig turned to the Tang monk.

> "Master, I think I should go ahead and check it out too. I trust Wukong, of course. But we must protect you, and I'd like to be extra safe."

## ⑨

  The Tang monk smiled.

> "I'm impressed, Bajie. Thank you for doing extra work."

  Bajie winked at Wukong and then headed up the road. The monster burped again and another wind blew. Then blew more fog from one nostril. The demons laughed hysterically.

## ⑩

  Suddenly there was a loud rumbling sound and everyone was silent. The monster patted his big belly.

> "I'm hungry. I'm hungry. I'm hungry."

  The monsters looked at one another.

> "I'm hungry too," said one.

> "It's probably time for lunch," said another.

## ⑪

  The monster stood up.

> "Let's go down to the road and find some people to eat."

  The demons cheered and followed the monster down the mountain. When they got to the road, they made themselves look like people.

> "The next time someone comes along this road, it's lunchtime."

## ⑫

  For a few moments, they waited. Then a large pig came around the bend.

> "Hello!" said Bajie, smiling. "I'm here for the feast."

# 🇨🇳 第九十四章：风屁

## ①

> "希望我们快点到达西天，"唐僧说，"这一路太危险了。"

  八戒点了点头。

> "刚才有三个妖怪想找我麻烦！"

  大家都笑了，连八戒也笑了。

> "那些妖怪怎么样了？"悟净问。

> "佛祖把他们收走了，"悟空说，"我相信他们受到了惩罚。"

## ②

> "再次感谢你们救了我们，"唐僧说。

  悟空摆了摆手。

> "没什么，师父。佛祖帮了大忙。"

  队伍感到一阵强风从前方山上吹下来。一团黑雾弥漫天空，然后消失了。

> "那阵风和雾是怎么回事？"唐僧紧张地问。

## ③

> "别紧张，师父，"悟空说，"应该没什么危险的。也许是小妖怪。我去看看。"

  猴子飞到路那头，拐过一个弯。他听到了笑声，抬头一看。路的正上方，一个胖怪物坐在悬崖边上。旁边坐着几个小妖怪。悟空飞近一看，胖怪物似乎在集中注意力。

## ④

  突然一个雷鸣般的屁朝山下吹来。小妖怪们咯咯笑了起来。

> "这是你最响的一个屁了！"一个小妖怪大喊。

  怪物拍了拍肚子。

> "是的，我也很满意。"

> "再给我们表演放雾吧，"另一个妖怪说。

> "没问题，"怪物说。

## ⑤

  他用手指捂住一个鼻孔，闭上眼睛放了一个屁。黑色的雾从另一个鼻孔里冒出来。妖怪们拍手叫好。

  怪物笑了笑——又是一阵大风朝山下吹来。笑声震耳欲聋，一个小妖怪差点从悬崖上掉下去。

## ⑥

  悟空摇了摇头。

> "我不担心这些妖怪，"他自言自语道，"他们很恶心，但没有危险。"

  猴子微微一笑。

  不过这倒给了我一个主意。

  悟空回到同伴身边，他们正在路边等着。

## ⑦

> "你看到妖怪了吗？"悟净问。

  悟空摇了摇头。

> "路上安全。"

  然后猴子把八戒拉到一旁。

> "八戒，"他小声说，"前面有个村子。村民们正在准备一桌大餐——馒头、面条、豆腐、包子，各种各样的美食。我想你可能想先去吃一点。"

## ⑧

  八戒舔了舔嘴唇。

> "谢谢你替我着想！我还真有点饿了。"

  猪转向唐僧。

> "师父，我觉得我应该先去探探路。我当然信得过悟空，但我们得保护您，我想再谨慎一些。"

## ⑨

  唐僧微微一笑。

> "八戒，你真让我刮目相看。谢谢你多做事。"

  八戒冲悟空眨了眨眼，然后上了路。怪物又放了一个屁，又是一阵风吹来，然后从一个鼻孔里冒出更多的雾。妖怪们笑得前仰后合。

## ⑩

  突然传来一声巨响，所有人都安静了。怪物拍了拍大肚子。

> "我饿了。我饿了。我饿了。"

  妖怪们面面相觑。

> "我也饿了，"一个小妖怪说。

> "大概到午饭时间了，"另一个说。

## ⑪

  怪物站了起来。

> "我们到路上去找几个人来吃吧。"

  妖怪们欢呼着跟随怪物下了山。到了路上，他们变成了人的模样。

> "下次有人路过这条路，就是午饭时间了。"

## ⑫

  等了一会儿，一个大猪从弯道处走了过来。

> "大家好！"八戒微笑着说，"我来赴宴了。"
