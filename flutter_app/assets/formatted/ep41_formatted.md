# Journey to the West · Chapter Forty-One: A Trade

## ①

  Wukong was angry at the guard at the southern gate of heaven.

> "Why can't you close the gate and dim the stars?"

  The guard looked nervous.

> "I could do that only if I have permission from the Jade Emperor."

> "I'm trying to save the Tang monk!" shouted Wukong. "But a demon is planning to eat him!"

> "I'm sorry," said the guard. "But I can't help you!"

  Wukong's eyes became wide and angry. He pulled the iron bar out of his ear.

## ②

  A messenger ran into the cloud palace and bowed.

> "Sun Wukong is at the southern gate of heaven, Your Majesty. He's going to destroy heaven if we don't do what he says."

  The Jade Emperor slammed his fist down.

> "That rotten monkey! What does he want?"

> "He wants us to close the gate and dim the stars," said the messenger.

> "Why?" asked the Emperor.

> "He wants to pretend heaven got sucked into a gourd," said the messenger.

  The Jade Emperor looked confused.

> "Why does he want to do that?"

> "He didn't say," said the messenger. "He just said he's trying to save the Tang monk."

  The Jade Emperor sighed.

> "You have my permission to close the gate and dim the stars. I don't want any more trouble from that monkey."

## ③

  The messenger returned to the gate.

> "We will close the gate and dim the stars," he said.

> "Good," said Wukong. He put the iron bar back in his ear.

  The sky went dark. Wukong had returned to his body, which was still in disguise as a demon.

> "Now I will call to heaven," said the monkey to the demon. "The whole place will get sucked into this gourd." He held up the gourd. "Heaven!"

  Right away the sky went dark.

> "Amazing!" cried the demon.

> "All of heaven is now in that gourd," said Wukong. "All the stars, planets, spirits, and gods are in here. Even the cloud palaces."

> "With the Jade Emperor too?"

## ④

> "Now I will let heaven back out." He held up the gourd. "Heaven, return!"

  The sky lit up again.

> "It works!" said the demon.

> "Let's trade gourds." He handed his gourd to Wukong. Wukong gave the fake gourd to the demon. Then he made the real gourd tiny and hid it in his robe.

> "I'll try to capture heaven now," said the demon. He held up the gourd. "Heaven!"

  Nothing happened.

> "Heaven!" said the demon again. "Heaven! Heaven!"

  He looked at the gourd.

> "Lord! This gourd isn't working! What's wrong with it?"

  Wukong laughed and made the fake gourd disappear. Then he made himself invisible.

> "I was tricked!" cried the demon. "I must tell Golden Horn!"

## ⑤

  The demon ran off and Wukong followed. Soon the road went up a mountain. The demon ran into a cave. Wukong made himself visible and stormed into the cave.

  Looking around at the group of demons, he pulled out his iron bar.

> "Return my friends at once!" he shouted. "Or I'll clobber all of you!"

  The small demons cried out. They ran in every direction, tripping over one another.

  Wukong swung his iron bar at Golden Horn. The demon stepped out of the way and pulled a golden rope from his sleeve. He swung the rope and it stretched toward Wukong.

  The monkey tried to get out of the way, but the rope snapped at him. It looped around his waist. The more Wukong tried to wriggle free, the tighter the rope got.

> "Ah!"

  Golden Horn laughed.

> "You'll never escape the golden rope. Tonight I will dine on the Tang monk!"

# 🇨🇳 第四十一章：交易


## ①

  悟空对南天门的守卫很生气。

> "你为什么不能关上天门、遮住星光？"

  守卫看起来很紧张。

> "那得玉皇大帝允许才行呀。"

> "我要去救唐和尚！"悟空大喊道，"有个妖怪要吃掉他！"

> "对不起，"守卫说道，"我帮不了你！"

  悟空气得双眼圆睁。他从耳朵里掏出了铁棒。

## ②

  一名使者跑进云宫跪下禀报。

> "陛下，孙悟空正在南天门，他说如果我们不听他的，他就要毁灭天宫。"

  玉皇大帝一掌拍了下去。

> "可恶的猴子！他要干什么？"

> "他要我们关上天门、遮住星光，"使者回答。

> "为什么？"玉帝问。

> "他想假装天宫被吸进葫芦里了，"使者说。

  玉皇大帝一脸困惑。

> "他干嘛要这么做？"

> "他没说，"使者回答，"他只说是要去救唐和尚。"

  玉皇大帝叹了口气。

> "好吧，准许他关上天门、遮住星光。我可不想再被那只猴子折腾了。"

## ③

  使者回到南天门。

> "我们答应关上天门、遮住星光，"他说。

> "好的，"悟空说。他把铁棒放回耳朵里。

  天空暗了下来。悟空回到了自己的身体，他那时仍装成小妖的模樣。

> "现在我要呼唤天宫了，"悟空对小妖说，"整个天宫都会被吸进这个葫芦。"他举起葫芦大喊："天宫！"

  天空一下就黑了。

> "太厉害了！"小妖惊叫道。

> "整个天宫都装进葫芦里了，"悟空说，"所有的星星、行星、仙人和神明都在这里面，连云宫也在。"

> "玉皇大帝也在吗？"

## ④

> "现在我把天宫放出来。"他举起葫芦喊道："天宫，回来！"

  天空又亮了。

> "真的灵验！"小妖称赞道。

> "我们换葫芦吧。"小妖把葫芦递给了悟空。悟空把假葫芦给了他，然后将真葫芦变小藏进袍子里。

> "现在我来试试吸天宫，"小妖说。他举起葫芦喊道："天宫！"

  什么都没发生。

> "天宫！"小妖又喊，"天宫！天宫！"

  他看了看葫芦。

> "天哪！这葫芦不灵了！怎么回事？"

  悟空大笑一声，让假葫芦消失了，然后隐了身。

> "我上当了！"小妖大哭，"我得去报告金角大王！"

## ⑤

  小妖拔腿就跑，悟空跟在后面。不一会儿路通到了一座山上，小妖跑进了一个洞府。悟空恢复了真身，大摇大摆地闯了进去。

  他环顾四周的群妖，抽出铁棒。

> "马上放了我朋友！"他大喝道，"不然把你们统统打死！"

  小妖们吓得尖叫起来，到处乱跑，互相绊倒。

  悟空挥棒朝金角打去。金角侧身躲开，从袖中取出一条金绳一甩，金绳伸展开来朝悟空飞去。

  悟空想躲开，但金绳已缠上他的腰身。他越是挣扎，绳子勒得越紧。

> "哎哟！"

  金角哈哈大笑。

> "你休想逃出金绳的缠绕！今晚我就吃唐和尚！"
