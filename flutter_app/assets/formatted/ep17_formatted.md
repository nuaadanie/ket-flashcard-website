# Journey to the West · Chapter Seventeen: A Dragon in the River

## ①

  To the West — Chapter 17: A dragon in the river.

  "That dragon ate my horse!" cried the Tang monk. "How will I ever get to the western paradise? There are so many dangers along the way." He fell to his knees in despair.

  Wukong laughed. "Master, you worry too much. There will be many dangers on your journey. But you have me, the Monkey King, to protect you. All of heaven's soldiers couldn't defeat me. So why worry about a dragon?"

## ②

  The monkey stepped into the water and pulled out his iron rod. "I'll get the dragon to come out of the water."

  He looked down at the water and shouted, "Dragon! Get up here right now!"

  The dragon rose from the water. "Oh, there you are. Who are you?"

  "It doesn't matter who I am," shouted Wukong. "Give that horse back!"

## ③

  The monkey attacked the dragon with his iron rod. The dragon spun around, blocking the rod with his tail. Then with a flash, he dove back under the water.

  Wukong jumped his foot and yelled at the river, "Coward!"

  "What will we do?" asked the Tang monk. "I have no horse."

  "And we can't cross the river with that dragon in there," said Wukong. "I'll try another plan."

## ④

  The monkey stirred the water with his iron rod. The water became rough; waves crashed and churned. The water dragged mud up from the river bottom. Soon the river was brown and muddy.

  The dragon appeared again. "What on earth are you doing now?" he cried. "I'm trying to take a nap."

  "Give us back our horse!" demanded Wukong.

  "I can't," cried the dragon. "It's in my stomach."

## ⑤

  The monkey attacked the dragon again. But once more the dragon disappeared under the water. For more than three hours, Wukong yelled at the river. He yelled all kinds of insults, but the dragon would not come back up.

  Wukong climbed up the river bank and sat down next to the Tang monk. "That dragon is tough. I'm not afraid of him, but I can't beat him either. I need help."

  "Who can help you?" asked the Tang monk.

## ⑥

  Wukong thought for a moment. "Guanyin told me I can ask her for help if I need it."

  "Yes," said the Tang monk. "She told me the same thing."

  Wukong felt the headband and shot a look at the monk. "She already did help you. She gave you this tight headband." He sighed. "But it doesn't matter now. You wait here. I'll be back soon."

## ⑦

  Wukong shot into the air and flew south. Soon he came to Mount Potalaka in the South Sea. Guanyin was in a garden next to her pagoda.

  "Hello, Wukong," she said. "How is the journey going? I see you're wearing the headband."

  "Yes, thanks to your dirty tricks," said the monkey.

  Guanyin smiled. "You need that headband, Wukong. You are too violent and cruel."

## ⑧

  Wukong rolled his eyes. "Fine. I'm here because I have a problem. We reached a wide river with a dragon in it. The dragon ate our horse."

  "Did you tell the dragon that you're traveling with the Tang monk?" asked Guanyin.

  "No," said the monkey.

  "Why?" Guanyin sighed. "That dragon was waiting for you. When I met him, he was being punished for setting fire to his father's palace. I told him to help the Tang monk reach the western paradise. That way, he can earn forgiveness."

  Wukong scratched his head. "It's too late for that now. He won't come out of the water."

# 🇨🇳 第十七章：黑河遇龙


## ①

  往西去 —— 第十七集：河里的龙。

  "那条龙吃了我的马！"唐僧喊道，"我怎样才能到达西方极乐世界呢？一路上有这么多危险。"他绝望地跪了下来。

  悟空笑道："师父，你太担心了。你的旅途上会有很多危险。但有我齐天大圣保护你。全天的兵将都打不败我。所以何必担心一条龙呢？"

## ②

  悟空跨进水里，掏出他的铁棒，"我去叫那龙从水里出来。"

  他低头朝水里喊道："龙！马上给我上来！"

  龙从水里钻了出来，"哦，你来啦。你是谁？"

  "我是谁不重要，"悟空喊道，"把我的马还来！"

## ③

  悟空用铁棒攻击龙。龙转身用尾巴挡住了铁棒，然后一闪身又潜回了水里。

  悟空跺了跺脚，对着河面大喊："胆小鬼！"

  "我们怎么办呢？"唐僧问，"我没有马了。"

  "那龙在水里，我们也没法过河，"悟空说，"我再想个办法。"

## ④

  悟空用铁棒搅动河水。河水变得汹涌起来，波浪翻滚。河底的泥沙被搅了上来，很快河水变得又浑又黄。

  龙又出现了，"你到底在干什么？"他喊道，"我想睡个觉呢！"

  "把我们的马还来！"悟空命令道。

  "不行啊，"龙喊道，"它在我肚子里呢。"

## ⑤

  悟空再次攻击龙。但龙又一次潜入了水中。悟空对着河面喊了一个多时辰。他骂了各种各样的话，但龙就是不出来。

  悟空爬上河岸，在唐僧身边坐了下来，"那条龙真厉害。我不怕他，但也打不过他。我需要帮忙。"

  "你能找谁帮忙呢？"唐僧问。

## ⑥

  悟空想了想，"观音说过，需要帮忙可以找她。"

  "对，"唐僧说，"她也跟我这么说过。"

  悟空摸了摸紧箍咒，看了唐僧一眼，"她已经帮过你了。她给了你这个紧箍。"他叹了口气，"不过算了。你在这里等着，我很快就回来。"

## ⑦

  悟空腾空而起，向南飞去。不一会儿就到了南海的普陀山。观音正在宝塔旁的花园里。

  "你好，悟空，"她说，"旅途怎么样呀？我看到你戴着紧箍了。"

  "是啊，多亏你的鬼把戏，"悟空说。

  观音笑了，"你需要那个紧箍，悟空。你太过暴力和残忍了。"

## ⑧

  悟空翻了个白眼，"好吧。我来是因为出了点问题。我们到了一条大河，河里有条龙。龙把我们的马吃了。"

  "你告诉那龙你是和唐僧一起旅行的吗？"观音问。

  "没有，"悟空说。

  "为什么呢？"观音叹了叹气，"那条龙一直在等你。我遇见他的时候，他正因烧毁他父亲的宫殿而受罚。我告诉他帮助唐僧到达西方极乐世界，这样他就能得到宽恕。"

  悟空挠了挠头，"现在说这些已经晚了。他不肯从水里出来。"
