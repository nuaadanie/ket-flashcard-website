# Journey to the West · Chapter Forty-Two: Sun Kong Wu

## ①

  Wukong struggled to free himself from the golden rope. The gourd fell from his robe.

> "My magic gourd!" said Golden Horn, picking it up.

  He dragged Wukong to a dark room in the cave and locked him in.

  Right away, Wukong made himself tiny and escaped. He flew down a tunnel and squeezed through the cave's main door. Once he was outside, he returned to his normal size. He banged on the cave door.

> "Open up right now!"

  The door flew open and Golden Horn appeared.

## ②

> "Sun Wukong!" shouted the demon. "How did you escape?"

> "I'm not Sun Wukong," the monkey lied.

> "What? Do you look just like him?" said Golden Horn.

> "Yes, I do," said Wukong. "Sun Wukong is my twin brother. My name is Sun Wu Kong."

> "Why are you here, Sun Wu Kong?" asked Golden Horn.

> "I know you captured my brother," said Wukong. "Set him free."

  Golden Horn laughed.

> "I'm not going to do that."

> "Oh, that's too bad," said Wukong, pretending to feel bad. "I was hoping to solve this problem without violence." He took the iron bar out of his ear.

## ③

  Golden Horn had been holding the gourd behind his back the whole time. He quickly held it up and shouted.

> "Sun Wu Kong, shoo!"

  Wukong was sucked into the gourd. Golden Horn put a cap on the gourd and went back into his cave.

  Inside the gourd, Wukong stamped his foot.

> "How will I get out of here? I know I won't turn into liquid because I'm going to live forever. But I still have to save my friends."

  He tapped his chin.

> "I have an idea."

  He plucked a hair and made it look almost exactly like himself. There was just one difference. The fake Wukong had no legs. Next, Wukong made a hair look like a puddle. He placed the fake Wukong on the puddle. Now it looked as if the fake Wukong's legs had turned into liquid.

## ④

  Then the monkey made himself very, very tiny. Wukong went to the opening of the gourd and shouted.

> "Oh no! My legs turned into liquid!"

  The gourd shook as Golden Horn removed the cap. The tiny Wukong leaped out unseen. Golden Horn peered into the gourd and saw the fake Wukong in the puddle.

> "Ah! The monkey's legs did turn into liquid. Soon this entire body will be liquid. Ha! Ha!" Golden Horn laughed again and closed the gourd.

  He put the gourd down and started eating.

  The real Wukong returned to his normal size and then made himself look like a demon. He walked over to Golden Horn.

> "Hello, sir," said Wukong. "Are you enjoying your lunch?"

> "Yes," said Golden Horn.

## ⑤

  Wukong picked up the gourd.

> "Is Sun Wu Kong still in here?"

> "Yes," said Golden Horn. "He will turn completely into liquid soon. Stop asking all your questions. I'm trying to eat my lunch."

  Golden Horn tilted his head to take a drink. Wukong slipped the gourd into his robe. He quickly made a fake gourd with one of his hairs. He put it on the table and then ran outside.

  Wukong changed back into himself and then banged on the cave door again.

  Golden Horn threw the door open.

> "My name is Kong Sun Wu!" said Wukong. "You captured my nephew. Return him at once!"

  Golden Horn held up the fake gourd.

> "Kong Sun Wu!"

> "Cried Wukong, interrupting the demon. I have a gourd that looks just like that."

# 🇨🇳 第四十二章：真假悟空


## ①

  悟空挣扎着想要挣脱金绳。这时葫芦从他袍子里掉了出来。

> "我的宝葫芦！"金角说着捡了起来。

  他把悟空拖进洞里一间黑屋子，锁上了门。

  但悟空立刻将自己变小，逃了出去。他顺着一条通道飞了出去，从洞府大门挤了出去。到了外面，他恢复正常大小，用力拍打着洞门。

> "快开门！"

  洞门打开了，金角出现在面前。

## ②

> "孙悟空！"妖怪大喊，"你怎么逃出来的？"

> "我不是孙悟空，"悟空撒了个谎。

> "什么？你怎么跟他长得一模一样？"金角说道。

> "是啊，"悟空说，"孙悟空是我的孪生兄弟。我叫孙空行。"

> "你来这里干什么，孙空行？"金角问。

> "我知道你抓了我哥哥，"悟空说，"快放了他。"

  金角哈哈大笑。

> "我才不放呢。"

> "唉，太可惜了，"悟空假装难过地说，"我本想不动武就解决此事。"他说完从耳朵里掏出了铁棒。

## ③

  金角一直把葫芦藏在身后，此刻连忙举起，大喊一声。

> "孙空行，收！"

  悟空被吸进了葫芦。金角盖好盖子，回到洞里去了。

  在葫芦里面，悟空急得直跺脚。

> "我怎么出去呢？我知道自己不会化成液体，因为我是长生不老的。可我还得去救朋友们啊。"

  他想了想。

> "我有办法了。"

  他拔下一根毫毛，变得跟自己几乎一模一样，唯一不同的是，假悟空没有腿。然后悟空又用一根毫毛变成一滩水。他把假悟空放在水滩上，看起来就好像假悟空的腿已经化成液体了一样。

## ④

  然后悟空把自己变得非常非常小。他走到葫芦口大声喊。

> "不好了！我的腿化成液体啦！"

  金角揭开盖子，葫芦口透进了光。小小的悟空趁机跳了出去，没有被发现。金角往里看去，看见假悟空站在水滩中。

> "哈哈！这猴子的腿真的化成液体了。很快全身都会化掉！哈哈！"金角大笑着盖回葫芦，把葫芦放下，开始吃饭。

  真正的悟空恢复正常大小，然后变成了一个妖怪的模样。他走到金角跟前。

> "大王好，"悟空说，"您午饭吃得可好？"

> "不错，"金角说。

## ⑤

  悟空拿起葫芦。

> "孙空行还在里面吗？"

> "在呢，"金角说，"他很快就要全部化成液体了。别再问我问题了，我要吃饭。"

  金角歪过头去喝水。悟空趁机把葫芦塞进袍子里，又用一根毫毛变出一个假葫芦放在桌上，然后跑到了外面。

  悟空恢复了自己的模样，又去敲打洞门。

  金角一把将门拉开。

> "我叫空悟孙！"悟空说，"你抓了我侄子，快放了他！"

  金角举起假葫芦。

> "空悟孙！"

  悟空打断妖怪的话，大喊道："我跟你长得一模一样的葫芦！"
