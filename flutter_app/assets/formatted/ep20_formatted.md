# Journey to the West · Chapter Twenty: The Black Bear Spirit

## ①

  Wukong glared at the two scared priests. "The robe is gone!" he cried. "Where did it go?"

  "We don't know," said one priest.

  "We tried to find it," added the other. "The robe was in a room that didn't get burned at all. But it's not there anymore."

  His iron rod instantly appeared from inside his ear. "Grow," commanded Wukong. The monkey swung it toward the two priests.

## ②

  Before the rod hit them, it dropped from the monkey's hands. Wukong fell to the ground crying in pain.

  "It hurts! It hurts!" he cried.

  The Tang monk was quietly reciting the tight headband spell. Wukong rolled around kicking and screaming. The two priests jumped up and backed away.

  "Stop reciting the spell!" Wukong yelled at the Tang monk. "I'm begging you! I won't hurt the priests."

  The Tang monk stopped and Wukong stood up. The monkey took a deep breath and put the iron rod back in his ear.

## ③

  The Tang monk looked at the two priests. "You really don't know where the robe is?"

  "No!" said one priest. "The head priest ran away last night. He didn't have it with him."

  Wukong tapped his chin. "A monster or demon might have stolen it. Are there any monsters or demons living near here?"

  "Some people believe there's a monster in the mountains nearby," said the other priest. "They say it's a black bear spirit."

  "I'll go see if the bear took the robe," said Wukong. "I'll be back soon."

## ④

  Wukong leaped into the air and landed up in the mountains. He walked for a bit and then heard voices. Peeking over a boulder, he saw a group of spirits talking. One was a large black bear.

  The bear laughed. "The monastery was on fire last night. I went down to check it out and found a wonderful Buddhist robe. I think it's a Buddhist treasure. Anyway, I stole it. So tonight I'm throwing a feast to celebrate."

## ⑤

  When Wukong heard the other spirits laugh, he was furious. He jumped over the boulder and landed in the middle of the group.

  "Give back the robe right now!" he cried. He pulled out his iron rod. "Or I'll destroy all of you."

  The spirits fled in different directions. Wukong chased the bear until they reached the cave entrance. The bear ran inside and slammed the door shut.

## ⑥

  Wukong pounded on the cave door. "Come out here right now, or I'll smash this door down."

  The door flew open. The bear stuck out, holding a lance.

  "Oh, dear, you pound on my door and yell at me," he cried.

  "You stole my master's robe," said Wukong.

  "I can do whatever I want," said the bear. "I am the Black Wind King. I'm the most powerful monster in these mountains."

## ⑦

  "I am Sun Wukong, King of the Fruit and Flower Mountain," said the monkey. "I caused lots of trouble in heaven 500 years ago. The Jade Emperor's entire army couldn't defeat me."

  The Black Wind King snorted. "You don't impress me." He jabbed his lance at Wukong.

  The monkey blocked the lance with his iron rod. They tumbled and rolled downhill as the two warriors fought.

## ⑧

  After an hour of fighting, the Black Wind King held up a hand. "I need to take a break. It's time for my lunch."

  Wukong's jaw fell open. "What?"

  Before Wukong could say anything else, the bear ran back into his cave. The door slammed shut.

# 🇨🇳 第二十章：黑熊精


## ①

  悟空瞪着两个惊恐的僧人，"袈裟不见了！"他喊，"到哪去了？"

  "我们也不知道，"一个僧人说。

  "我们找过了，"另一个说，"袈裟原来在一间没被烧的房子里，已经不在了。"

  他的铁棒立刻从耳朵里冒了出来。"长！"悟空一声令下，举起铁棒打向两个僧人。

## ②

  铁棒还没打中他们，就从悟空手中掉了下来。悟空倒在地上，痛苦地叫喊。

  "好痛！好痛！"他大喊。

  唐僧正在默念紧箍咒。悟空在地上翻滚，又踢又叫。两个僧人跳起来后退。

  "别再念咒了！"悟空对唐僧大喊，"我求你了！我不会伤害这些僧人了。"

  唐僧停住了口，悟空站了起来。猴子深吸一口气，把铁棒收回耳朵里。

## ③

  唐僧看着两个僧人，"你们真的不知道袈裟在哪？"

  "不知道！"一个僧人说，"主僧昨天晚上跑了，袈裟不在他身上。"

  悟空摸着下巴想了想，"可能是妖怪或妖精偷走了。这附近有妖怪吗？"

  "有人说附近山里有妖怪，"另一个僧人说，"听说是一只黑熊精。"

  "我去看看是不是那熊偷了袈裟，"悟空说，"我很快就回来。"

## ④

  悟空腾身一躍，落到了山上。他走了一阵，听到了说话声。从一块大石头后面探头一看，他看到一群妖精在聊天，其中一个是一只大黑熊。

  熊哈哈大笑，"寺院昨天夜里起了火。我下去察看，发现了一件了不起的佛门袈裟。我猜那是佛门至宝。总之，我把它偷了。今晚我要大摆宴席庆祝一番。"

## ⑤

  悟空听到众妖精的笑声，怒火中烧。他跳过大石头，落在众人中间。

  "马上把袈裟还回来！"他吼道，掏出铁棒，"不然我就把你们全部消灭。"

  众妖精四散逃窜。悟空追着黑熊一直追到洞口。黑熊跑进洞里，把门关得紧紧的。

## ⑥

  悟空用力捶打洞门，"马上出来，不然我就把这扇门砸了。"

  门突然开了。黑熊窜了出来，手里拿着一杆长枪。

  "哎呀，你又敲门又喊的，"黑熊说。

  "你偷了我师父的袈裟，"悟空说。

  "我想干什么就干什么，"黑熊说，"我是黑风大王，这座山里最厉害的妖怪。"

## ⑦

  "我是花果山美猴王孙悟空，"悟空说，"五百年前我在天宮大闹了一场，玉皇大帝的全部军队都打不过我。"

  黑风大王哼了一声，"少来吓唬我。"他举枪朝悟空刺去。

  悟空用铁棒挡住长枪。两人翻滚扭打，从山上一直打到山下。

## ⑧

  打了一个时辰后，黑风大王举起一只手，"我要歇一歇，该吃午饭了。"

  悟空惊得下巴都快掉了，"什么？"

  悟空还没来得及说什么，黑熊就逃回洞里去了，门又紧紧关上了。
