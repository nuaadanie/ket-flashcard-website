# Journey to the West · Chapter Eleven: Trouble in Heaven

## ①

  The soldiers quickly tied up Wukong and brought him up to Heaven.

  "I want Sun Wukong destroyed," said the Jade Emperor.

  The soldiers tied Wukong to a pillar in a courtyard. They struck him with swords. But his body was as hard as a diamond. Wukong laughed.

  "Your swords can't hurt me!" he said.

## ②

  The Jade Emperor turned to a messenger.

  "Bring in the fire gods!" he ordered.

  Several fire gods entered the courtyard and threw balls of flame at Wukong. The courtyard filled with smoke. But when the smoke cleared, not one hair on Wukong's body was burned.

  "Thank you," Wukong laughed. "I was feeling a little cold!"

## ③

  "Gather the Thunder Gods," said the Jade Emperor.

  Several Thunder Gods entered the courtyard. The Jade Emperor smiled as lightning bolts shot towards Wukong. But even the lightning couldn't harm the monkey.

  "That tickles!" said Wukong with a smile.

  The Jade Emperor shook his head. "It seems that Wukong can't be destroyed."

  "That's because he ate all the peaches," said Laozi. "Just one peach would make someone live forever. He ate every peach in the garden."

  The Emperor frowned. "Then how will we get rid of him?"

  "I have an idea," said Laozi. "I'll put him in the large pot I use for making potions. Maybe we can burn him up slowly."

  "Perfect," said the Jade Emperor.

## ④

  Soldiers brought Wukong to a room in Laozi's palace. A large pot sat in the middle of the room. Laozi put Wukong into the pot and closed the lid. Then he started a fire under the pot. Day and night, he attended the fire. He kept it burning for forty-nine days.

  "Wukong must have been destroyed by now," thought Laozi.

  He slowly took the lid off the pot. Smoke poured from the opening, and the pot fell over. Burning pieces of coal flew everywhere. Wukong staggered out, and he was coughing.

  "That smoke was horrible!" he cried.

  He pushed Laozi, knocking the old sage onto his back. Wukong pulled out his iron bar and smashed everything in the room.

## ⑤

  "Somebody stop him!" cried Laozi.

  Wukong ran out of Laozi's palace. Gods and spirits fled as Wukong smashed everything in his path. Laozi ran into the cloud palace.

  "Your Majesty, Wukong is causing trouble all over Heaven!" he said.

  "What?" cried the Emperor. "He's alive?"

  "Yes," said Laozi. "I took the lid off the pot because I thought he was burned up. But the fire didn't hurt him at all."

  "This is an outrage," said the Jade Emperor. "What are we going to do with that monkey?"

  The Emperor thought for a moment. "We must ask Buddha for help," he said.

  He turned to a messenger. "Go to the Western Paradise quickly. See if Buddha can come to help us."

## ⑥

  Flying as fast as he could, the messenger headed toward the Western Paradise.

  Meanwhile, everybody hid behind doors as Wukong ran through Heaven. Using his iron bar, he smashed doors and windows and knocked over statues. Suddenly, he stopped. Before him was a huge figure, glowing with golden light.

  "Who are you?" said Wukong.

  "I am Buddha," said the figure. "And who are you, and why are you causing all this trouble?"

  Wukong smiled. "I am Sun Wukong, King of the Mountain of Fruit and Flowers. I should be the King of Heaven too. I am going to the Cloud Palace to tell the Jade Emperor to leave. If he doesn't, I'll never stop causing trouble!"

# 🇨🇳 第十一章：大闹天宫

## ①

  天兵天将迅速绑住悟空，把他押上了天庭。

  "我要把孙悟空除掉。"玉皇大帝说。

  士兵们把悟空绑在庭院的一根柱子上，用刀剑砍他。可他的身体坚硬如钻石。悟空哈哈大笑。

  "你们的刀枪伤不了我！"他说。

## ②

  玉皇大帝转身对一名传令官说。

  "去把火神请来！"他下令道。

  几位火神进入庭院，朝悟空投掷火球。庭院里顿时浓烟滚滚。可是当烟雾散去时，悟空身上一根毫毛也没有被烧伤。

  "谢谢你们，"悟空笑着说，"我正觉得有点冷呢！"

## ③

  "把雷神们都召来。"玉皇大帝说。

  几位雷神进入庭院。玉皇大帝微笑着，只见一道道闪电射向悟空。然而，即便是闪电也伤不了这只猴子。

  "真舒服呀！"悟空笑着说。

  玉皇大帝摇了摇头。"看来悟空是消灭不了了。"

  "那是因为他吃了所有的仙桃，"太上老君说，"只要吃一个仙桃就能长生不老。他把园里的仙桃全都吃光了。"

  玉帝皱起眉头。"那我们怎么才能除掉他呢？"

  "我有一个主意，"太上老君说，"我把他放进我炼丹用的大炉子里。也许我们可以慢慢把他炼死。"

  "太好了。"玉皇大帝说。

## ④

  士兵们把悟空带到太上老君的宫殿里。屋子中间放着一口大炉子。太上老君把悟空放进炉中，盖上盖子，然后在炉子下面生起火来。他日夜不停地添柴加火，一连烧了四十九天。

  "悟空这下一定被消灭了吧，"太上老君心想。

  他缓缓揭开炉盖。烟雾从炉口涌出，大炉子翻倒在地，燃烧的煤块四处飞溅。悟空摇摇晃晃地爬了出来，不停地咳嗽着。

  "这烟太呛人了！"他喊道。

  他一把推倒太上老君，从炉中翻出身来。悟空掏出金箍棒，把屋里的东西砸了个稀巴烂。

## ⑤

  "快拦住他！"太上老君大喊道。

  悟空冲出太上老君的宫殿。众神和天将纷纷逃窜，悟空所到之处，一切都被他砸得粉碎。太上老君急忙跑进凌霄宝殿。

  "陛下，悟空在天庭到处大闹！"他说。

  "什么？"玉帝惊叫道，"他还活着？"

  "是的，"太上老君说，"我以为他已经被炼化了，就揭开了炉盖。谁知道那火根本伤不到他。"

  "真是岂有此理！"玉皇大帝说，"我们拿这只猴子到底怎么办？"

  玉帝沉思了一会儿。"我们只好请如来佛祖来帮忙了，"他说。

  他转身对一名传令官说。"快去西天极乐世界，看看如来佛祖能不能来帮我们。"

## ⑥

  传令官以最快的速度向西天极乐世界飞去。

  这时，众人都躲在门后，悟空在天庭横冲直撞。他挥起金箍棒，砸门破窗，推倒神像。突然，他停了下来。在他面前出现了一个巨大的身影，浑身散发着金色的光芒。

  "你是谁？"悟空问。

  "我是如来佛祖，"那身影说，"你又是谁？为什么在这里大闹天宫？"

  悟空微微一笑。"我是孙悟空，花果山之王。我也应该做这天庭之王。我要去凌霄宝殿告诉玉皇大帝，让他把位子让给我。如果他不让，我就永远不停手！"
