# Journey to the West · Chapter Forty-Seven: Two Tang Monks

## ①

  The Taoist had vanished. Wukong ran outside and shot into the air. He looked in every direction.

> "Where did the Taoist go?" asked the monkey.

  Then he heard a shout from inside the palace.

> "There are two Tang monks!" cried Bajie.

  Wukong flew back inside. Two Tang monks stood side by side.

> "We can't tell them apart," said Wujing. "They look exactly alike."

> "Which one of you is the real Tang monk?" asked Wukong.

> "I am," said one.

  The other monk glared at the first one.

> "No, I am!"

  The first monk looked at Wukong.

> "Don't believe him, Wukong. I am the real Tang monk."

> "Don't believe him!" said the other monk. "I am the real Tang monk!"

## ②

  Angry, Wukong pulled out his iron bar.

> "Which one of you is the real Tang monk!"

  Both Tang monks fell to their knees, shaking with fear.

> "Don't hit me!" they both cried.

  The monkey sighed. He turned to Bajie and Wujing.

> "What can we do?"

> "I have an idea," said Bajie, smiling. "But you won't like it."

> "What is it?" asked Wukong.

> "The two Tang monks should recite the Tight Headband Spell," said the pig. "Only the Tang monk, Guanyin and Buddha know the spell. We'll know right away which Tang monk is fake."

  "You just want to see me in pain," said Wukong. Then he sighed. "But it is a good idea."

## ③

  Bajie and Wujing stood near the monks.

> "Master, recite the Tight Headband Spell," said Wukong.

  Both monks began speaking. And Wukong fell to the floor.

> "It hurts!" he cried.

  Bajie watched the Tang monks carefully. One of them was reciting the spell. But the other one wasn't.

> "This is a fake Tang monk!" shouted the pig, pointing. "He's just mumbling nonsense!"

  The real monk stopped reciting the spell. Bajie and Wujing tried to grab the fake monk, but the monk changed into a green lion and ran out of the throne room.

> "Get him!" shouted Wukong.

## ④

  He and his companions chased the lion.

  Outside, the lion leaped into the air. Wukong and the others shot after him. Clouds swirled and soon they had the lion surrounded.

  Wukong pulled out his iron bar and raised it to strike. Just then a deep voice called out.

> "Do not hit him, Sun Wukong."

  Everyone looked up.

> "It's the Bodhisattva Manjushri," said Wukong.

> "This green lion is my pet," said the Bodhisattva. "The king of the Black Rooster Kingdom was cruel to someone many years ago, so my lion came to earth to punish him. The king's punishment is over now."

  The Bodhisattva and the lion disappeared above the clouds.

## ⑤

  That night the king of the Black Rooster Kingdom hosted a feast. During the feast he turned to the Tang monk.

> "Thank you for your help. I hope you will reach the western paradise very soon."

  Weeks passed and the travelers continued west. One day Wukong saw a mysterious red cloud over the mountains ahead.

> "Something is wrong," he said quietly.

> "What's wrong?" asked the Tang monk.

  The red cloud disappeared. Wukong shook his head.

> "Nothing, Master. I thought I saw a red cloud."

  They walked some more. Wukong saw the red cloud again. He held up his hand for the others to stop.

  The red cloud disappeared once again, and Wukong started to walk again. But then the red cloud reappeared.

> "Saw it again," said the Tang monk.

> "Wukong, should we keep going or not?"

# 🇨🇳 第四十七章：两个唐僧


## ①

  道士消失了。悟空跑到外面，飞入空中，朝四面八方张望。

> "那道士去哪儿了？"悟空问。

  忽然他听到宫里传来一声喊叫。

> "有两个唐僧！"八戒大叫。

  悟空飞回宫内。两个唐僧并排站在了一起。

> "我们分不清他们啊，"悟净说，"他们长得一模一样。"

> "你们两个究竟谁是真正的唐僧？"悟空问。

> "我是，"一个说道。

  另一个瞪了第一个一眼。

> "不，我才是！"

  第一个唐僧看向悟空。

> "别信他，悟空，我才是真的唐僧。"

> "别信他！"另一个唐僧说，"我才是真正的唐僧！"

## ②

  悟空勃然大怒，抽出了铁棒。

> "到底谁是真唐僧！"

  两个唐僧都扑通跪在地上，吓得直抖。

> "别打我！"两人同时喊道。

  悟空叹了口气，转向八戒和悟净。

> "咱们怎么办？"

> "我有个主意，"八戒笑着说，"不过你不一定乐意听。"

> "什么主意？"悟空问。

> "让两个唐僧都念紧箍咒，"八戒说，"只有唐僧、观音菩萨和佛祖才会念紧箍咒。谁不会念，谁就是假的。"

> "你就是想看我被念咒头疼吧，"悟空说，然后叹了口气，"不过还真是个好主意。"

## ③

  八戒和悟净站在两个唐僧旁边。

> "师父，念紧箍咒吧，"悟空说。

  两个唐僧都开始念念有词悟空疼得摔倒在地。

> "好疼啊！"悟空大叫。

  八戒仔细端详两个唐僧。其中一个确实在念咒，可另一个根本就没念。

> "这个唐僧是假的！"八戒指着他大喊，"他只是在胡言乱语！"

  真唐僧停住了口。八戒和悟净上前去抓假唐僧，可那唐僧变成了一头青狮子，跑出了大殿。

> "抓住他！"悟空大喊。

## ④

  悟空和同伴们追了出去。

  到了外面，青狮子一跃而起。悟空等人也腾空追去。白云翻滚，很快他们就把狮子团团围住。

  悟空抽出铁棒，正要打下去，忽然一个低沉的声音传来。

> "不要打他，悟空！"

  众人抬头一看。

> "是文殊菩萨，"悟空说。

> "这头青狮是我的坐骑，"文殊菩萨说，"几年前，乌鸡国国王对一个神仙不敬，所以我的狮子下凡来惩罚他。现在国王的惩罚已经结束了。"

  文殊菩萨和青狮子一起，消失在云层之上。

## ⑤

  当晚，乌鸡国国王设宴款待众人。席间，他对唐僧说。

> "感谢你们的帮助。希望你们早日踏上西天取经的路途。"

  几个星期过去了，一行人继续向西行。一天，悟空看到前方山头飘着一团神秘的红云。

> "情况不妙，"他小声说。

> "怎么了？"唐僧问。

  红云消失了。悟空摇了摇头。

> "没什么，师父。我以为看到了一团红云。"

  他们继续往前走，悟空又看到了那团红云。他举起手，示意大家停下来。

  红云又消失了，悟空继续往前走。可红云再次出现了。

> "悟空，又出现了，"唐僧不安地问道。

> "悟空，我们到底走还是不走？"
