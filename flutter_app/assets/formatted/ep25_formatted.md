# Journey to the West · Chapter Twenty-Five: Powerful Wind Magic

## ①

  The wind stopped. Wukong jumped to his feet and called back the little Wukong.

  "Bajie! I need a nap. Get up!" yelled the monkey.

  "We have to save the Tang monk before the demon king eats him."

  Bajie rubbed his head.

> "How can we save him? That wind was too powerful."

## ②

  Wukong tapped his chin and thought.

> "I'll sneak into the cave to see what I can find out."

  The monkey changed into a mosquito and squeezed through a crack in the cave's door. Deep in the cave, he heard someone crying.

> "That's the Tang monk," thought Wukong.

  The monkey found his master tied up in a dark room.

> "Master!" cried Wukong. He landed on the monk's shoulder.

> "Wukong?" asked the Tang monk, looking around. "Where are you?"

> "I'm on your shoulder," said Wukong. "I turned myself into a mosquito. You must save me."

  The Tang monk started to cry again.

> "Stop crying," said Wukong. "You'll be free soon. First, I must defeat the demon king. I'll be back."

## ③

  Wukong flew down a tunnel and arrived in the throne room. Yellow Wind was talking to some other demons.

> "Your wind magic flew Wukong far away," said one demon.

  Yellow Wind laughed.

> "Only the Bodhisattva Lingji can stop my wind magic. Don't worry. We're having a feast tonight!"

> "Aha!" thought Wukong. "The Bodhisattva Lingji lives on Mount Sumeru. I'll ask him for help."

## ④

  Quickly, the monkey leaped onto a cloud and raced through the sky. When he arrived on Mount Sumeru, Lingji came out of his pagoda.

> "Would you like some tea?" said Lingji.

> "No, thank you," said Wukong. "I have an emergency."

  He quickly told the bodhisattva everything that had happened.

> "I tried to fight the demon," said the monkey. "But then he used powerful wind magic."

> "I know who the Great King Yellow Wind is," said Lingji. "He's a weasel spirit. Buddha asked me to keep an eye on him. I told the spirit he could live on that mountain if he behaved."

  Lingji shook his head.

> "I guess he's not behaving anymore."

> "Can you help me?" asked Wukong. "They're going to eat the Tang monk tonight."

> "Of course," said Lingji.

  He picked up a staff and put a small object into his robe.

> "Let's go."

## ⑤

  Soon they were in the sky, looking down at the cave door.

> "Knock on the door," said Lingji. "Tell the demon to come out. I'll do the rest."

  Wukong went down to the cave door and took out his iron bar. The door turned into a pile of rubble. Yellow Wind rushed outside and yelled.

> "You smashed my door!"

  He took three deep breaths. But nothing happened, said Yellow Wind. He looked up and saw Lingji in the sky. The bodhisattva was holding a pearl in one hand.

> "The wind was stopped in hell!" cried Yellow Wind.

## ⑥

  The demon turned around and ran. Lingji grew his staff, which turned into a golden dragon. The dragon threw Yellow Wind against some rocks. There was a puff of smoke, and the demon turned into a yellow weasel. Lingji picked up the weasel.

> "I'll bring him to Buddha to be punished. Wukong, you may go find your companions. Good luck."

  Wukong ran into the cave and chased away the other demons. Then he untied the Tang monk. The two of them went back out and found Bajie sleeping.

> "Wake up, lazybones!" shouted Wukong.

# 🇨🇳 第二十五章：神风


## ①

  风停了。悟空跳了起来，叫回了小猴子们。

> "八戒！我要打个盹。起来！"猴子喊道。

> "我们得在魔王吃掉唐僧之前把他救出来。"

  八戒揉了揉脑袋。

> "我们怎么救他？那风太厉害了。"

## ②

  悟空托着下巴想了想。

> "我偷偷进洞去打探一下。"

  猴子变成一只蚊子，从门缝里挤进了山洞。在洞的深处，他听到了有人在哭。

> "是唐僧！"悟空心想。

  猴子发现师父被绑在一间黑屋子里。

> "师父！"悟空叫道，落在了和尚的肩膀上。

> "悟空？"唐僧四下张望，"你在哪里？"

> "我在你肩膀上，"悟空说，"我变成了一只蚊子。你得救我啊。"

  唐僧又开始哭了。

> "别哭了，"悟空说，"你很快就能脱身了。不过我先得打败魔王。我很快就回来。"

## ③

  悟空飞到一条隧道里，来到了王座大厅。黄风怪正在和其他妖怪说话。

> "你的风魔法把悟空吹得很远，"一个妖怪说。

  黄风怪哈哈大笑。

> "只有灵吉菩萨能破解我的风魔法。别担心，我们今晚要吃一顿大餐！"

> "哈哈！"悟空心想，"灵吉菩萨住在须弥山。我去找他帮忙。"

## ④

  猴子纵身一跃，驾上云朵飞上了天空。他到了须弥山，灵吉菩萨从宝塔里走了出来。

> "你要喝点茶吗？"灵吉问。

> "不了，谢谢，"悟空说，"我有急事。"

  他迅速把发生的事情都告诉了菩萨。

> "我本想和妖怪斗一斗，"猴子说，"可他使出了厉害的风魔法。"

> "我知道那个黄风大王是谁，"灵吉说，"他是一只黄鼠狼精。佛祖叫我盯着他。我告诉他，只要他守规矩，就让他住在那座山上。"

  灵吉摇了摇头。

> "看来他不再守规矩了。"

> "你能帮帮我吗？"悟空问，"他们今晚要吃掉唐僧。"

> "当然可以，"灵吉说。

  他拿起一根法杖，把一件小东西放进了长袍里。

> "走吧。"

## ⑤

  很快两人便飞在天空，俯视着洞口。

> "去敲门，"灵吉说，"让妖怪出来。剩下的交给我。"

  悟空落到洞门口，掏出铁棒。大门顿时变成了一堆碎石。黄风怪冲了出来，大声吼叫。

> "你砸了我的门！"

  他猛吸了三口气。但什么也没发生，黄风怪惊讶地说。他抬头看到天上的灵吉菩萨，菩萨一只手正托着一颗宝珠。

> "这风怎么失效了！"黄风怪叫道。

## ⑥

  妖怪转身就跑。灵吉催动法杖，法杖化作一条金龙。金龙将黄风怪撞向岩石。只听一声轻响，妖怪变成了一只黄鼠狼。灵吉抓起黄鼠狼。

> "我把他带到佛祖那里去受罚。悟空，你去和你的同伴会合吧，祝你好运。"

  悟空冲进山洞赶走了其他妖怪，然后解开了唐僧身上的绳子。两人一起出了洞，发现八戒正在睡觉。

> "醒醒，懒猪！"悟空大喊道。
