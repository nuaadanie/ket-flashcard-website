# Journey to the West · Chapter Thirty-Nine: The Injured Monk

## ①

  Bajie swung his rake at the demon Golden Horn.

> "You'll never harm the Tang Monk!" shouted the pig. "My rake is very powerful."

  Bajie rushed toward Golden Horn. But the demon was strong. Bajie was knocked out. When he woke up, he was tied up in a dark cave.

## ②

> "Bajie is taking a long time to search the mountain," said the Tang Monk. "I hope he's okay."

> "I'm not sure," said Wukong. "He probably went back to sleep. I should have followed him again."

> "Let's keep walking," said Wu Jing. "We'll find Bajie along the way."

> "We must stay alert though," said Wukong. "We still don't know if there are any demons on this mountain."

  The group headed up the mountain.

## ③

  Soon they heard a voice.

> "Help me!" cried a man.

> "Someone is in trouble," said the Tang Monk. He and his companions hurried ahead and found an old monk lying near the road.

> "Goodness!" cried the old monk. "You must help me!"

  The Tang Monk got down from his horse.

> "Of course we will help you. Please stand up."

## ④

> "I can't," said the old man. "My leg is injured. It hurts terribly." He started to cry. "We'll never get back home."

> "Please don't cry," said the Tang Monk. "We will help you. How did your leg get hurt?"

> "A tiger attacked me," said the old monk.

  Wukong looked suspiciously at the man.

> "He isn't really a monk," he thought. "And I'm sure he wants to harm Master. But I can't tell Master that. He won't believe me."

## ⑤

> "Wukong," said the Tang Monk. "Please carry this poor monk down the mountain."

  Wukong walked over and picked up the old monk.

> "Ouch!" cried the monk.

> "Be careful, Wukong," said the Tang Monk. "His leg is hurt badly."

  The group continued along the road. Wukong carried the injured monk. He walked slowly, letting the Tang Monk and Wu Jing go ahead.

## ⑥

> "I know you're really a demon," the monkey whispered. "And I know you want to eat the Tang Monk."

> "I don't know what you're talking about," said the monk. "I'm just a simple monk who was attacked by a tiger."

  Wukong laughed.

> "Do you know who I am? I am Sun Wukong. Heaven's entire army couldn't defeat me. You're going to wish you'd never tried to harm the Tang Monk."

## ⑦

  As Wukong talked, he felt a heavy weight pressing down on him. He glanced up and realized an entire mountain was balanced on top of his head. The monkey laughed again.

> "So you're going to use magic tricks to trap me under a mountain? Well, let me tell you, Buddha himself once trapped me under a mountain, and I was able to lift it."

  Wukong felt more weight. Now he had two mountains on top of him. He walked more slowly.

> "This still isn't enough weight to stop me," he said. "Now that the Tang Monk isn't around, I should clobber you with my iron bar."

## ⑧

  Suddenly, the old monk vanished. A third mountain appeared on top of the first two. Wukong's knees buckled and all three mountains crashed down on top of him. The monkey was trapped.

  The old monk appeared again. There was a flash of light as he changed back into his demon form. Golden Horn laughed with delight.

> "Wukong was the most powerful of these travelers. It will be easy to defeat Wu Jing. Tonight I will dine on the Tang Monk."

# 🇨🇳 第三十九章：受伤的高僧


## ①

  八戒抡起钉耙朝金角大王砸去。

> "你休想伤害师父！"八戒大喊道，"我的钉耙可不是吃素的。"

  八戒朝金角冲过去。可是妖怪力大无比，八戒被打得昏了过去。等他醒来时，已经被绑在一个黑暗的山洞里。

## ②

> "八戒搜山搜了这么久，"唐僧说，"真希望他没事。"

> "不好说，"悟空道，"他八成又跑去睡觉了。我真该再跟上去盯紧他。"

> "咱们继续赶路吧，"悟净说，"路上也许能找到八戒。"

> "不过我们得提高警惕，"悟空说，"还不确定这座山上有没有妖怪。"

  一行人继续向山上走去。

## ③

  不一会儿，他们听到了一个声音。

> "救命啊！"一个男人在喊叫。

> "有人遇难了，"唐僧说。他和徒弟们赶紧朝前走，发现一个老和尚躺在路边。

> "善人啊！"老和尚叫道，"求求你们救救我！"

  唐僧下了马。

> "我们当然会帮你。请站起来吧。"

## ④

> "我站不起来，"老头说，"我的腿受伤了，疼得要命。"他哭了起来，"我们再也回不了家了。"

> "请别哭了，"唐僧说，"我们会帮你的。你的腿是怎么伤的？"

> "一只老虎攻击了我，"老和尚说。

  悟空狐疑地打量着那个人。

> "他才不是什么真正的和尚，"悟空想，"而且我敢肯定他是要害师父的。可是我不能告诉师父，他不会信的。"

## ⑤

> "悟空，"唐僧说，"请背着这位可怜的师傅下山。"

  悟空走过去，把老和尚背了起来。

> "哎呦！"老和尚叫了一声。

> "轻一点，悟空，"唐僧说，"他的腿伤得很重。"

  一行人继续沿着山路走。悟空背着受伤的和尚，故意走得很慢，让唐僧和悟净走在前面。

## ⑥

> "我知道你其实是个妖怪，"猴子低声说，"我也知道你想吃唐僧。"

> "我不明白你在说什么，"和尚说，"我只是个被老虎袭击的普通和尚罢了。"

  悟空笑了。

> "你知道我是谁吗？我是孙悟空。天兵天将都打不过我。你敢害我师父，就等着后悔吧。"

## ⑦

  悟空正说着，忽然觉得一股沉重的力量压了下来。他抬头一看——发现一整座大山压在了自己头上。猴子又笑了。

> "想用妖术把我压在山下？我告诉你，如来佛祖当年把我压在五行山下，我都能把它扛起来。"

  悟空觉得更重了——现在他头顶上有两座山了。他的脚步慢了下来。

> "这点分量还不足以拦住我，"他说，"等师父走远了，我就用铁棒收拾你。"

## ⑧

  突然，老和尚消失了。第三座山出现在前两座之上。悟空双腿一软，三座山轰然压了下来。猴子被彻底困住了。

  老和尚再次现身。一道闪光之后，他又变回了妖怪的本来面目。金角大王得意地大笑。

> "悟空是这些行者中最厉害的，剩下的悟净就不足为虑了。今晚，我就拿唐僧当晚餐！"
