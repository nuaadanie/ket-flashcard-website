# Journey to the West · Chapter Twenty-Four: The Great King Yellow Wind

## ①

  Wukong and Bajie looked all around, but the Tang monk was gone.

  "Let's forget our master," said Bajie. He shrugged. "I guess we don't have to protect him anymore. Goodbye, Wukong."

  The pig started to walk away, but Wukong grabbed him. "You're not leaving?" said the monkey. "We have to save our master. The tiger said he works for a demon, the Great King Yellow Wind. The King's cave must be nearby."

## ②

  Inside the king's cave, the Tang monk was slumped over the tiger's shoulder. The Great King Yellow Wind was sitting on his throne.

  "Great King!" cried the tiger. "I found the Tang monk."

  The demon king looked surprised and happy. "I have heard that anyone who eats the Tang monk will live forever. Was the monk alone?"

  "He had two companions," said the tiger. "One is a pig. The other is a monkey."

## ③

  Yellow Wind trembled. "A monkey?" he said. "He must be Sun Wukong — the monkey who caused all that trouble in heaven."

  "Maybe," said the tiger. "But don't worry. I distracted them with a fake tiger skin. They'll never find us."

  The tiger padded his stomach and looked at the Tang monk. "I'll tell the other demons to start a fire and prepare some spices."

## ④

  Yellow Wind held up his hand. "Sun Wukong is powerful. He and that pig might come looking for the monk. They'll be angry if they find out. Let's wait until they leave the area. Then we'll be able to enjoy our meal."

  A small demon ran into the throne room.

  "My King!" cried the demon. "There are two strangers outside, a monkey and a pig. They said to hand over the Tang monk. If we don't, they'll destroy our entire mountain!"

## ⑤

  "Oh no!" Yellow Wind said, nervous.

  "Don't worry, my King," said the tiger. "I will get rid of them."

  The tiger walked out of the throne room. The small demon followed him.

  Outside the cave, there was some yelling and then a crashing sound. A moment later, the small demon ran back in.

## ⑥

  "My King! Disaster!" he cried. "The tiger is dead. The pig killed him with his rake!"

  "What?" cried Yellow Wind. He stood up and grabbed a trident. He puffed out his chest and stomped out of the cave.

  He saw Wukong. "So you're the monkey who caused all the trouble in heaven," said Yellow Wind.

## ⑦

  "Hand over the Tang monk," demanded Wukong.

  "I didn't know you were so small," said Yellow Wind.

  "Hit me on the head," said the monkey. "I'll grow bigger."

  The demon king hesitated. "Go ahead," said Wukong.

  Yellow Wind knocked the monkey on the head. Instantly Wukong grew tall.

## ⑧

  "Now I'll hit you!" cried Wukong. He swung his iron rod.

  The demon king blocked the rod with his trident. Clang! The two fought up and down the side of the mountain.

  "I'll use some magic," thought the monkey. He grabbed a handful of hair and blew on it. Dozens of little Wukongs ran toward the demon king.

## ⑨

  Yellow Wind laughed. "Ha ha ha! I don't know what magic could do."

  He looked down and blew three times. The sky grew dark. A powerful yellow wind whipped across the mountain. Trees cracked and rocks tumbled. Wukong, Bajie, and all the little Wukongs tumbled down the mountain. They landed at the bottom in a cloud of dust.

## ⑩

  The demon king went back into his cave and slammed the door shut.

# 🇨🇳 第二十四章：黄风大王


## ①

  悟空和八戒四处寻找，唐僧却不见了踪影。

  "算了，师父不要了，"八戒说。他耸了耸肩，"不用保护他了。再见了，悟空。"

  猪八戒转身要走，悟空一把抓住他，"你这就走了？"悟空说，"我们得救师父。那只老虎说他给一个妖魔做事，叫黄风大王。那大王的洞一定就在附近。"

## ②

  大王的洞里，唐僧软绵绵地趴在老虎的肩膀上。黄风大王正坐在宝座上。

  "大王！"老虎大喊，"我抓到唐僧了。"

  妖魔国王又惊又喜，"我听说吃了唐僧肉就能长生不老。那和尚是一个人吗？"

  "他有两个徒弟，"老虎说，"一个是猪，另一个是猴子。"

## ③

  黄风大王浑身一颤，"猴子？"他说，"莫非是孙悟空 —— 那个大闹天宫的猴子。"

  "也许吧，"老虎说，"不过别担心。我用一张假虎皮把他们骗了。他们永远找不到我们。"

  老虎拍了拍肚子，看着唐僧说，"我去叫其他小妖生火准备调料。"

## ④

  黄风大王举起手拦住他，"孙悟空法力高强。他和那头猪可能会来找和尚。要是被他们发现了就不好办了。我们先等他们离开这一带，然后再慢慢享用。"

  一个小妖跑进了大殿。

  "大王！"小妖喊道，"外面来了两个陌生人，一个是猴，一个是猪。他们说要交出唐僧，不然就踏平我们整座山！"

## ⑤

  "糟糕！"黄风大王紧张地说。

  "别怕，大王，"老虎说，"我去收拾他们。"

  老虎走出大殿，小妖跟在后面。洞外传来一阵叫喊声，接着是一声巨响。过了一会儿，小妖跑了回来。

## ⑥

  "大王！大事不妙！"小妖哭喊道，"老虎死了，被那猪用钉耙打死了！"

  "什么？"黄风大王惊叫。他站起来抓起一把三叉戟，挺起胸膛大步走出山洞。

  他看见了悟空，"原来你就是大闹天宫的那只猴子，"黄风大王说。

## ⑦

  "把唐僧交出来！"悟空喝道。

  "没想到你这么矮小，"黄风大王说。

  "打我头一下，"悟空说，"我会长得更高。"

  妖魔国王犹豫了一下。"来吧，"悟空说。

  黄风大王在猴子头上敲了一下。霎时间，悟空变得高大无比。

## ⑧

  "现在轮到我了！"悟空大喊，挥起铁棒打去。

  妖魔国王用三叉戟挡住铁棒。铛！两人在山上山下大战起来。

  "我得用点法术，"悟空心想。他拔下一把毫毛，吹了一口气。几十个小小的悟空朝妖魔国王奔去。

## ⑨

  黄风大王哈哈大笑，"哈哈哈！我不知道这法术有什么用。"

  他低下头吹了三口气。天空顿时暗了下来。一阵强劲的黄色狂风吹过整座山。树木折断，岩石滚落。悟空、八戒和所有小悟空都被吹得滚下了山，摔在底下的一团尘土中。

## ⑩

  妖魔国王回到洞里，把门重重关上。
