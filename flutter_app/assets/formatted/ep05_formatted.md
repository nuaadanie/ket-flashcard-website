# Journey to the West · Chapter Five: The Demon of Chaos

## ①

  > "Your Majesty!" the king reported. "Something terrible has happened."
  > "What happened here?" asked Wukong.
  Several more monkeys and apes gathered around, looking frightened.
  > "A demon attacked us," said the king. "We tried to fight him, but he was too powerful. He stole most of our things and kidnapped many of our people."
  Wukong was furious.
  > "Who is he? Where does he live?" he demanded.
  > "He calls himself the Demon of Chaos," replied the king. "He came from the north."

## ②

  > "Wait here," ordered Wukong. "I will deal with the demon."
  > "The demon is very powerful," the king warned.
  > "I learned many things from a sage," Wukong replied. "I am now as powerful as a god. I am not afraid of any demon. I'll be back soon."
  He leaped into the air and flew north. His subjects stared in amazement.
  > "Our king can fly!" exclaimed the king.

## ③

  Wukong soon spotted a gloomy mountain with dark clouds hanging over it.
  > "That looks like a demon's lair," he thought.
  He landed on the mountain and found a cave. Two guards stood at the entrance, practicing sword fighting.
  > "I am the king of the Fruit and Flower Mountain!" Wukong yelled. "The Demon of Chaos attacked my subjects. I seek revenge!"
  The guards shrieked and ran into the cave.

## ④

  The Demon of Chaos was sitting at a table, eating.
  > "Disaster!" one guard cried, running in.
  > "What sort of disaster?" asked the demon.
  > "There's a monkey outside," said the other guard. "He says he's the king of the Fruit and Flower Mountain. You attacked his subjects, and now he wants revenge."
  > "The monkeys on the Fruit and Flower Mountain spoke of a king," said the demon. "He left to find a sage. So he's back."
  The demon stood up.
  > "Does the monkey have any weapons?" he asked.
  > "No," replied the guard.
  > "Then bring me my armor and sword," ordered the demon.
  A moment later, the Demon of Chaos walked outside. He looked at Wukong and laughed.
  > "You're just a little monkey," he said.
  Wukong looked up at the huge demon.
  > "I am small," he said, "but I am more powerful than you can imagine. I am not afraid of you."
  The demon swung his sword. Wukong jumped out of the way and then pounced on the demon.
  Bam! Wukong hit the demon on the nose.

## ⑤

  > "Ah!" cried the demon.
  Wukong pulled out a handful of his own hair and blew on it. Each strand turned into a duplicate of Wukong, exactly like him. All at once, the duplicates attacked the demon, punching and kicking.
  > "What's going on?" the demon cried, trying to fight off the clones.
  > "It's a magic spell," said Wukong, smiling. "It's called 'Body Beyond the Body.'"
  The Demon of Chaos was soon destroyed.

## ⑥

  Wukong chased away the guards and then entered the cave. He found all the monkeys and apes that the demon had kidnapped. When they saw him, they cheered.
  > "Your Majesty!" they cried. "You came to rescue us!"
  Wukong smiled.
  > "I will take you home," he said.

## ⑦

  The next morning, Wukong woke early.
  > "Other demons might attack us," he thought. "We must be ready to defend ourselves."
  He spent the day teaching his subjects how to fight. They practiced with swords and spears. Before long, the monkeys and apes had become a fierce army.
  > "No demon will dare bother us now," said the king.
  > "That's true," said Wukong, "but there's still one problem."
  > "What's that?" asked the king.
  > "I can't use any of these weapons," said Wukong. "They're too light for me."

# 🇨🇳 第五章：混沌魔王

## ①

  > "陛下！"猴王跑来报告，"出大事了。"
  > "这里发生了什么事？"悟空问猴王。
  又有几只猴子、猿猴围拢过来，他们一脸惊恐。
  > "有个妖怪袭击我们，"猴王说，"我们试图抵抗，但他太厉害了。他抢走了大部分财物，还绑架了许多同伴。"
  悟空勃然大大怒。
  > "他是谁？住在哪里？"
  > "他自称混沌魔王，"猴王道，"从北方来的。"

## ②

  > "你们在这儿等着，"悟空下令，"我来对付那妖怪。"
  > "那妖怪非常厉害，"猴王警告道。
  > "我从仙人那里学了好多本事，"悟空回答，"我现在法力如神，什么妖怪都不怕。我很快就回来。"
  他腾空而起，向北飞去。臣民们惊讶得目瞪口呆。
  > "我们的国王会飞啊！"猴王惊叹道。

## ③

  悟空很快发现一座阴暗的山峰，乌云笼罩。
  > "这定是妖魔的巢穴，"他想。
  悟空降落到山上，找到一个洞穴。洞口站着两个守卫，正在练习剑术。
  > "我是花果山之王！"悟空大喝道，"混沌魔王袭击我的子民，我要报仇！"
  守卫们尖叫着跑进洞中。

## ④

  混沌魔王正坐在桌旁用餐。
  > "大事不好！"一名守卫慌张跑入。
  > "什么灾难？"魔王问。
  > "洞外有只猴子，"另一名守卫说，"他说他是花果山之王。您袭击了他的子民，他要来复仇。"
  > "花果山的猴子们曾说起有位国王，"魔王道，"他离开去寻访仙人了。原来他回来了。"
  魔王站起身。
  > "那猴子有武器吗？"
  > "没有，"守卫答。
  > "那就给我拿盔甲和剑来，"魔王吩咐。
  片刻后，混沌魔王走出洞外。他看着悟空，冷笑起来。
  > "你不过是一只小猴子，"他说。
  悟空仰视着庞大的魔王。
  > "我虽小，"悟空道，"但我的力量超乎你想象。我不怕你。"
  魔王挥剑劈来。悟空灵巧躲开，随即扑向魔王。
  砰！悟空一拳击中魔王的鼻子。

## ⑤

  > "啊！"魔王惨叫一声。
  悟空拔下一把毛发，吹了一口气。每一根毛发立刻变成一只假悟空，一模一样。霎时间，假悟空们群起进攻，拳打脚踢。
  > "怎么回事？"魔王惊慌失措，试图抵挡众多假悟空。
  > "这是我的法术，"悟空笑道，"名叫'身外化身'。"
  混沌魔王很快就被消灭了。

## ⑥

  悟空赶走守卫，进入洞穴。他发现所有被绑架的猴子、猿猴。他们见到悟空，纷纷欢呼。
  > "大王！"猴子、猿猴们齐声喊道，"您回来救我们了！"
  悟空微笑。
  > "我送你们回家。"

## ⑦

  第二天一早，悟空早早醒来。
  > "其他妖魔可能还会来犯，"他想，"我们必须做好防御准备。"
  悟空花了一整天教导子民们战斗技巧。他们练习刀剑和长矛。不久，猴子、猿猴们成了一支勇猛之师。
  > "现在没有妖魔敢来骚扰我们了，"猴王说。
  > "是的，"悟空道，"但还是有个问题。"
  > "什么问题？"猴王问。
  > "我们现有的兵器我都没法用，"悟空说。
