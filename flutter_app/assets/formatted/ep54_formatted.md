# Journey to the West · Chapter Fifty-Four: The River God

## ①

> "I should have stopped Master from getting in that canoe," said Wukong. "But whenever I warn him about someone, he doesn't believe me."

  Wujing nodded and the water spirit dived into the river. He soon came to a palace. A sign on the gate read, "Palace of the Black River God." Wujing tapped on the door with his spear.

> "Open up!"

  The door opened and a lizard demon peeked out.

## ②

> "What do you want?" asked the lizard.

> "I'm looking for my companions," said Wujing. "One is a monk and the other is a pig. They were kidnapped by a man pretending to be the river god."

> "They were kidnapped by me," said the lizard.

> "What?" said Wujing. "Yes."

  The lizard smiled. "I planned to eat the Tang Monk and he'll live forever."

  Wujing struck the lizard with his spear. The lizard spun around, blocking his spear with his tail. The blow nearly knocked Wujing over. Right away the lizard was on top of him.

## ③

> "Got me!" shouted Wujing.

  He shoved the lizard. The lizard attacked again. Wujing swung his spear and then swam upward. He splashed out of the river and landed next to Wukong.

> "There's a lizard demon in the river," said Wujing, breathing heavily. "He's very powerful."

  Wukong was about to respond when he noticed someone nearby. The man from the canoe was walking toward them. The monkey pulled out his iron bar.

> "Out there! You kidnapped my friends!"

## ④

  The man fell to his knees, shaking.

> "It wasn't me!" he cried. "It was a lizard demon! I am the Black River God, and this is my river. But that lizard demon pretended he was me."

  Wukong put the iron bar back in his ear.

> "I believe you," he said. "Do you know who the lizard is?"

> "I don't," said the river god. He looked even more frightened now.

> "What? His uncle is the mighty Dragon King of the Eastern Sea?"

  Wukong laughed.

> "Ha ha! That dragon is a friend of mine. He's also a bit scared of me. I'll go have a talk with him."

## ⑤

  A moment later, the monkey was at the Dragon King's palace.

> "Hello again, Wukong," said the Dragon, smiling. "What can I do for you?"

> "Why did you let your nephew take over the Black River?" demanded the monkey.

  The Dragon's smile faded.

> "What are you talking about?"

> "Your nephew, a lizard demon, took over the Black River," said Wukong. "And now he's planning to eat the Tang Monk."

> "Oh dear," said the Dragon. "I had no idea. I'll take care of it."

## ⑥

  Wukong and the Dragon flew back to the Black River. The Dragon came shouting.

> "Nephew, come out of that river at once!"

  The lizard crept from the river.

> "Uncle! It's nice of you to visit."

> "I'm not here to visit," yelped the Dragon. "I'm here to take you to the Jade Emperor. You will be punished."

## ⑦

  The lizard gulped, his head hung low. He followed his uncle up into the sky. With a splash, the river god appeared from the water. The Tang Monk and Bajie were with him.

> "Thank you, Wukong," said the river god.

> "You're welcome," said the monkey. "But we still need to cross your river."

## ⑧

> "That's easy," said the river god.

  He held up his hand. Instantly the river stopped flowing. The water formed walls. The travelers walked across the muddy riverbed and continued west.

# 🇨🇳 第五十四章：河神


## ①

> "我本不该让师父上那条船的，"悟空说，"可每次我提醒他当心别人，他就是不信。"

  悟净点了点头，水神纵身跃入河中。他很快就来到了一座宫殿前，门上写着"黑水河河神之府"。悟净用长枪敲了敲门。

> "快开门！"

  门开了，一个鼍龙妖怪探出头来。

## ②

> "你要干什么？"妖怪问。

> "我在找我的同伴，"悟净说，"一个是和尚，一个是猪。他们被一个假扮河神的人抓走了。"

> "是我抓的，"妖怪说。

> "什么？"悟净说。

  妖怪笑了起来："我计划吃掉唐僧，就能长生不老。"

  悟净一枪刺向妖怪。妖怪转了个身，用尾巴挡住了长枪。这一击差点把悟净打翻，转眼间妖怪就骑在了他身上。

## ③

> "放开我！"悟净大喊道。

  他用力推开妖怪。妖怪再次进攻。悟净挥枪挡了一下，然后向上游去。他冲出水面，落在了悟空身旁。

> "河里有个妖怪，"悟净喘着粗气说，"他法力很强。"

  悟空正要说话，发现附近有个人在朝他们走来，正是那个船夫。猴子掏出了金箍棒。

> "原来是你！你抓了我的朋友！"

## ④

  那人扑通跪在地上，浑身发抖。

> "不是我！"他哭喊道，"是河里有个妖怪！我才是黑水河河神，这是我的河。那妖怪假扮成了我。"

  悟空把金箍棒放回耳朵里。

> "我相信你，"他说，"你知道那妖怪是什么来头吗？"

> "我不知道，"河神说。他看上去更害怕了，"不过他的舅舅好像是东海龙王。"

  悟空大笑起来。

> "哈哈！那龙王可是我的朋友，他也有点怕我。我去跟他理论。"

## ⑤

  不一会儿，猴子就到了龙王的宫殿。

> "又见面了，悟空，"龙王笑着说，"有什么事吗？"

> "你为什么让你外甥霸占黑水河？"猴子厉声问道。

  龙王的笑容消失了。

> "你在说什么？"

> "你外甥，一个鼍龙妖怪，霸占了黑水河，"悟空说，"他现在还要吃唐僧呢。"

> "哎呀，"龙王说，"我完全不知道。我来处理这件事。"

## ⑥

  悟空和龙王飞回黑水河，龙王大声喊道：

> "外甥，马上从河里给我出来！"

  鼍龙从河里爬了出来。

> "舅舅！您能来看我真好。"

> "我不是来看你的，"龙王喝道，"我是来抓你去见玉皇大帝的，你要受罚！"

## ⑦

  鼍龙咽了口唾沫，低下了头，跟着舅舅飞上了天空。哗啦一声，河神从水里出来了，唐僧和八戒跟他在一起。

> "谢谢你啊，悟空，"河神说。

> "不客气，"猴子说，"不过我们还是要过你的河。"

## ⑧

> "这好办，"河神说。

  他举起手来，河水立刻停止了流动，水流变成了两堵水墙。行者们从河床走了过去，继续向西前行。
