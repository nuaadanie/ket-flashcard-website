# Journey to the West · Chapter One: The Monkey

## ①

  From the beginning of time, a rock stood high on top of the mountain. The sun, the moon, heaven and earth, all worked their magic on the rock. One day the rock split open. Inside was a monkey. The monkey stood up and stretched. Suddenly powerful light shot from its eyes. For a moment the light reached the Cloud Palace in heaven.

## ②

  The Jade Emperor, ruler of heaven, sat up. He turned to one of his captains who was standing nearby.
  > "Find out what caused that light," said the Emperor.
  > "Yes, Your Majesty," said the captain.
  The captain rushed out of the hall. He looked out the southern gate of heaven and saw the monkey on earth below.

## ③

  The light was gone. But the captain had magical sight. He could see everything that had just happened.
  > "Interesting," said the captain.
  When the captain returned, the Jade Emperor asked, "What caused the light?"
  > "It was a monkey," said the captain.
  > "A monkey?" asked the Jade Emperor.
  > "Yes," said the captain. "He's on the Fruit and Flower Mountain down on earth. There had been a rock on the mountain. The monkey came from the rock." The light shone from the monkey's eyes.

## ④

  The Emperor thought for a moment and then shrugged.
  > "I'm not surprised. Amazing things happen when heaven and earth work together," he said.
  As time passed, the monkey learned to walk, run, and climb. He drank from streams and ate grasses and leaves. He gathered fruit and explored caves and mountain tops. He became friends with all the other monkeys and apes. At night, he slept under a cliff.

## ⑤

  One very hot morning, all the monkeys and apes were playing in the shade.
  > "It is so hot today!" cried one monkey. "Let's play in the stream."
  > "Good idea!" said another monkey.
  The monkeys and apes ran to the stream.

## ⑥

  The water rolled and bounced over the rocks. The monkeys jumped into the cool water. Everyone splashed and played. After a few minutes, one monkey scratched her head.
  > "We all love this stream," she said. "We drink from it, we swim in it, we know that it flows all the way to the ocean. But does anyone know where the stream begins?"
  They all looked at one another and shook their heads.

## ⑦

  > "We have nothing to do today," the monkey continued. "Why don't we follow the stream to find its source?"
  > "That sounds like fun!" said another monkey.
  > "Let's go!" shouted a third monkey.
  Everyone jumped out of the water. They ran alongside the stream and swung from tree to tree.

## ⑧

  They jumped over rocks and climbed up steep cliffs. Higher and higher they went up the mountain until they reached an enormous waterfall. Mist filled the air. The water crashed down, roaring like thunder.
  > "I wonder what's behind that waterfall," said one monkey.
  > "Me too!" said a second monkey.
  But nobody could jump through it.
  > "Whoever can jump through and come back alive will be king," said one of the monkeys.
  > "Is anyone brave enough to try?" All the monkeys looked at one another. They shook their heads.
  > "I'm not going to try," said a tall monkey.
  > "Me neither," said a short monkey.

## ⑨

  A voice called out.
  > "I'll do it!"
  Everyone moved out of the way as the speaker stepped forward. It was the monkey.
  > "Are you sure?" they asked.
  > "It looks dangerous," someone said.
  The monkey laughed.
  > "I'm not afraid of anything. And when I come back, I will be king."

## ⑩

  Monkey took a deep breath and leaped into the waterfall. He splashed through the waterfall and landed in a large cave. His eyes opened wide. The cave was filled with bamboo, trees and beautiful flowers.

# 🇨🇳 第一章：石猴出世

## ①

  从远古开始，一块石头高耸于山顶。太阳、月亮、天地都对这块石头施展魔力。一天，石头裂开，里面是一只猴子。猴子站起来伸展身体，突然，强大的光芒从双眼射出。片刻间，光芒直达天宫的云殿。

## ②

  玉皇大帝端坐在天宫宝座上。他转向旁边站着的一位天将："查清楚那光芒是什么原因。"
  "是，陛下。"天将回答。
  天将冲出大殿，望向天宫南天门，看到了地上的猴子。

## ③

  光芒消失了。但那位天将拥有神通，能看清刚才发生的一切。
  "有趣。"天将说。
  玉皇大帝等天将回来时问道："是什么原因引起的？"
  "是一只猴子。"天将答道。
  "是一只猴子？"玉皇大帝问。
  "是的。"天将答，"它在人间的花果山上。山上原来有块石头，猴子就是从那块石头里出来的。"光芒从猴子的眼睛发出。

## ④

  皇帝想了一会儿，然后耸耸肩："我并不惊讶。当天地协同作用时，总会发生神奇之事。" 随着时间的流逝，猴子学会了走路、奔跑和攀爬。他喝溪水，吃草和树叶，采集水果，探索洞穴和山顶。他和其他所有的猴子、猿猴成为朋友。夜晚，他在悬崖下睡觉。

## ⑤

  一个炎热的早晨，所有的猴子、猿猴都在树荫下玩耍。
  "今天太热了，"一只猴子叫道，"我们去溪水里玩吧。"
  "好主意。"另一只猴子说。
  猴子们和猿猴们跑向溪边。

## ⑥

  溪水在岩石上翻滚跳跃。猴子们跳进清凉的水中，大家嬉戏打闹，溅起水花。几分钟后，一只猴子挠了挠头："我们都喜欢这条溪流，"她说，"我们喝它的水，在里面游泳，知道它一直流向大海。可是谁知道溪流的源头在哪里？" 大家互相看了看，摇了摇头。

## ⑦

  "我们今天没事做，"那只猴子接着说，"为什么不沿溪流找到它的源头呢？"
  "听起来很有趣。"另一只猴子说。
  "我们走吧！"第三只猴子叫道。 大家跳出水面，沿着溪流奔跑，在树间荡秋千。

## ⑧

  他们跳过岩石，爬上陡峭的悬崖。越爬越高，直到一个巨大的瀑布挡在前面。空中雾气弥漫。水流轰鸣着冲下，如同雷鸣。
  "我想知道瀑布后面是什么。"一只猴子说。
  "我也是！"第二只猴子说。
  但谁也跳不进去。
  "能跳过去并活着回来的，将成为大王。"一只猴子提议。
  "有谁敢试试吗？" 所有的猴子面面相觑，摇了摇头。
  "我不试。"一只高个子猴子说。
  "我也不。"一只矮猴子说。

## ⑨

  一个声音叫道："我来！"
  大家纷纷让开，说话者走上前，正是那只猴子。
  "你确定吗？"有人问。
  "看起来很危险。"
  猴子笑道："我什么都不怕。等我回来，我就要做大王。"

## ⑩

  猴子深吸一口气，跳进了瀑布。猴子穿过瀑布，落在一个大洞穴里。他的眼睛睁得大大的。洞穴里长满了竹子、树木和美丽的花朵。
