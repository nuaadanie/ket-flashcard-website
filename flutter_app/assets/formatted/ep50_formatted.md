# Journey to the West · Chapter Fifty: Magic Fire

## ①

  Wukong and his companions ran back down the mountain, escaping the fire.

> "Red Boy's fire is powerful," said the monkey. "We were nearly roasted."

> "Yes," said Bajie.

  Wujing looked up at the mountain peak.

> "The fire is dying down now."

> "Yes," said Wukong, looking at the last few wisps of smoke. "But he'll use it again if we go back up there."

> "We'd need water," said Wujing. "Then if he makes fire again, we can just put it out."

## ②

  Wukong nodded.

  "You two wait here. I know who can help us."

  The monkey shot into the air and flew away. When he returned, the Dragon King of the Eastern Sea was with him. Wukong introduced the Dragon King to Bajie and Wujing.

> "I can summon rain clouds," said the Dragon King. "Go back up the mountain and get Red Boy to come out. As soon as he makes his fire, I'll create a huge rainstorm."

  The Dragon King flew up into the sky. Wukong and the others returned to the cave.

## ③

  "Open up, Red Boy!" The monkey pounded on the cave door.

  The door flew open and Red Boy ran outside.

> "Release the Tang Monk right now!" shouted Wukong.

> "Never!" shouted the demon.

  He made a fist and punched himself in the nose. Smoking fire poured from his face again.

> "Dragon King!" called the monkey.

  Thunder rumbled as dark clouds filled the sky. Rain poured down. The fire however didn't go out. Instead of shrinking, the fire grew bigger. The smoke became thick, choking and unable to breathe.

## ④

  Wukong covered his face. He saw Bajie and Wujing running away. The monkey tried to get to the cave door, but the smoke was too thick. He tripped on a rock and tumbled down the side of the mountain.

  *Splash!* Wukong landed in a river and floated downstream. Finally, he washed up on the riverbank, coughing and wheezing. Bajie and Wujing rushed over to the monkey.

> "Wukong, are you alright?" asked Wujing.

> "I think so," said the monkey. He coughed again. "That fire is no ordinary fire. The water made it worse."

> "You're right," said the pig. "It must be magic fire."

## ⑤

> "What will we do now? Red Boy still has the Tang Monk in his cave."

> "I'll go to Guanyin for help," said Wukong, standing up.

> "You rest," said Bajie. "You breathed in a lot of smoke."

  "Bajie, go straight to Guanyin," said the monkey, coughing again. "No naps."

## ⑥

  Meanwhile, Red Boy was sitting on his mountain peak.

  "My fire was too powerful for Sun Wukong and his friends," he said to himself. "They must be dead now." The demon patted his belly. "And that is good because I am hungry. I'll go inside now and start cooking the Tang Monk."

  The demon stood up to leave when he spotted something in the sky.

  "It's the pig," he said. "I wonder what."

  Red Boy thought for a minute. "The pig must be going to Guanyin. Putuo Luojia is in the South Sea. He must be going to ask her for help. I'll stop him."

## ⑦

  The demon recited a quick spell and light sparkled around him. Now Red Boy looked exactly like Guanyin.

  Bajie soared through the sky on his cloud. He saw Guanyin coming toward him.

> "Bajie!" called the fake Guanyin with a smile. "This is a surprise. I was just about to go see you."

# 🇨🇳 第五十章：三昧真火


## ①

  悟空和同伴们逃下了山，躲避火焰。

> "红孩儿的火真厉害，"猴子说，"我们差点被烤熟了。"

> "是啊，"八戒说。

  悟净抬头望向山顶。

> "火现在已经小了。"

> "不错，"悟空望着最后几缕烟雾说，"可是如果我们再上去，他还会放火的。"

> "我们需要水，"悟净说，"这样他再放火的时候，我们就可以把火扑灭。"

## ②

  悟空点了点头。"你们两个在这里等着，我知道谁能帮忙。"

  猴子腾空而起，飞走了。等他回来时，东海龙王跟他在一起。悟空把龙王介绍给了八戒和悟净。

> "我能召唤雨云，"龙王说，"你们回到山上去，把红孩儿引出来。他一放火，我就下大雨。"

  龙王飞上了天空。悟空和其他人回到了洞口。

## ③

  "快开门，红孩儿！"猴子用力敲打着洞门。

  洞门猛然打开，红孩儿冲了出来。

> "立刻把唐僧放出来！"悟空大喊道。

> "休想！"妖怪大叫。

  他攥紧拳头朝自己鼻子捶了一拳，浓烟烈火再次从脸上冒出来。

> "龙王！"悟空喊道。

  雷声隆隆，乌云密布，大雨倾盆而下。然而火焰并没有熄灭，反而越烧越旺。浓烟滚滚，呛得人喘不过气来。

## ④

  悟空捂住脸，他看见八戒和悟净正在逃跑。猴子想跑到洞口，但烟太浓了。他被一块石头绊倒，顺着山坡滚了下去。

  *哗啦！*悟空掉进了一条河里，顺流漂下。最后他冲上了河岸，不停地咳嗽喘息。八戒和悟净冲到猴子身边。

> "悟空，你没事吧？"悟净问。

> "应该没事，"猴子说，他又咳嗽了一声，"那火不是普通的火，水反而让它更厉害了。"

> "你说得对，"八戒说，"那一定是三昧真火。"

## ⑤

> "现在怎么办？红孩儿还把师父关在洞里呢。"

> "我去找观音菩萨帮忙，"悟空站起身来说。

> "你先歇着，"八戒说，"你吸了那么多烟。"

  "八戒，你直接去找观音菩萨，"猴子又咳了一声，"别半路偷懒。"

## ⑥

  与此同时，红孩儿正坐在山顶上。

  "我的火把孙悟空和他的同伴们打得落花流水，"他自言自语道，"他们肯定已经死了。"妖怪拍了拍肚子，"那正好我肚子饿了，我现在就进去开始烹唐僧。"

  妖怪起身要走，忽然看到天上有动静。

  "是那头猪，"他说，"不知道他去干什么。"

  红孩儿想了一会儿："那猪一定是去观音那儿。普陀洛迦山在南面，他肯定是去请观音帮忙。我得拦住他。"

## ⑦

  妖怪念了一段咒语，周身发出光亮。此刻红孩儿变得跟观音菩萨一模一样。

  八戒驾着云在空中飞行，看见观音迎面走来。

> "八戒！"假观音笑着喊道，"真是巧了，我正想去找你呢。"
