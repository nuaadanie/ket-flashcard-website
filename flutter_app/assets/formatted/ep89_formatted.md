# Journey to the West · Chapter Eighty-Nine: The Demon's Plan

## ①

  The man on the road continued to wave a warning. Travelling with fear, the Tang monk fell from his horse. Wujing rushed over to help him stand up.

> "I'll be right back," said Wukong. "I'll ask that man what sort of danger is up ahead."

  The monkey walked toward the man.

## ②

  Looking closely, Wukong could tell that he was not an ordinary man.

> "Hello, Venus," said Wukong with a smile.

  The man smiled back.

> "Yes, it's me," said the spirit of the planet Venus. "I came and disguised myself to warn you, Wukong. There are three powerful demons on the mountain up ahead. The first is a lion demon. The second is an elephant demon. And the third is a raptor demon."

## ③

  The monkey shrugged.

> "Thanks for the warning, Venus. But I'm not worried about three silly demons."

  Venus shook his head.

> "You should be worried. The three demons command a demon army."

## ④

  Wukong rubbed his chin.

> "Once again, thank you for the warning. But even an entire army of demons doesn't worry me."

  Venus nodded.

> "Very well, Wukong. Good luck."

## ⑤

  The spirit vanished. The monkey walked back to his companions.

> "Wow," said Bajie. "What did the man say? Is there danger up ahead? Are there demons?"

  asked the Tang monk, still scared.

## ⑥

> "Actually, yes," said Wukong. "There's an army of demons. But I'm not worried."

  The Tang monk's jaw dropped.

> "How can you not be worried?"

  Wukong laughed.

> "Master, have you forgotten? I once defeated Heaven's entire army."

  Wujing frowned.

> "Yes, we all know about that, Wukong. But this is different. The Tang monk is with us. We must keep him safe."

## ⑦

  Bajie nodded.

> "Yes. And I'd like to be safe too."

  Wukong sighed.

> "You're right. We can't let the demons harm Master."

  He gave a sideways glance at Bajie and smirked.

> "Or the pig."

  Bajie snorted.

## ⑧

> "You all wait here," said Wukong. "I'll check out the mountains to see how big the demon army is."

  The monkey flew to the mountain. Several demons were talking in front of a cave. They were dressed like military officers. Wukong spied on them from behind a rock.

## ⑨

> "The Tang monk will be coming over our mountain soon," said a demon general. "Our leaders want all the troops to watch for him."

> "I hope we catch the Tang monk," said a captain. "I've heard that anyone who eats him will live forever."

  Wukong was furious.

## ⑩

> "But who will get to eat the monk?" asked another captain.

> "There are thousands of troops in our army. We'll all get to eat him," said the general. "Our leaders promised to share him with the entire army."

  The other officers nodded and smiled.

## ⑪

> "That was very kind of our leaders," said a captain.

> "We must be careful though," warned the general.

> "Why?" asked a colonel. "The monk is just a man. He can't possibly stop us."

## ⑫

> "I've heard that the Tang monk isn't alone," said the general. "Sun Wukong is with him."

  Everyone grew quiet.

> "He's the spirit who defeated Heaven's entire army," spat a captain.

  Another captain nodded.

> "He is quite powerful."

  Wukong beamed proudly when he heard that.

## ⑬

> "Don't worry about Sun Wukong," said the general. "Just tell the troops to watch for the Tang monk."

> "Yes, General," said the colonel.

## ⑭

  I have an idea, thought Wukong.

  The monkey made himself look like a demon general. Then he stepped out from behind the rock and hurried over to the officers.

> "I have good news!" shouted Wukong. "The Tang monk is heading toward our mountain."

# 🇨🇳 第八十九章：妖怪的计谋

## ①

  路上的男子不停地挥手发出警告。唐僧害怕地从马背上摔了下来。悟净赶紧上前扶他站起来。

> "我马上回来，"悟空说，"我去问问那个人前面有什么危险。"

  猴子朝那人走去。

## ②

  仔细一看，悟空发现这个人不是凡人。

> "你好，太白金星，"悟空笑着说。

  那人微微一笑。

> "没错，是我，星球金星之灵。我来是为了警告你，悟空。前面的山上有三个厉害的妖怪。第一个是狮子精，第二个是大象精，第三个是猛禽精。"

## ③

  猴子耸了耸肩。

> "谢谢你的警告，金星。但我不怕三个蠢妖怪。"

  金星摇了摇头。

> "你应该担心。这三个妖怪率领着一支妖怪大军。"

## ④

  悟空摸着下巴。

> "再次感谢你的警告。但就算是一整支妖怪大军，我也不怕。"

  金星点了点头。

> "好吧，悟空。祝你好运。"

## ⑤

  仙人消失了。猴子走回到同伴身边。

> "哇，"八戒说，"那个人说了什么？前面危险吗？有妖怪吗？"

  唐僧仍然害怕地问。

## ⑥

> "确实有妖怪，"悟空说，"有一支妖怪大军。不过我不担心。"

  唐僧惊得下巴都掉了。

> "你怎么能不担心呢？"

  悟空大笑起来。

> "师父，您忘了吗？我曾经打败过天庭的整支大军。"

  悟净皱起了眉头。

> "是的，悟空，那些我们都知道。但是这次不一样。唐僧和我们在一起，我们必须保护他的安全。"

## ⑦

  八戒点了点头。

> "没错，我也想保命。"

  悟空叹了口气。

> "你们说得对。我们不能让妖怪伤害师父。"

  他白了八戒一眼，笑了。

> "也不能让妖怪伤害猪。"

  八戒哼了一声。

## ⑧

> "你们在这里等着，"悟空说，"我去山上看看妖怪大军有多大。"

  猴子飞到山上。有几个妖怪在洞口谈话。他们打扮成军官的样子。悟空躲在一块石头后面偷听。

## ⑨

> "唐僧很快就要经过我们这座山了，"一个妖怪将军说，"大王要所有兄弟盯紧他。"

> "希望能抓住唐僧，"一个队长说，"听说吃了他就能长生不老。"

  悟空听了怒火中烧。

## ⑩

> "但是轮到谁吃唐僧呢？"另一个队长问。

> "我们大军有几千人，大家都能吃上，"将军说，"大王答应和全军分享。"

  其他军官都点头微笑。

## ⑪

> "大王真是太好了，"一个队长说。

> "不过我们要小心，"将军警告道。

> "为什么？"一个校尉问，"唐僧不过是个凡人，怎么可能挡得住我们？"

## ⑫

> "听说唐僧不是一个人，"将军说，"孙悟空和他一起。"

  所有人都沉默了。

> "他就是五百年前打败天庭大军的齐天大圣，"一个队长嘀咕。

  另一个队长点头。

> "他确实厉害。"

  悟空听了暗自得意。

## ⑬

> "不用担心孙悟空，"将军说，"只管叫弟兄们盯紧唐僧。"

> "遵命，将军，"校尉说。

## ⑭

  我有主意了，悟空想。

  猴子变成了一个妖怪将军的模样，然后从石头后面走出来，快步走向军官们。

> "好消息！"悟空大喊，"唐僧正在向我们这座山走来！"
