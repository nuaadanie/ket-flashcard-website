# Journey to the West · Chapter Fourteen: A Promise to Protect

## ①

  The Tang Monk trembled with fear. The angry voice spoke again.

  "Who is walking on my mountain?"

  "Oh!" cried the Tang Monk. He fell off his horse and tumbled down the mountain.

  At the bottom, he saw a crack between the rocks. The monk looked in and saw a moss-covered monkey peering out at him.

  "My name is Sun Wukong," said the monkey. "I am King of the Fruit and Flower Mountain."

  "Why are you in there?" asked the Tang Monk. He stood up.

  "Buddha put me here," said Wukong. "I am being punished for causing trouble in Heaven. Who are you?"

  "I am just a monk," said the Tang Monk. "I am going to the Western Paradise to get scriptures from Buddha."

## ②

  Wukong's eyes lit up. "You are the Tang Monk!" he cried. "I am supposed to go with you."

  The Tang Monk took a step back. Was this strange monkey telling the truth?

  "The Bodhisattva Guanyin visited me," Wukong went on. "She said I would be set free if I promised to protect you on the journey. Set me free and I will go with you to the West."

  The Tang Monk relaxed. He looked at the rocks. "How can I free you?"

  "There is a small piece of paper on top of this mountain," said Wukong. "You must remove it."

## ③

  The Tang Monk went back up the mountain. He pushed aside shrubs and brambles and pulled himself over large boulders. Finally, he reached the mountain's peak.

  The monk picked up the paper. Right away a breeze swept it from his hand. The paper floated up and then disappeared among the clouds.

  "The paper floated away," he said to the monkey.

  "It returned to Buddha," said Wukong. "Step back. Go farther," said Wukong. The monk took another step back. "Go farther," said Wukong again.

  The Tang Monk went far from the mountain.

## ④

  He gasped as the top of the mountain rose into the air. Wukong stood under the mountain, holding it up with his hands. He jumped out and the mountain crashed down behind him.

  Wukong grabbed the Tang Monk's hands. "Thank you, Master. I will help you reach the Western Paradise."

  The Tang Monk rode the horse as Wukong walked. The monkey told the monk about himself.

  "The Jade Emperor's entire army could not defeat me," he laughed. "So I said I should be in charge of Heaven."

  "What powers do you have?" asked the Tang Monk.

  "I can become other animals," said Wukong. "Watch this."

  He quickly changed into a tiger. The Tang Monk gulped. Wukong changed into a snake, a bird, and then back into himself.

## ⑤

  "And that is not all," he said. "I can grow big."

  The monkey's body grew so big that his head reached the clouds.

  "I can become tiny too," said Wukong. He shrank to the size of a bean and hopped onto the saddle. He changed back to his normal size. His iron bar appeared from his ear. The monkey spun it around and then struck a huge boulder. The boulder exploded into tiny pieces. The Tang Monk watched in horror.

  Wukong laughed. "Do not look so scared, Master. I am protecting you."

## ⑥

  The two traveled farther until they saw a group of men blocking the road. The men looked rough and mean. They all had weapons. One man stepped forward.

  "Give us your money, or you will all be sorry!"

# 🇨🇳 第十四章：护行之诺

## ①

  唐僧吓得浑身发抖。那个愤怒的声音又响了起来。

  "是什么人在我山上走动？"

  "哎呀！"唐僧大叫一声，从马上摔了下来，顺着山坡滚了下去。

  在山脚，他看到岩石间有一道裂缝。他探头朝里张望，看见一只长满青苔的猴子正望着他。

  "我叫孙悟空，"猴子说，"我是花果山之王。"

  "你为什么被压在这里？"唐僧问。他站了起来。

  "是如来佛祖把我压在这里的，"悟空说，"因为我在天庭惹了大祸，这是对我的惩罚。你是什么人？"

  "我只是个普通和尚，"唐僧说，"我要去西天极乐世界，向如来佛祖取经。"

## ②

  悟空的眼睛一亮。"你就是唐僧！"他叫道，"我应该跟你一起走。"

  唐僧后退了一步。这只奇怪的猴子说的会是真话吗？

  "观音菩萨来看过我，"悟空接着说，"她说只要我答应保护你去西天取经，就会有人来放我。救我出来吧，我跟你一起向西走。"

  唐僧放松了一些。他看了看压在悟空身上的山石。"我怎么救你呢？"

  "这座山顶上有一张小小的纸符，"悟空说，"你把它拿走就行了。"

## ③

  唐僧又往山上爬去。他拨开灌木和荆棘，翻过大块的巨石，终于登上了山顶。

  和尚把那张纸拿起来。顿时一阵风把纸从他手中吹走。纸飘上天空，消失在云层里。

  "纸被风吹走了，"他对猴子说。

  "它回到佛祖那里去了，"悟空说，"往后退，再退远些。"

  唐僧又退了几步。"再远一点，"悟空又说了一遍。

  唐僧离山远远地站定了。

## ④

  只见山顶轰然升起。悟空站在山下，双手托住了大山。他纵身一跃跳了出来，身后的山轰隆一声塌了下去。

  悟空一把握住唐僧的手。"多谢师父，我一定帮你到西天取得真经。"

  唐僧骑上马，悟空在前面步行。这只猴子向师父讲述了自己的经历。

  "玉皇大帝的整支天兵天将都打不过我，"他大笑着说，"所以我说这管天庭的位子应该让给我坐。"

  "你都有什么本事？"唐僧问。

  "我会七十二变，能变成各种动物，"悟空说，"你看好了。"

  他摇身一变变成了一只老虎。唐僧倒吸一口凉气。悟空又变成了一条蛇、一只飞鸟，然后变回了自己的模样。

## ⑤

  "还不止呢，"他说，"我能变得很高大。"

  悟空的身体越长越大，头顶直穿云霄。

  "我也能变得很小，"悟空说。他缩小到一粒豆子那么大，跳上了马鞍，然后恢复正常大小。他的金箍棒从耳朵里出现。悟空抡起棒子，朝一块巨石打过去。巨石爆炸，碎成无数小块。唐僧看得目瞪口呆。

  悟空哈哈大笑。"别害怕，师父，我是来保护你的。"

## ⑥

  两个又走了一程，忽然看见一群人在前头拦住了去路。那些人面目凶恶，个个手拿兵器。其中一个人走上前来。

  "把钱财交出来，不然有你们好看！"
