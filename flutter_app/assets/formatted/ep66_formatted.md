# Journey to the West · Chapter Sixty-Six: Help from Heaven

## ①

  One-Horned Buffalo went back into his cave and slammed the door shut.

> "He stole my iron bar," cried Wukong. "What am I going to do?"

  He thought for a moment.

> "That demon said he remembered me. He also mentioned the Jade Emperor. Maybe that demon escaped from Heaven."

## ②

  The monkey shot into the sky and soon arrived at the Southern Gate of Heaven. He told the guard everything that had happened.

  The guard sent a messenger to see if any spirits were missing from Heaven. The messenger returned quickly with a warrior. The warrior held a large sword.

## ③

> "The Emperor sent me," said the warrior. "Nobody is missing from Heaven. But I will arrest One-Horned Buffalo for you."

  Wukong returned to earth with the warrior. The warrior banged on the cave door.

> "Open up, One-Horned Buffalo!"

## ④

  The demon came outside and eyed the warrior.

> "What do you want?"

  The warrior puffed out his chest.

> "I'm here to arrest you."

  One-Horned Buffalo sneered.

> "Go away."

> "How dare you tell me to go away?" said the warrior. "The Jade Emperor sent me." He pointed his sword at the demon. "If you don't come with me, I'll have you."

## ⑤

  One-Horned Buffalo held up the metal ring and *whoosh* -- the warrior's sword flew into it.

  The warrior's face fell as he looked at his empty hand. The demon laughed and went back into the cave.

## ⑥

> "That metal ring is powerful," said the warrior to Wukong. "We need more help."

  They returned to Heaven and bowed before the Jade Emperor.

> "I'm sorry, Your Majesty," said the warrior. "But the demon stole my sword. I need more help."

  The Jade Emperor turned to his general.

> "Send the Fire God to earth with us, too."

## ⑦

  The Fire God joined Wukong and the warrior. The god held a large, flaming spear.

> "I'll arrest the demon for you," said the god.

  The three of them went down to earth and landed on the mountain. The Fire God stood in front of the cave door. He held up his fire spear and shouted:

> "Come out, One-Horned Buffalo. You are under arrest."

## ⑧

  The cave door opened and the demon stepped outside.

> "Who are you?"

> "I'm the Fire God, sent by the Jade Emperor," said the god.

  One-Horned Buffalo scoffed.

> "I'm not afraid of you."

  The Fire God threw the flaming spear at the demon. But once again the demon held up the metal ring, and the spear was gone. The demon returned to his cave.

## ⑨

  Wukong shook his head.

> "This is bad."

  The three of them returned to Heaven and bowed before the Jade Emperor.

> "Your Majesty," said the Fire God. "One-Horned Buffalo is very powerful. I wasn't able to arrest him. We need more help."

  The Jade Emperor sighed and turned to his general.

> "Send the Thunder God within this time."

## ⑩

  Back down on earth, Wukong watched as the Thunder God approached the cave door.

> "Be careful," warned the warrior.

> "Yes," said the Fire God. "The demon's metal ring is very dangerous."

> "Ha ha ha," the Thunder God laughed. "Don't worry about me."

## ⑪

  He turned to the cave door and boomed:

> "One-Horned Buffalo, you are under arrest."

  The cave door opened and One-Horned Buffalo stepped outside.

  The Thunder God instantly hurled his thunderbolts at the demon. One-Horned Buffalo held up the metal ring and *whoosh* -- the thunderbolts were gone.

## ⑫

  The demon looked around at everyone and laughed.

> "Thank you for all the weapons."

  He went back into his cave and slammed the door shut again.

# 🇨🇳 第六十六章：天兵相助


## ①

  独角牛魔王回到洞里，用力关上了门。

> "他偷走了我的金箍棒，"悟空哭喊道，"我该怎么办？"

  他想了一会儿。

> "那个妖怪说他记得我，还提到了玉皇大帝。也许那个妖怪是从天上逃下来的。"

## ②

  猴子飞上了天空，很快就到了南天门。他把发生的一切告诉了守卫。

  守卫派了一名使者去查看天上有没有少什么灵官。使者很快带着一名天将回来了。天将手中握着一把大剑。

## ③

> "是玉帝派我来的，"天将说，"天上没有缺少什么灵官，不过我愿意帮你们去捉独角牛魔王。"

  悟空和天将回到了地上。天将用力拍打洞门。

> "开门，独角牛魔王！"

## ④

  妖怪走了出来，打量了一番天将。

> "你们想干什么？"

  天将挺起胸膛。

> "我来捉拿你。"

  独角牛魔王冷笑了一声。

> "滚吧。"

> "竟敢叫我滚？"天将说，"是玉皇大帝派我来的。"他用剑指着妖怪，"你不跟我走的话，我可就不客气了。"

## ⑤

  独角牛魔王举起金属圈，"嗖"的一声，天将的剑飞进了圈里。

  天将看着自己空空的手，面如土色。妖怪大笑着走回洞中。

## ⑥

> "那个金属圈法力高强，"天将对悟空说，"我们需要更多的帮手。"

  他们回到了天庭，跪拜在玉帝面前。

> "陛下，臣惭愧，"天将说，"妖怪偷走了臣的剑，臣需要更多的帮手。"

  玉帝转身对将军下令。

> "派火神下凡帮忙。"

## ⑦

  火神加入了悟空和天将的队伍。火神手中握着一柄烈焰长矛。

> "我去帮你们捉拿妖怪，"火神说。

  三人下到凡间，落在山上。火神站在洞门前，举起火矛大喊道。

> "独角牛魔王，滚出来！你被捕了。"

## ⑧

  洞门开了，妖怪走了出来。

> "你是什么人？"

> "我是玉皇大帝派来的火神，"火神说。

  独角牛魔王轻蔑地哼了一声。

> "我才不怕你呢。"

  火神将烈焰长矛朝妖怪掷去。但妖怪又一次举起金属圈，长矛瞬间消失了。妖怪回到了洞里。

## ⑨

  悟空摇了摇头。

> "这可不妙。"

  三人又回到了天庭，跪拜在玉帝面前。

> "陛下，"火神说，"独角牛魔王太厉害了，臣没能捉拿他，需要更多帮手。"

  玉帝叹了叹气，对将军说。

> "这次派雷神去吧。"

## ⑩

  又回到了凡间，悟空看着雷神走向洞门。

> "小心点，"天将提醒道。

> "是啊，"火神也说，"那个妖怪的金属圈很危险。"

> "哈哈哈，"雷神大笑着，"不用担心我。"

## ⑪

  他转过来对着洞门大喊道。

> "独角牛魔王，你被捕了！"

  洞门开了，独角牛魔王走了出来。

  雷神立刻将雷电朝妖怪掷去。独角牛魔王举起金属圈，"嗖"的一声，雷电消失了。

## ⑫

  妖怪看着众人，哈哈大笑。

> "谢谢你们送的所有兵器。"

  他又走回洞里，再次重重关上了门。
