# Journey to the West · Chapter One Hundred and Three: Wukong Returns to the Land of Darkness

## ①

> "The Squire is coming back to life!" said the Tang monk.

  Squire Kou sat up and smiled inside his coffin. Wukong had leaned over the coffin and recited a spell.

  Lady Kou gasped as color returned to her husband's face.

> "It's working!" cried Wujing.

## ②

  Bajie licked his lips.

> "Maybe the Squire will thank us with a big feast!"

  Squire Kou sat up and smiled.

> "Thank you, Wukong."

  He climbed out of the coffin and turned to his wife. Tears filled her eyes, and the two embraced.

## ③

  That night, Squire Kou and Lady Kou held a feast to thank Wukong. And the next morning, they let the travelers leave without any argument.

  *Click-clack, click-clack* — the horse's hooves sounded across the earth.

  The Tang monk and his companions travelled for many more months. Through rain and snow, heat and cold, they slowly continued west.

## ④

  One day, the road led into a beautiful land. There were flowers and magnificent trees. People smiled at the travelers and offered food. The Tang monk stared in amazement.

> "What is this place?"

> "We are in the Western Paradise," said Wukong. "We are very close."

## ⑤

  A woman held out a basket of plums. Bajie scooped up a handful.

> "I love this place!"

> "Look up ahead," said Wujing, pointing to a row of tall buildings. "It's an abbey."

  The road led through a large gate in the abbey wall. At the gate, the monk got down from his horse. A young man greeted them.

## ⑥

> "Are you the Tang monk?" asked the man.

> "Yes," said the monk. He waved towards the rest of the group. "And these are my —"

> "You finally arrived!" the man laughed. "I was starting to think that Bodhisattva Guanyin had tricked me. She told me you would arrive in a year or so. But that was over ten years ago."

## ⑦

  The Tang monk bowed.

> "Forgive me. I wanted to arrive much sooner, but we faced many dangers and delays along the way."

  Bajie snorted.

> "And this place wasn't very easy to find either."

## ⑧

  The man smiled.

> "There's no need to apologize. I'm the Great Immortal of the Golden Summit, and this abbey is my home. Beyond it lies the Western Paradise. Come inside and rest before you continue."

  Servants filled tubs with warm perfumed water, and the travelers took baths.

## ⑨

> "It feels so good to get clean," said Bajie. "I haven't had a bath in months."

  Wukong laughed.

> "Now we won't have to cover our noses when you're near."

  Bajie snorted and splashed water at Wukong. The monkey splashed back. Everyone laughed.

## ⑩

  After their baths, the Tang monk put on the Buddhist robe Guanyin had given them. Then the travelers enjoyed a large meal with the Immortal. Servants brought trays loaded with noodles, vegetables, tofu, and steamed buns.

## ⑪

  In the morning, the Immortal let the group out the back door of the abbey. The road continued west through a land even more beautiful and magical. Birds sang as animals leaped playfully through the flowers. Brilliant light sparkled on everything.

## ⑫

  In the distance stood a huge mountain, its peak hidden by clouds. The Tang monk stared silently at the mountain. Wukong put his hand on the monk's shoulder.

> "That's the Spirit Mountain, Master. We're almost at the end of our journey."

  A smile spread across the monk's face.

## ⑬

  The travelers thanked the Immortal. Filled with energy now, they hurried down the road. The steep slopes of the Spirit Mountain towered over them.

# 🇨🇳 第一百零三章：悟空重归幽冥界

## ①

  "员外还阳了！"唐僧叫道。悟空趴在棺材上念了一道还阳咒。

  寇员外在棺材里坐了起来，脸上露出了笑容。

  寇夫人惊叫着看着丈夫的脸色渐渐红润。

> "生效了！"悟净大喊。

## ②

  八戒舔了舔嘴唇。

> "说不定员外会用一顿大餐来谢谢我们呢！"

  寇员外坐起来，脸上带着笑。

> "悟空，谢谢你。"

  他从棺材里爬出来，转向妻子。寇夫人眼中泪光闪闪，两人紧紧拥抱在一起。

## ③

  那天晚上，寇员外和寇夫人设宴款待，以感谢悟空。第二天一早，他们没再挽留，送行者们上路了。

  *嗒嗒嗒，嗒嗒嗒*——马蹄声响彻大地。

  唐僧师徒又赶了许多个月的路。风雨无阻，寒暑交替，他们继续向西。

## ④

  一天，前方的道路通向了一片美丽的土地。到处是鲜花和奇树异木。人们微笑着向旅人们招手，还送食物给他们。唐僧目瞪口呆。

> "这是什么地方？"

> "我们已到西天了，"悟空说，"极乐世界近在眼前。"

## ⑤

  一个妇人端出一篮李子。八戒伸手抓了一大把。

> "我太喜欢这个地方了！"

> "看前面，"悟净指向一排高大的建筑，"是一座庵院。"

  道路穿过庵院的一扇大门。到了门口，唐僧下了马。一个年轻人出来迎接他们。

## ⑥

> "您就是唐僧吗？"那人问道。

> "是的，"唐僧说。他指向身后一行人。"这是我的——"

> "你们终于到了！"那人大笑起来，"我差点以为观音菩萨骗了我呢。她说你们一年左右就能到。可这已经是十多年前的事了！"

## ⑦

  唐僧鞠躬致歉。

> "请原谅。我本想早些到，但一路上遭遇了许多危险和耽搁。"

  八戒哼了一声。

> "再说了，也不好找啊。"

## ⑧

  那人笑了。

> "不用道歉。我是金顶大仙，这庵院就是我的家。庵院后面就是西天极乐世界了。你们先进来歇息一番再走吧。"

  侍从们端来了温热的香汤，旅人们美美地洗了个澡。

## ⑨

> "洗澡真舒服，"八戒说，"我可有好几个月没洗澡了。"

  悟空笑了笑。

> "这下你走近了我们也不用捂鼻子了。"

  八戒哼了一声，朝悟空泼水。悟空也泼了回去。大家哈哈大笑。

## ⑩

  洗完澡后，唐僧穿上了观音送的锦襕袈裟。旅人们和大仙一起享用了一顿丰盛的饭菜。侍从们端来了面条、蔬菜、豆腐和包子。

## ⑪

  第二天一早，大仙从庵院后门送一行人出去。道路继续向西延伸，沿途的土地更加美丽奇妙。鸟儿歌唱，小动物在花丛中欢蹦乱跳。万物闪耀着灿烂的光芒。

## ⑫

  远处矗立着一座巨大的山，山顶隐在云中。唐僧默默地注视着那座山。悟空把手放到唐僧肩上。

> "那就是灵山，师父。我们的旅程快到终点了。"

  唐僧脸上绽开了笑容。

## ⑬

  旅人们谢过了大仙。他们满怀能量，沿着道路快步赶路。灵山的陡峭山壁巍然矗立在他们面前。
