# Journey to the West · Chapter Fifty-Two: An Ocean from a Vase

## ①

  Wukong and Guanyin stood on the lotus platform above Red Boy's mountain.

> "Let's attack Red Boy now!" said the monkey.

> "Wait, Wukong," said Guanyin, holding up a hand. "There's a lot of water in this vase. Enough for an ocean. I don't want any animals harmed when I pour it out."

  She recited a quick spell. A moment later, the local spirit arrived.

> "What is your order?" said the spirit. "What can I do for you?"

> "I'm going to pour magic water around Red Boy's mountain," said Guanyin. "Please make sure all the animals are far away from the mountain."

## ②

> "Yes, my lady!" said the spirit.

  The spirit flew down to Earth. Wukong watched the ground below. First, he saw some small animals scurry out from under bushes. Birds flew from branches. Then tigers, bears, and other large animals stampeded down the mountainside. Soon there was not a single animal anywhere near the mountain.

> "Now I can pour the water out," said Guanyin.

  She took the vase and rolling waves of water poured out. The waves washed down the side of the mountain. The water level rose higher and higher, forming an ocean. Only the top of the mountain was above water.

## ③

> "Wukong, go down there and get the demon to come out," said Guanyin. "Fight him, but don't defeat him. Instead, lead him up here to me."

  Wukong jumped down to the mountain. He pulled out his iron bar and swung it. The door to the cave exploded. Red Boy ran out of the cave looking furious.

> "You smashed my front door!" he shouted.

> "That's right," said the monkey. "And I'll smash you if you don't release my master."

## ④

  Red Boy laughed and then punched himself in the nose. Nothing happened.

> "Why didn't my fire magic work?" asked the demon.

  Then he noticed the ocean surrounding his mountain. Wukong laughed.

> "Ha ha ha ha ha! That is magic water brought here by the Bodhisattva Guanyin. You can't make fire anywhere near it!"

> "Ah!" Red Boy stabbed at Wukong with his lance.

  Clang! The monkey blocked the lance with his iron bar.

  Red Boy grunted and stabbed again. Wukong swung his iron bar, nearly knocking the lance from Red Boy's hands.

## ⑤

  The two fought until Wukong pretended to be scared. He leaped into the air. Red Boy followed.

  Up into the sky they flew. Red Boy kept jabbing his lance at Wukong. The monkey blocked it with his iron bar.

  Wukong hurried toward Guanyin on the lotus platform. He flew fast and ducked behind her. When Red Boy saw the Bodhisattva, he stopped.

> "What are you doing here, Bodhisattva?" he demanded.

  Guanyin stared back at the demon, but she didn't say a word.

## ⑥

> "I asked why you're here!" shouted Red Boy.

  Again Guanyin didn't answer.

> "Did Wukong call after you to come here?" shouted the demon. "Why don't you speak?"

  Guanyin continued to stare silently at Red Boy.

  The demon became furious. He raised his lance and struck toward Guanyin.

  The Bodhisattva vanished.

## ⑦

  Red Boy laughed. He looked at the empty lotus platform.

> "Wukong is a fool! He couldn't defeat my fire magic, so he asked Guanyin for help. Then what did she do? She ran away as soon as I raised my weapon. Now this lotus throne is mine!"

  Red Boy sat down on the platform. Instantly the platform turned into swords, which surrounded the demon. Sharp points stuck to him on all sides.

> "Oh no!" cried Red Boy.

# 🇨🇳 第五十二章：瓶中大海


## ①

  悟空和观音站在莲花台上，俯瞰红孩儿的山头。

> "现在就去攻打红孩儿吧！"猴子说。

> "等等，悟空，"观音举起手来，"这瓶子里有很多水，足足装得下一个海洋。我不希望倒水的时候伤到任何动物。"

  她念了一段咒语。不一会儿，当地的土地神来了。

> "请问有何吩咐？"神灵说，"我能为您做什么？"

> "我要在红孩儿的山的周围倒下甘露，"观音说，"请确保所有动物都离开这座山。"

## ②

> "遵命，菩萨！"神灵说。

  神灵飞下凡间。悟空注视着下方。首先他看到一些小动物从灌木丛中逃了出来。鸟儿从树枝上飞走。接着老虎、熊和其他大型动物也从山坡上奔逃而下。不一会儿，这座山附近一只动物也没有了。

> "现在我可以倒水了，"观音说。

  她拿起净瓶，滚滚波涛倾泻而出。巨浪顺着山坡奔涌而下，水位越来越高，汇成了一片汪洋。只有山顶还露出水面。

## ③

> "悟空，你下去把那妖怪引出来，"观音说，"跟他打，但别打败他。把他引到我这里来。"

  悟空跳下山去，掏出金箍棒一棒挥下，洞门轰然爆炸。红孩儿怒气冲冲地从洞里跑了出来。

> "你砸碎了我的大门！"他大喊道。

> "没错，"猴子说，"你要是不放出我师父，我就把你一块儿砸了！"

## ④

  红孩儿大笑起来，然后朝鼻子捶了一拳。什么也没发生。

> "我的三昧真火怎么不灵了？"妖怪问道。

  然后他注意到山四周变成了汪洋大海。悟空大笑起来。

> "哈哈哈！这是观音菩萨带来的甘露，你在这儿可放不了火！"

> "啊！"红孩儿挺枪朝悟空刺去。

  铛！猴子用金箍棒挡住了枪。

  红孩儿哼了一声，又是一枪。悟空挥动金箍棒，差点把枪从红孩儿手里打落。

## ⑤

  两人打了一阵，悟空装作害怕，纵身跃上了天空。红孩儿追了上去。

  他们飞上天空，红孩儿不停地用枪刺杀悟空，猴子用金箍棒一一挡住。

  悟空飞回莲花台上观音菩萨身边，飞快躲到了她身后。红孩儿看到菩萨，停下了手。

> "你怎么在这儿，菩萨？"他质问道。

  观音盯着妖怪，一言不发。

## ⑥

> "我问你在这儿干什么！"红孩儿大喊道。

  观音还是没回答。

> "是悟空请你来的吗？"妖怪大喊道，"你怎么不说话？"

  观音继续默默注视着红孩儿。

  妖怪勃然大怒，举起长枪向观音刺去。

  菩萨消失了。

## ⑦

  红孩儿大笑起来，他看着空荡荡的莲花台。

> "悟空真是个蠢货！他打不过我的火，就去请观音帮忙。结果呢？我一举兵器她就跑了。这莲花台归我了！"

  红孩儿坐到了莲花台上。刹那间，莲花台变成了宝剑，从四面八方将妖怪围住。锋利的剑尖刺入他的身体。

> "不好了！"红孩儿大叫道。
