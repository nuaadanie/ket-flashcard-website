# Journey to the West · Chapter Nine: The Peach Garden

## ①

  Wukong plucked a peach from a branch and bit into it. It was so juicy and sweet.

  He said, "It is delicious!" He ate another peach. And then he ate another one. Each morning after that, Wukong secretly ate more and more peaches. After eating, he climbed into a tree and took a nap. Time went by, and Wukong enjoyed his life in heaven.

## ②

  Meanwhile, the queen of heaven was busy. It was almost time for the annual peach festival. She called for her maidens, Red Gown and Blue Gown.

> "How may we help you?" asked Red Gown.

> "The peach festival is tomorrow," said the queen. "All the important gods and spirits in heaven will be here. I also invited the bodhisattva Guanyin, the goddess of mercy. Go to the peach garden now and pick plenty of ripe peaches."

  Red Gown and Blue Gown went to the garden. As soon as they went through the gate, both maidens gasped.

> "Where are all the peaches?" cried Red Gown.

> "They're gone," cried Blue Gown.

> "This is terrible," said Red Gown. "The queen will be angry."

## ③

  Wukong had been sleeping up on a branch. When he heard the maidens, he woke up.

> "Who is making all this noise?" he asked, jumping down to the ground. "I am trying to sleep."

  The maidens were startled.

> "We are sorry to wake you, sir," said Red Gown. "We came to pick peaches for the peach festival. Do you know what happened to all the peaches?"

> "The peach festival?" said Wukong. His eyes lit up. "I did not hear about that."

> "Oh, it is a wonderful festival," said Blue Gown. "All the important gods and spirits will be there."

> "That sounds like fun," said Wukong. "I am sure I am invited, right?"

  Red Gown bit her lip.

> "Um, I don't think so."

  Wukong was furious.

> "The queen did not invite me? I am just as important as any other spirit or god!"

  The maidens looked nervous.

> "Sir," said Blue Gown. "Where are the peaches?"

> "I ate them," Wukong said. "I ate all of them, every single one. And they were delicious."

> "Oh no!" cried Red Gown. "We must tell the queen!"

> "No, you are not going to tell the queen," said Wukong. He quickly cast a spell. Instantly, Red Gown and Blue Gown fell asleep.

  Wukong looked at all the empty peach trees. He shook his head.

  "I am in big trouble."

## ④

  When Red Gown and Blue Gown woke up, they ran back to the Cloud Palace.

> "Your Majesty," cried Red Gown. "Something terrible has happened. Sun Wukong ate all the peaches."

> "He got very angry and cast a spell on us," added Blue Gown. "When we woke up, he was gone."

  The queen's eyes flashed with anger. She went to the royal court and told the Jade Emperor.

> "That horrible monkey!" cried the Emperor. He turned to his captain. "Gather 100,000 soldiers. I want Wukong arrested."

  The captain nodded and then left the palace.

## ⑤

  Meanwhile, the bodhisattva Guanyin left her home on Mount Potalaka in the South Sea. She soon arrived at the Cloud Palace.

> "I am sorry to tell you this," said the queen. "But there will not be a peach festival."

> "Why not?" asked Guanyin.

> "A monkey named Sun Wukong ate all the peaches," said the Emperor. "I sent 100,000 soldiers to arrest him. They will bring him back to heaven soon."

  Just then, a messenger ran into the court.

> "We need more soldiers, Your Majesty!"

> "What?" cried the Emperor.

> "Wukong defeated the entire army," said the messenger.

  The Jade Emperor put his head in his hands.

> "We do not have any more soldiers."

# 🇨🇳 第九章：蟠桃园

## ①

  悟空从树枝上摘下一个桃子，咬了一口。汁水四溢，香甜可口。

  他说："太好吃了！"他又吃了一个，接着又吃了一个。从那以后，每天早晨，悟空都偷偷地吃越来越多的桃子。吃完之后，他就爬上树去睡个午觉。日子一天天过去，悟空在天庭享受着快乐的生活。

## ②

  与此同时，王母娘娘正忙着。一年一度的蟠桃盛会马上就要到了。她召来了她的侍女红袄和蓝袄。

> "娘娘有什么吩咐？"红袄问道。

> "蟠桃盛会明天就要举行了，"王母说。"天上所有重要的神仙都会来。我还请了南海观世音菩萨。你们现在就去蟠桃园，多摘些熟透的桃子来。"

  红袄和蓝袄来到了桃园。一进园门，两位侍女都倒吸了一口凉气。

> "桃子都到哪里去了？"红袄惊叫道。

> "全都不见了，"蓝袄叫道。

> "这可糟了，"红袄说。"娘娘一定会生气的。"

## ③

  悟空正睡在一根树枝上。听到侍女们的声音，他醒了过来。

> "是谁在这儿这么吵闹？"他一边问，一边跳到地上。"我正睡觉呢。"

  侍女们吓了一跳。

> "惊扰了您，真对不起，"红袄说。"我们来为蟠桃盛会摘桃子。您知道桃子都到哪里去了吗？"

> "蟠桃盛会？"悟空说。他眼睛一亮。"我可没听说啊。"

> "那可是一个了不起的盛会呢，"蓝袄说。"所有重要的神仙都会到场。"

> "听起来真好玩，"悟空说。"我肯定也在邀请之列，对吧？"

  红袄咬了咬嘴唇。

> "嗯，我想恐怕没有。"

  悟空勃然大怒。

> "王母娘娘没有邀请我？我和任何神仙一样重要！"

  侍女们看起来很紧张。

> "大人，"蓝袄说。"桃子在哪里？"

> "被我吃了，"悟空说。"全都吃了，一个不剩。而且都好吃极了。"

> "糟了！"红袄叫道。"我们必须禀告娘娘！"

> "不行，你们不能去禀告娘娘，"悟空说。他迅速施了一个法术。红袄和蓝袄立刻睡着了。

  悟空看着光秃秃的桃树，摇了摇头。

  "这下我可惹了大麻烦了。"

## ④

  红袄和蓝袄醒来后，急忙跑回了天宫。

> "娘娘，"红袄哭喊道。"出大事了。孙悟空把所有的桃子都吃光了。"

> "他非常生气，还对我们施了法术，"蓝袄接着说。"我们醒来的时候，他已经不见了。"

  王母娘娘眼中怒火闪烁。她来到凌霄宝殿，禀告了玉皇大帝。

> "这只可恶的猴子！"玉帝大叫。他转向天将。"点起十万天兵天将，我要把悟空捉拿归案。"

  天将点了点头，随即离开天宫。

## ⑤

  与此同时，观世音菩萨从南海普陀洛迦山出发。她很快就赶到了天宫。

> "很抱歉告诉您，"王母娘娘说。"蟠桃盛会办不成了。"

> "为什么呢？"观音问道。

> "一个叫孙悟空的猴子吃光了所有的桃子，"玉帝说。"我已经派了十万天兵天将去捉拿他。很快就能把他带回天庭了。"

  就在这时，一个信使跑进大殿。

> "陛下，我们需要更多兵马！"

> "什么？"玉帝惊叫。

> "悟空打败了整个天军，"信使说。

  玉皇大帝以手扶额。

> "我们再也没有更多的兵马了。"
