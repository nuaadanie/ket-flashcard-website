# Journey to the West · Chapter Thirty-Seven: Missing from Heaven

## ①

> "Who are you?" the monster asked the monkey. "What are you doing here?"

> "I am Sun Wukong, King of the Fruit and Flower Mountain," said the monkey. "I'm helping the Tang Monk reach the Western Paradise. Five hundred years ago, I caused a lot of trouble in heaven. Now I'm here to cause a lot of trouble for you."

> "I remember you," said the monster.

  Wukong pulled out his iron bar and swung it. *Whoosh!* The monster vanished.

## ②

  Wukong leaped into the air. He looked in every direction but didn't see the monster anywhere.

> "That monster said he remembered me," thought Wukong. "He must be from heaven. I'll go up there now to see if any spirits are missing."

  Wukong soared higher and higher until he reached the Southern Gate of Heaven.

> "Greetings, Wukong!" said the guard.

> "There's a monster on Earth causing trouble for the Tang Monk," said Wukong. "I think the monster is a spirit who escaped from heaven. Is anyone missing?"

## ③

  The guard sent a messenger to check. A moment later, the messenger returned with a guard.

> "You found the Wood Wolf Star," said the guard.

> "Who?" asked Wukong.

> "The Wood Wolf Star is a wolf spirit who lives in heaven," said the guard. "He escaped thirteen days ago and I've been looking all over for him."

  Wukong rubbed his chin.

> "That makes sense. The monster kidnapped a princess thirteen years ago on Earth. Thirteen years on Earth is equal to thirteen days in heaven."

## ④

  The guard nodded.

> "Yes. Let's go. I'll help you catch the Wood Wolf Star."

  Wukong went down to Earth with the guard. From the sky, the guard called down.

> "Wolf Star! Come up here at once!"

  Wukong saw a small splash in a river below. The monster leaped out. The monster turned into a wolf and flew up to the god. The wolf looked nervous.

## ⑤

> "You will be punished," said the god. He turned and led the wolf back up to heaven.

  Wukong flew to the Precious Image Kingdom and went into the palace. A big celebration was taking place. The king and queen were both hugging their daughter, Princess Hundred Flowers. The princess saw Wukong and smiled.

> "Father, Mother, this is Sun Wukong. He's a monkey spirit who rescued me and Sha Wu Jing earlier."

  The king and queen bowed.

> "We can never thank you enough," said the queen. "You and your friends have made us very happy."

## ⑥

> "It was nothing at all," said Wukong with a laugh. "We save people from monsters and demons almost every day."

  He looked around.

> "Where's the Tang Monk?"

  The king looked at the ground.

> "Didn't your friends tell you? He's really a tiger."

> "That's it," said Wukong. "That monster turned him into a tiger. That's all. Where is he?"

  The king and queen led Wukong out of the hall. They went down a flight of stairs and arrived at the palace dungeon. A large tiger sat in a cage.

## ⑦

> "Don't worry, Master," said Wukong. "I'll change you back into yourself."

  Wukong recited a spell. Bright light surrounded the cage for a moment. The Tang Monk was back. Wukong grabbed the bars and twisted them apart.

> "Thank you, Wukong!" cried the monk. He took the monkey's hands in his own. "I never should have told you to leave. Will you continue to journey with us?"

  Wukong smiled.

> "Of course, Master. We will reach the Western Paradise together."

# 🇨🇳 第三十七章：天宫失踪


## ①

> "你是谁？"妖怪问道，"你来这里干什么？"

> "我是花果山大王孙悟空，"猴子说，"我是护送唐僧前往西天取经的。五百年前，我在天庭大闹了一场。现在轮到你尝尝我的厉害了。"

> "我记得你，"妖怪说道。

  悟空掏出铁棒，抡了一下。呼！妖怪消失了。

## ②

  悟空跃入空中，四下张望，却到处都找不到妖怪的踪影。

> "那妖怪说记得我，"悟空想，"他一定来自天庭。我上去看看有没有哪路神仙不见了。"

  悟空越飞越高，一直飞到了南天门外。

> "悟空，你好！"守卫说。

> "地球上有个妖怪正在找唐僧的麻烦，"悟空说，"我猜他是从天庭逃走的精灵。你们有没有谁丢了？"

## ③

  守卫派了一名信使去查看。不一会儿，信使带着一位天神回来了。

> "就是木狼星，"天神说。

> "谁？"悟空问。

> "木狼星是天庭的一只狼精，"天神说，"他十三天前逃走了，我一直在到处找他。"

  悟空摸了摸下巴。

> "这就对了。妖怪十三年前在人间掳走了一位公主。人间十三年就是天庭十三天。"

## ④

  天神点了点头。

> "不错。走吧，我来帮你捉拿木狼星。"

  悟空跟随天神下到了人间。天神在空中向下喊叫。

> "狼星！立刻上来！"

  悟空看见下方小河里溅起一阵水花。妖怪一跃而出，变成了一只狼，飞到了天神身边。狼一脸慌张。

## ⑤

> "你将会受到惩罚，"天神说。他转身带着狼回到了天庭。

  悟空飞回宝象国，进入了宫殿。宫殿里正在举行盛大的庆祝活动，国王和王后都拥抱着他们的女儿百花公主。公主看到悟空，露出了微笑。

> "父王、母后，这位是孙悟空，他是一位猴仙，之前救出了我和沙悟净。"

  国王和王后鞠躬致谢。

> "我们感激不尽，"王后说，"你和你的朋友们让我们太高兴了。"

## ⑥

> "这算不了什么，"悟空大笑着说，"我们每天都得从妖怪手中救人呢。"

  他四下看了看。

> "唐僧呢？"

  国王低下了头。

> "你的伙伴没告诉你吗？他变成了一只老虎。"

> "哼，我就知道，"悟空说，"是那个妖怪把他变成老虎的。他在哪里？"

  国王和王后带着悟空出大殿，走下台阶，来到了地牢。一只大老虎被关在笼子里。

## ⑦

> "别担心，师父，"悟空说，"我马上就变回来。"

  悟空念起了咒语。明亮的光芒把笼子笼罩了一会儿。唐僧就恢复了人形。悟空抓住铁栏，用力一掰，把牢笼扯开了。

> "谢谢你，悟空！"唐僧叫道。他把猴子的手紧紧握住，"我真不该赶你走。你还愿意跟我们一起取经吗？"

  悟空笑了。

> "当然愿意，师父。我们一起到达西天。"
