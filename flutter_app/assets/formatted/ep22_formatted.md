# Journey to the West · Chapter Twenty-Two: A Very Strange Pill

## ①

  Journey to the West, Chapter 22: A Very Strange Pill.

  The Black Wind King took a step back. He looked confused. "That pill jumped down my throat!"

  The entire cave filled with light. The bear squeezed his eyes shut. When he opened them, Guanyin was standing before him.

  "Guanyin!" the bear cried. "You tricked me with a disguise!"

## ②

  "Return the robe at once," said Guanyin.

  "No!" said the Black Wind King. "I don't want to."

  He held his stomach and groaned.

  "Are you feeling okay?" asked Wukong's voice.

  The bear looked around, surprised. "I just heard the monkey." He fell to his knees and groaned again. "Where are you?"

  "I'm inside you," said Wukong. "I changed myself into a pill to trick you. Now I'm going to practice some of my fighting skills."

## ③

  The monkey punched and hit the bear's inside. Boom! Pow! Boom! Pow!

  "Ah!" cried the Black Wind King, rolling on the floor.

  Wukong jumped up and down, did cartwheels, and ran around in circles.

  "Please, stop!" shouted the Black Wind King. "You're hurting my belly!"

## ④

  "Give back the robe," said Guanyin.

  "I will. I promise," said the bear. "I will do anything you say. Just please tell the monkey to stop."

  Guanyin held up a metal headband. It looked just like the one on Wukong's head. She placed it on the bear's head.

  "Wukong, come out now," said Guanyin.

  A tiny Wukong popped out of the Black Wind King's nose. He quickly grew to his normal size.

## ⑤

  "Where's the robe?" asked the monkey.

  The bear pointed to a dark room in the back of the cave. After Wukong left to get the robe, the Black Wind King tried to run away. But the bear was too slow.

  Guanyin threw four more metal bands at him. Two clamped onto his ribs. The other two clamped onto his ankles.

  When the bear started making trouble, Guanyin began reciting a spell. The bear screamed.

## ⑥

  "Ah! Please stop!"

  "Do you promise to be good?" asked Guanyin.

  "Yes!" answered the Black Wind King. "I promise!"

  Wukong came back with the robe. When he saw the bear, he laughed. "You have five of those bands!"

## ⑦

  The Black Wind King looked at the ground and sighed.

  "I got the robe," said the monkey. "What should we do with the bear?"

  "I will show him mercy," said Guanyin. "He can be my guardian on Mount Potalaka if he behaves."

  The Bodhisattva led the bear out of the cave and vanished.

## ⑧

  Wukong flew back to the monastery. "You got the robe!" cried the Tang monk when he saw the monkey.

  "I did," said Wukong.

  The two travelers headed west again. Wukong told the monk about his adventure with the Black Wind King.

## ⑨

  "I destroyed the Taoist priest," the monkey explained. "He was really an evil wolf spirit. Then Guanyin made herself look like him."

  "And you turned yourself into a pill," said the Tang monk.

  "And I went right into that bear's belly," Wukong laughed. "He was very surprised."

## ⑩

  The Tang monk smiled. "I think we both learned a valuable lesson, Wukong. I will never show anyone the robe again."

  "That's a good idea," said Wukong. "The robe is a gift from Buddha. We must be careful."

  He stopped talking and pulled out his iron rod. "There's a spirit nearby."

  He looked toward a nearby cave. There was a snort. The Tang monk shook with fear. A fierce-looking creature leaped from the cave, swinging a heavy rake.

# 🇨🇳 第二十二章：奇异的仙丹


## ①

  往西去 —— 第二十二集：一粒奇怪的丹药。

  黑风大王后退了一步，他一脸困惑，"那颗丹药跳进我喉咙里了！"

  整个洞府被亮光笼罩。熊紧闭双眼。睁开眼睛时，观音已经站在他面前。

  "观音！"熊喊道，"你假扮道士骗我！"

## ②

  "马上把袈裟还回来，"观音说。

  "不！"黑风大王说，"我才不想还。"

  他捂着肚子，呻吟起来。

  "你还好吗？"传来悟空的声音。

  熊惊讶地东张西望，"我刚才听到了猴子叫。"他跪倒在地，又呻吟了一声，"你在哪里？"

  "我在你肚子里呢，"悟空说，"我变成丹药来骗你。现在我要在里面练练功夫了。"

## ③

  猴子在熊的肚子里拳打脚踢。砰！啪！砰！啪！

  "啊！"黑风大王倒在地上翻滚。

  悟空又跳又翻跟斗，在里面跑来跑去。

  "求求你，停下来吧！"黑风大王喊道，"你弄得我肚子好痛！"

## ④

  "把袈裟交出来，"观音说。

  "好的，我答应你，"熊说，"你说什么我都照做。只要你让那只猴子停下来。"

  观音举起一个金属箍圈，看起来和悟空头上的那个一模一样。她把它安在了熊的头上。

  "悟空，现在出来吧，"观音说。

  一个小小的悟空从黑风大王的鼻子里钻了出来，很快长到了正常大小。

## ⑤

  "袈裟在哪里？"悟空问。

  熊指向山洞深处的一个暗室。悟空去取袈裟的时候，黑风大王企图逃跑。但熊太慢了。

  观音又朝他掷出四个金属箍圈。两个扣在了他的肋骨上，另外两个扣在了他的脚踝上。

  熊一开始不老实，观音就开始念咒。熊痛苦地惨叫。

## ⑥

  "啊！求你停下来！"

  "你发誓改邪归正吗？"观音问。

  "我发誓！"黑风大王回答，"我发誓！"

  悟空拿着袈裟回来了，看见熊那模样，哈哈大笑，"你身上有五个箍呢！"

## ⑦

  黑风大王低头叹了叹气。

  "袈裟拿到了，"悟空说，"我们怎么处置这只熊？"

  "我饶他一命，"观音说，"如果他肯安分，就让他做我在普陀山的守卫吧。"

  菩萨领着熊走出山洞，消失不见。

## ⑧

  悟空飞回了寺院。"袈裟拿到了！"唐僧见到悟空就喊。

  "拿到了，"悟空说。

  两位旅行者再次向西进发。悟空把跟黑风大王的一番经历告诉了唐僧。

## ⑨

  "我消灭了那个道士，"悟空解释说，"他其实是个恶狼精。然后观音变成他的模样。"

  "你就把自己变成了一颗丹药，"唐僧说。

  "然后我直接钻进了那熊的肚子，"悟空笑着回忆道，"他可吓了一跳。"

## ⑩

  唐僧微微一笑，"我想我们都吸取了一个宝贵的教训，悟空。我再也不会把袈裟给别人看了。"

  "这是个好主意，"悟空说，"袈裟是佛祖赐给我们的礼物，我们得小心保管。"

  他说着话突然停了下来，掏出铁棒，"附近有妖精。"

  他望向附近的一个山洞。传来一声低吼。唐僧吓得浑身发抖。一头面目凶恶的怪物从洞里跳了出来，挥舞着一把沉重的钉耙。
