# Journey to the West · Chapter Forty: The Magic Gold

## ①

  Bajie was in the cave when the door opened. Two small demons entered, dragging Wu Jing and the Tang Monk.

> "What happened?" cried Bajie after the demons left.

> "A demon attacked us," said Wu Jing. "I tried to fight him but he was too powerful."

> "I'm worried," said the Tang Monk. "We don't know what happened to Wukong."

## ②

  In another room inside the cave, Golden Horn talked to some smaller demons. They all laughed when Golden Horn told them that Wukong was trapped under three mountains.

> "Let's celebrate!" said one demon.

> "Not yet," said Golden Horn. "I'm afraid some Wukong might escape."

  He handed a gourd to one of the demons.

> "Take this magic gourd to the monkey. Say his name and he will be sucked into the gourd. In an hour, he will turn into liquid."

## ③

  Elsewhere, Wukong pushed as hard as he could. But the mountains were too heavy.

> "How will I ever get out of this?" thought the monkey.

  He was about to push again when the mountains rose into the air. Wukong jumped to his feet and looked around. A spirit stood nearby.

> "Wukong, I am the spirit of these mountains. Please forgive me. I tried to stop that demon from taking my mountains, but he was too powerful. I'm afraid some demons would be able to escape."

  The spirit bowed and then vanished with the mountains.

## ④

> "I'm lucky that spirit arrived," said Wukong. "Now I must find my companions. I wonder where that demon lives."

  He spotted a small demon coming down the road. The demon was carrying a gourd. Wukong ducked behind a rock before the demon saw him.

> "Another demon," thought the monkey. "He probably knows the one who trapped me under the mountains."

  Wukong made himself look like a demon and stepped back onto the road.

## ⑤

> "Hello!" he called. "Where are you going?"

> "My master sent me to capture Sun Wukong," said the demon.

> "Ha ha!" Wukong laughed. "I know that monkey and I don't like him at all. I'll help you capture him."

> "Thank you," said the demon. "But I can catch him myself quite easily."

> "Oh really?" asked Wukong. "How will you catch him?"

  The demon showed Wukong his gourd.

> "This gourd is magical. If it holds a demon or a spirit, I just have to say, 'Sun Wukong!' and he'll be sucked into it. After an hour, he'll turn into liquid."

## ⑥

  "Huh!" Wukong laughed. "I have a better gourd."

  He quickly plucked a hair and turned it into a gourd. He showed the gourd to the demon.

> "It can hold all of heaven."

  The demon looked interested.

> "I wish I had your gourd."

> "Well," said Wukong. "I might be willing to trade gourds with you."

  The demon gave Wukong a suspicious look.

> "How do I know you're telling the truth? Prove that your gourd can hold all of heaven. Then we'll trade."

## ⑦

> "I'll prove it to you," said Wukong.

  The monkey recited a quick spell and left his body standing on the road. He flew fast up to heaven.

> "I must make it look like heaven got sucked into this gourd," he thought. "I'll ask the guard to close the gate and dim the stars. Then it will look like heaven is gone."

  Greetings, Wukong. Said the guard at the Southern Gate of Heaven.

> "I need to pretend that heaven has been sucked into a gourd," said Wukong. "When I say the word, close the gate and dim the stars."

  The guard frowned.

> "I can't do that."

# 🇨🇳 第四十章：金箍棒


## ①

  八戒在山洞里待着，忽然门开了。两个小妖怪拖进来了悟净和唐僧。

> "怎么回事？"妖怪走后八戒叫了起来。

> "一个妖怪来攻击我们，"悟净说，"我试图跟他打，可他太厉害了。"

> "我担心的是，"唐僧说，"我们不知道悟空到底怎么样了。"

## ②

  在山洞的另一间屋子里，金角大王正在跟手下的小妖怪说话。当金角把悟空被三座大山压住的事告诉大家时，他们都哄笑起来。

> "咱们庆祝一下吧！"一个小妖怪说。

> "还不行，"金角大王说，"万一悟空跑了呢。"

  他把一个葫芦递给一个小妖怪。

> "拿着这个宝葫芦去找那只猴子。只要叫他的名字，他就会被吸进葫芦里。过上一个小时，他就会化成水。"

## ③

  另一边，悟空正拼命地推，可是山实在太重了。

> "我怎样才能逃出去呢？"猴子心想。

  他正准备再用力，忽然三座山被搬了起来。悟空一跃而起，四下张望。一位山神站在旁边。

> "悟空，我是这山神。实在抱歉。那个妖怪抢我的山，我拼命想拦住他，可他法力太高强了。那些妖怪恐怕是要作恶了。"

  山神鞠了一躬，然后带着山消失了。

## ④

> "幸亏山神来了，"悟空说，"现在我得去找同伴们。不知道那妖怪住在哪里。"

  他看到一个小妖怪沿着山路走来，手中拿着一个葫芦。悟空赶紧躲到石头后面，不让小妖怪发现。

> "又是妖怪，"猴子想，"他八成认识把我压在下面的那个。"

  悟空变成了一个妖怪，回到路上。

## ⑤

> "你好！"他招呼道，"去哪呀？"

> "我大王派我去捉拿孙悟空，"小妖怪说。

> "哈哈！"悟空大笑，"那只猴子我认识，我可不待见他。不如我帮你去抓他吧。"

> "谢谢你了，"小妖怪说，"不过我自己就能轻松抓住他。"

> "哦？真的吗？"悟空问，"怎么抓？"

  小妖怪把葫芦拿给悟空看。

> "这个葫芦是法宝。只要对着妖怪或神仙叫他的名字，比如'孙悟空！'他就会被吸进去。过上一个小时，他就化成水了。"

## ⑥

  "哼！"悟空笑了，"我有一个更好的葫芦。"

  他拔下一根毫毛，瞬间变成了一个大葫芦。他把葫芦拿给小妖怪看。

> "这个葫芦能把整个天庭都装进去。"

  小妖怪来了兴趣。

> "我真想拥有你的葫芦。"

> "要不，"悟空说，"咱们交换葫芦怎么样？"

  小妖怪一脸怀疑。

> "我怎么知道你说的是真话？证明一下你的葫芦能装下天庭，咱们再交换。"

## ⑦

> "我就证明给你看，"悟空说。

  猴子念了个咒语，留下肉身站在路上，元神飞上了天庭。

> "我得让天庭看起来像是被葫芦吸进去了，"他想，"我让守门的天官关上南天门，再把星星都遮住，看起来就像天庭消失了。"

  他飞到南天门。

> "你好啊，悟空，"南天门的天官说。

> "我需要假装天庭被葫芦吸进去了，"悟空说，"听我号令，把南天门关上，再把星星都熄了。"

  天官皱起了眉头。

> "这可不行。"
