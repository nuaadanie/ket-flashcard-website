# Journey to the West · Chapter Forty-Eight: The Scared Boy

## ①

  Sun Wukong stared at the mountains. A red cloud was appearing over those mountains.

> "He said, there must be a demon ahead. The road leads right into those mountains."

  Said the Tang monk nervously.

> "Sun Wukong, we must be careful."

  He pulled out his iron bar and held it up. Wujing held up his spear, and Bajie held up his rake. The group walked cautiously.

  A red demon sat on a mountain peak, watching the travelers approach.

> "The Tang monk has finally arrived in my mountain," the demon said to himself. "I've heard that even just one bite of him will make me live forever."

  The demon looked at the travelers again.

> "Uh-oh. The monk's companions have their weapons out. They probably saw my red cloud. I'll have to trick them with a disguise."

## ②

  The demon made himself look like a scared little boy. Then he went down to the road, sat alongside it, and waited.

  Soon the travelers approached. The Tang monk saw the boy and got down from his horse.

> "Master, that's a demon," warned Wukong.

  The Tang monk scowled at the monkey.

> "He's just a scared little boy." He turned back to the boy. "What's wrong?"

> "My family was attacked by robbers," said the boy. "Please help me."

> "I know what you are," growled Wukong. "You're a demon. I will not let you eat the Tang monk."

  The boy looked horrified.

> "Wukong, stop scaring the child. You're scaring him!"

> "Ah! Master, you're being a fool again," cried the monkey. "You always fall for a demon's tricks and you never believe me."

> "Be quiet, Wukong," said the Tang monk. "Or I'll recite the Tight Headband Spell."

  Wukong glared angrily at the boy. But he didn't say another word.

## ③

  The Tang monk helped the boy to stand up.

> "Can you walk?" asked the monk.

  The boy shook his head.

> "My leg is injured."

  The Tang monk waved Wukong over.

> "Please carry the boy. And don't be mean to him."

  The group continued west with Wukong carrying the boy on his back. The monkey walked slowly, and soon his companions were far ahead.

> "You tricked the Tang monk," said Wukong to the boy. "But you haven't tricked me. I know you're a demon."

> "I don't know what you're talking about," said the boy.

## ④

  A powerful wind blew up, bending trees and almost knocking over Wukong.

  The monkey laughed.

> "I know that you're making this wind blow. Use all the magic you want. You won't be able to blow me away. I once defeated heaven's entire army."

  The road now ran along the edge of a cliff. Wukong looked down.

> "I should throw you over the edge of this cliff," he said. "Then I'll just tell the Tang monk you ran away."

  The monkey tried to grab the boy from his back. But at that moment the boy disappeared.

  Wukong laughed.

> "He must have gotten scared."

  The wind suddenly grew stronger and dust filled the air. Wukong heard screams and rushed ahead to find his friends.

## ⑤

> "Master!" cried the monkey, trying to see through the dust. "Where are you?"

  When the wind stopped, he saw Bajie and Wujing lying by the road.

> "That was some wind," said the pig, getting up. "It knocked us over," said Wujing.

  Wukong looked frantically around.

> "Where's Master?"

> "Oh no!" cried Bajie. "That boy was a demon. He kidnapped Master!"

  Wukong shook his head.

> "Let's all go home. The journey is over."

# 🇨🇳 第四十八章：受惊的男孩


## ①

  悟空凝视着前方的山峦。一团红云正在山头上空出现。

> "前方肯定有妖怪，"悟空说，"这条路正好通进那些山里。"

  唐僧紧张地说："悟空，我们得小心。"

  悟空掏出铁棒举在手中。悟净举起长枪，八戒举起钉耙。一行人小心翼翼地向前走去。

  一个红色的小妖怪坐在山顶上，注视着渐渐走近的师徒们。

> "唐僧终于来到我的地盘了，"小妖怪自言自语，"听说只要吃上一口他的肉就能长生不老。"

  妖怪又看了看随行的众人。

> "坏了。这和尚的徒弟都拿着兵器。他们八成看到我的红云了。得变个装来骗他们。"

## ②

  妖怪变成了一个受惊的小男孩。然后他沿路坐在路边等着。

  不一会儿，师徒们走近了。唐僧看见小男孩，从马上下来。

> "师父，那是妖怪，"悟空警惕地说。

  唐僧瞪了悟空一眼。

> "他只是个受了惊吓的小娃娃。"他转身对男孩说，"你怎么了？"

> "我家里人遇上强盗了，"小男孩说，"请你们帮帮我。"

> "我知道你是什么，"悟空低声喝道，"你是妖怪休想吃掉唐和尚。"

  小男孩装出一副害怕的样子。

> "悟空，别吓唬孩子。你吓着他了！"唐僧说。

> "哎！师父，您又犯糊涂了，"悟空大叫，"您老是上妖怪的当，就是不信我。"

> "闭嘴，悟空，"唐僧说，"不然我就念紧箍咒。"

  悟空狠狠瞪了小男孩一眼，没再说话。

## ③

  唐僧扶小男孩站了起来。

> "你能走路吗？"唐僧问。

  小男孩摇了摇头。

> "我的腿受伤了。"

  唐僧朝悟空招了招手。

> "你背这孩子吧。别再对他不客气了。"

  一行人继续向西走，悟空背着小男孩。悟空故意走得慢，渐渐地把同伴们远远甩在了后面。

> "你骗了我师父，"悟空对小男孩说，"可你骗不了我。我知道你是妖怪。"

> "我不知道你在说什么，"小男孩说。

## ④

  忽然狂风大作，树木被吹得东倒西歪，悟空也差点被吹倒。

  悟空哈哈大笑。

> "我知道这风是你搞的鬼。你用尽法力也刮不走我。当年我可是大闹过天宫，天兵天将都被我打败了。"

  这时道路沿着一处悬崖延伸。悟空往下看。

> "我干脆把你扔下悬崖，"他说，"就跟师父说你跑了。"

  悟空伸手想从背上把那男孩拽下来。可就在那一刻，男孩消失了。

  悟空笑了。

> "看来他是被吓跑了。"

  忽然风更大了，飞沙走石遮天蔽日。悟空听到呼救声，赶紧跑去找同伴。

## ⑤

> "师父！"悟空在沙尘中大喊，"您在哪儿？"

  风停之后，他看见八戒和悟净倒在路上。

> "好大的风啊，"八戒爬起来说。"把我们刮倒了，"悟净说。

  悟空焦急地四处张望。

> "师父呢？"

> "不好！"八戒惊叫道，"那男孩就是妖怪，他把师父抓走了！"

  悟空摇了摇头。

> "咱们都回家吧。这趟取经之路结束了。"
