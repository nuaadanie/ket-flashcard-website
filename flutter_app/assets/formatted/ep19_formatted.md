# Journey to the West · Chapter Nineteen: An Evil Plan

## ①

  The head priest called for two other priests, who quickly ran into the room.

  "Is everything okay, Master?" one priest asked.

  "I can't give this robe back to the Tang monk," said the head priest.

  "What? You must give it back!" said the other priest. "This robe is a Buddhist treasure. It comes from the Western Paradise."

  "As Buddhists, we must do everything we can to keep it in our monastery," said the head priest.

  "The Tang monk won't let you keep it," said the first priest.

## ②

  The head priest smiled. There was an evil look in his eye. "I have a plan," he said.

  Wukong was awake, meditating on a rooftop, when he heard footsteps. He looked down and saw two priests walking across the courtyard. They were carrying bundles of firewood.

  "It's the middle of the night," thought Wukong. "Why are they starting a fire?"

  The priests stacked the firewood next to the building the Tang monk was sleeping in.

## ③

  "Aha!" thought Wukong. "I'll bet that head priest wants to keep our robe. He's planning to kill us."

  Wukong waited until the priests set the wood on fire. Then he used his magic powers. The priests screamed and ran as the fire shot across the courtyard. One building went up in flames and then another. Soon most of the monastery's buildings were on fire. Walls crumbled and roofs caved in.

  "Run for your life!" cried one priest.

  There was one building, however, that the fire didn't touch. The Tang monk slept peacefully as the rest of the monastery burned down.

## ④

  The next morning, the Tang monk woke up and opened the door. He gasped. Smoke drifted through the air. Burned pieces of wood lay in heaps. The building's walls were black and crumbled.

  "Oh no!" cried the Tang monk. "What happened to the monastery?"

  He turned around. That's when he realized that the building he'd slept in was fine.

  Wukong walked over.

  "Wukong!" cried the monk. "What happened here?"

## ⑤

  "The head priest tried to kill you," said Wukong. "He tried to set the building you were in on fire."

  The Tang monk was confused. "But that building is fine. The fire didn't touch it."

  Wukong laughed. "Ha ha! I used my magic to stop the fire."

  The Tang monk scratched his head. "But the rest of the buildings burned."

  "I used my magic for that too," said Wukong with a smile. "I used a spell that protected only your building and the robe. The spell made the fire spread everywhere else though."

## ⑥

  The Tang monk's eyes filled with tears. He looked angry. "That's horrible, Wukong," he cried. "If you had the power to stop the fire, you should have stopped all of it."

  Wukong shrugged. "The priests here are evil. They deserved what they got."

## ⑦

  Just then they heard a shout. Two of the priests ran toward them.

  "Villains!" the priests cried to Wukong. "How dare you try to burn my master!"

  "I should club you with my iron bar!" said Wukong.

  The two priests fell to their knees and bowed. "Please forgive us," one cried. "We didn't want to harm you or the Tang monk. It was the head priest's idea. He's evil."

  Wukong glared at the two priests for a moment. Then he relaxed. "Stand up. I believe you. Just bring us the robe so we can continue our journey to the west."

  "Wait, we can't," said one priest. He was shaking. "The robe's gone."

# 🇨🇳 第十九章：恶计


## ①

  主僧叫来了另外两个僧人，他们立刻跑进了房间。

  "师父，一切都好吗？"一个僧人问。

  "我不能把袈裟还给唐僧，"主僧说。

  "什么？您一定要还给他！"另一个僧人说，"那袈裟是佛门至宝，来自西方极乐世界。"

  "作为佛门弟子，我们必须想尽办法把它留在寺里，"主僧说。

  "唐僧不会让您留下的，"第一个僧人说。

## ②

  主僧微微一笑，眼中闪过一丝邪念，"我有一个计划，"他说。

  悟空正醒着，在屋顶打坐，忽然听到脚步声。他低头看见两个僧人穿过院子，手里搬着一捆捆柴火。

  "深更半夜，"悟空心想，"他们生火干什么？"

  两个僧人把柴火堆在唐僧睡觉的那间屋子旁边。

## ③

  "好啊！"悟空想，"我敢打赌那个主僧想霸占我们的袈裟。他计划杀我们。"

  悟空等到僧人点着了柴火，然后施展了他的法力。火势蔓延过院子，僧人们尖叫着四散逃跑。一座楼房起了火，接着又是一座。很快寺院里的大部分建筑都燃起了大火。墙壁坍塌，屋顶塌陷。

  "逃命去吧！"一个僧人大喊。

  然而有一幢建筑，大火却丝毫未及。唐僧在安稳地睡着，其余的寺院都烧成了灰烬。

## ④

  第二天一早，唐僧醒来推开门。他倒吸了一口凉气。烟雾在空中弥漫。烧焦的木头堆积成山，建筑的墙壁漆黑一片， crumbling倒塌。

  "坏了！"唐僧大喊，"寺院怎么了？"

  他转过身，这才发现他睡觉的那幢建筑完好无损。

  悟空走了过来。

  "悟空！"唐僧大喊，"这里发生了什么事？"

## ⑤

  "主僧想害死你，"悟空说，"他想放火烧你住的房子。"

  唐僧不解地问："可是这幢房子没事啊，火没有烧到它。"

  悟空哈哈大笑，"我用魔法把火挡住了。"

  唐僧挠了挠头，"可是其他的房子都烧了。"

  "那也是我故意的，"悟空笑着说，"我用了一道咒语，只保护了你住的房间和袈裟。但这道咒也让火势蔓延到了其他地方。"

## ⑥

  唐僧眼中含着眼泪，他生气了，"太过分了，悟空！"他喊道，"你既然有力量灭火，就该把火全部灭掉。"

  悟空耸了耸肩，"这里的僧人都是恶人，罪有应得。"

## ⑦

  就在这时，他们听到一声喊。两个僧人跑了过来。

  "坏蛋！"两个僧人对悟空喊道，"你们竟敢烧死我们的师父！"

  "我该用铁棒揍你们！"悟空说。

  两个僧人扑通跪下，叩头道："请原谅我们！"一个僧人哭道，"我们不想伤害你和唐僧，这是主僧的主意，他是坏人。"

  悟空瞪了两个僧人一会儿，然后放松下来，"起来吧。我相信你们。把袈裟还给我们，我们要继续西行。"

  "不行，我们拿不出来，"一个僧人浑身发抖地说，"袈裟不见了。"
