# Journey to the West · Chapter Forty-Nine: Red Boy

## ①

  "Wukong is right," said Bajie. "We should all go home and forget about reaching the Western Paradise. I'll return to my cave and take a nice long nap." Bajie started to walk away.

> "Are you crazy?" cried Wukong.

  "We can't go home," said Wujing.

## ②

> "I know that," said the monkey. "I wasn't serious about going home. But I'm angry with Master. How many times have I warned him about demons? He never believes me. If he had listened to me earlier, he wouldn't have been kidnapped."

  Wukong sighed. "We need to figure out where the demon took him. I'll summon a local spirit."

  The monkey closed his eyes and muttered some words. A soft breeze blew and then a spirit appeared.

> "Hello, Sun Wukong," said the spirit. "I am the spirit of this mountain."

## ③

> "Our master was kidnapped by a demon," said Wukong. "We think the demon lives near here, but we don't know where."

> "I can tell you," said the spirit. He looked nervous. "Your master was probably kidnapped by Red Boy, a very nasty demon. His cave is near the peak of that mountain." The spirit pointed to a nearby mountain. "You shouldn't go there, though," the spirit continued. "Red Boy is very powerful. His father is the Bull Demon King, one of the most horrible demons ever."

## ④

  A smile spread across Wukong's face and he began to laugh.

> "Ha ha ha ha! That is good news!"

  Everyone looked at the monkey in surprise.

> "Why is that good news?" asked Wujing.

> "The Bull Demon King is an old friend of mine," said Wukong. "Before I caused trouble in Heaven, I was friends with a group of demon kings. The Bull Demon King was one of them. When I tell Red Boy who I am, he'll release the Tang Monk right away."

## ⑤

  The three travelers said goodbye to the spirit and headed up the mountain. They came to a cave near the peak. The door was closed.

> "Open up, Red Boy!" Wukong shouted. "I need to speak with you."

  The door blew open and Red Boy stepped out.

> "Who are you?" demanded Red Boy. "What do you want?"

> "You kidnapped our master, the Tang Monk," said the monkey. "Please return him to us."

## ⑥

  Red Boy snorted.

> "I'm not going to return the Tang Monk to you. I'm going to eat him and live forever. Now go away before I get very angry."

  Wukong laughed.

> "My dear boy, you don't realize who you're talking to. I am Sun Wukong, the Monkey King. Your father and I are old friends."

> "I don't care who you are," said Red Boy. "Now all of you get off my mountain!"

## ⑦

  Wukong pretended to be disappointed.

> "I was hoping we could solve this problem nicely."

  He removed the iron bar from his ear. Red Boy laughed.

> "Do you think I'm scared of your little weapon?"

> "This iron bar is a treasure from Heaven," said Wukong. "It was used to measure the oceans and rivers. It is very powerful and can do whatever I tell it to do." The monkey puffed out his chest. "Return the Tang Monk at once, or I'll destroy your entire mountain!"

## ⑧

> "I also have a powerful weapon," said Red Boy. "I'll show it to you."

  The demon punched himself in the nose. Smoke poured from his nostrils and fire from his mouth. A moment later, flames covered the entire mountainside.

# 🇨🇳 第四十九章：红孩儿


## ①

  "悟空说得对，"八戒说，"我们都该回家，别再去西天了。我要回洞里去睡个长觉。"八戒转身就走。

> "你疯了吗？"悟空喊道。

  "我们不能回家，"悟净说。

## ②

> "我知道，"猴子说，"我可不是真说要回家。可是我对师父很生气。我警告过他多少次有妖怪？他就是不听。要是他早听我的，就不会被妖怪抓走了。"

  悟空叹了口气："我们得查查妖怪把师父带到哪里去了。我来召唤一个当地的土地神。"

  猴子闭上眼睛念了几句咒语。一阵微风吹过，一个神灵出现了。

> "你好，孙悟空，"神灵说，"我是这座山的山神。"

## ③

> "我们的师父被妖怪抓走了，"悟空说，"我们觉得妖怪就住在附近，但不知道具体在哪里。"

> "我可以告诉你，"神灵说。他看上去很紧张，"抓走你师父的很可能是红孩儿，一个非常凶恶的妖怪。他的洞就在这座山的山顶附近。"神灵指着附近的一座山说："不过你们不该去那里，"神灵继续说，"红孩儿法力高强，他的父亲是牛魔王，是最可怕的妖怪之一。"

## ④

  悟空脸上露出笑容，哈哈大笑起来。

> "哈哈哈！这可是好消息！"

  大家惊讶地看着猴子。

> "为什么是好消息？"悟净问。

> "牛魔王是我的老朋友，"悟空说，"我大闹天宫之前，跟一群妖王结成了兄弟。牛魔王就是其中之一。我告诉红孩儿我是谁，他会立刻把唐僧放了的。"

## ⑤

  三位行者告别了神灵，向山上走去。他们来到山顶附近的一个洞前，洞门关着。

> "快开门，红孩儿！"悟空叫道，"我有话要跟你说。"

  洞门猛然打开，红孩儿走了出来。

> "你是谁？"他质问道，"你想干什么？"

> "你抓走了我们的师父唐僧，"猴子说，"请把他交还给我们。"

## ⑥

  红孩儿哼了一声。

> "我不会把唐僧还给你们。我要吃了他，好长生不老。现在快滚，别惹我发火。"

  悟空笑了起来。

> "小伙子，你还不明白自己在跟谁说话。我是孙悟空齐天大圣，你爹跟我可是老交情。"

> "我才不管你是谁，"红孩儿说，"赶紧滚出我的山！"

## ⑦

  悟空装作很失望的样子。

> "我本来还想跟你好好商量呢。"

  他从耳朵里掏出了金箍棒。红孩儿大笑起来。

> "你以为我会怕你那根小棍子？"

> "这根金箍棒可是天上的宝贝，"悟空说，"当年大禹治水用来测量江河湖海的神针。它法力无边，我说什么它就做什么。"猴子挺起胸膛，"立刻把唐僧交出来，不然我把你的整座山都砸个稀巴烂！"

## ⑧

> "我也有一件厉害的兵器，"红孩儿说，"让你见识见识。"

  妖怪朝自己鼻子上捶了一拳，浓烟从鼻孔里冒出来，火焰从嘴里喷出来。不一会儿，整座山坡都被火焰吞没了。
