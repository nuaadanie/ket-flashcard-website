# Journey to the West · Chapter Twelve: The Bet with Buddha

## ①

  Buddha smiled at Wukong.

  "How did you get so powerful?"

  "A sage taught me secret formulas," said Wukong. "I practiced them for many years. I am now equal to the Jade Emperor."

  "The Jade Emperor has been practicing the same formulas for many lifetimes," said Buddha. "How can you think you are equal to him? Go back to your mountain now and behave yourself."

  "I am not leaving until I get to rule heaven," said Wukong.

## ②

  Buddha thought for a minute.

  "Sun Wukong, what powers do you have?"

  "I can change into other animals," said Wukong. "I can soar through the sky on clouds. I can do anything I want."

  "Then let us make a bet," said Buddha. "If you win, you can rule heaven. The Jade Emperor will come to live with me in the Western Paradise. But if you lose, you will be punished severely."

  "What do I have to do?" asked Wukong.

## ③

  "It is simple," said Buddha. He held out his hand. "You just have to jump off my hand."

  Wukong laughed. "That is easy. I can jump across entire oceans."

  Buddha smiled. "Well then, let us see you do it."

  Wukong stepped up onto Buddha's palm. "You are a fool," he said to Buddha. "I am going to jump farther than you could ever imagine. When I return, I will be the ruler of heaven."

## ④

  Wukong leaped and soared through the air. After a long time, he saw five enormous pillars. He landed in front of them.

  "This must be the end of the world," he said to himself. "I will go back now and tell Buddha to take the Jade Emperor away. Heaven is mine."

  Wukong was just about to jump back when he thought of something.

  "There is nobody around. How will I prove that I was here?"

  He tapped his chin. "I will write a note on this pillar. Buddha can come here and check."

## ⑤

  Wukong plucked two hairs and turned them into a brush and a jar of ink. On one of the pillars he wrote: *Sun Wukong was here.*

  "There," said Wukong. "Now I will go and tell Buddha how far I jumped."

  Wukong leaped once more. After a long time he landed on Buddha's palm again.

  "I am back!" said Wukong. "Tell the Jade Emperor to leave."

## ⑥

  Buddha put on a frown. "How can you say you are back? You never left my hand."

  Wukong was confused. "Yes, I did. I reached the end of the world. There were five enormous pillars. I even wrote on one. Come with me and I will show you."

  "There is no need to go anywhere," said Buddha. "Just look down."

  Wukong looked down. Near the bottom of Buddha's middle finger was the note: *Sun Wukong was here.*

## ⑦

  Wukong stamped his foot. "It is a trick! You used some sort of magic."

  "It is not a trick," said Buddha. "You never left my palm."

  "I did! I did! I did! I jumped all the way to—"

  "You lost the bet, Sun Wukong," said Buddha. "And now you will be punished."

  Holding Wukong tightly, Buddha reached down to earth. His hand turned into a mountain, which he placed on top of the monkey. Wukong grunted and pushed with all his strength. The mountain started to rise. Buddha pulled a small sheet of magic paper from his sleeve. He laid the paper on top of the mountain. The mountain crashed back down.

  Buddha turned to the Jade Emperor. "Sun Wukong cannot lift the mountain now," he said. "He will be trapped until his punishment is over."

# 🇨🇳 第十二章：与如来之赌

## ①

  佛祖微笑着看着悟空。

  "你是怎么变得这么厉害的？"

  "一位神仙教了我秘诀，"悟空说，"我练了很多年，现在已经和玉皇大帝一样厉害了。"

  "玉皇大帝同样修行了许多世代，"佛祖说，"你怎么能和他平起平坐呢？回到你的山上去，安分守己吧。"

  "我非要统治天庭不可，"悟空说，"不然我绝不走。"

## ②

  佛祖沉思了一会儿。

  "孙悟空，你有什么本事？"

  "我会七十二变，能变成各种动物，"悟空说，"我能腾云驾雾。我想做什么就做什么。"

  "那我们打个赌吧，"佛祖说，"如果你赢了，你就可以统治天庭，玉皇大帝就跟我到西天极乐世界去住。但如果你输了，就要受到严厉的惩罚。"

  "我要做什么？"悟空问。

## ③

  "很简单，"佛祖说。他伸出一只手，"你只要跳出我的手心就行了。"

  悟空哈哈大笑。"那太容易了，我一个筋斗就能翻过整片大海。"

  佛祖微微一笑。"那好，就让我看看吧。"

  悟空纵身跳上佛祖的手掌。"你可真傻，"他对佛祖说，"我要跳到一个你想都想不到的地方。等我回来的时候，我就是天庭之王了。"

## ④

  悟空纵身一跃，翱翔空中。过了很久，他看到了五根巨大的柱子。他落在柱子前面。

  "这一定就是世界的尽头了，"他自言自语道，"现在回去吧，叫佛祖把玉皇大帝带走。天庭是我的了。"

  悟空正准备跳回去，忽然想到了一件事。

  "周围一个人也没有，我怎么证明自己来过这里呢？"

  他摸了摸下巴。"我在这根柱子上写几个字吧。佛祖可以亲自来看看。"

## ⑤

  悟空拔下两根毫毛，变成一支毛笔和一罐墨水。他在一根柱子上写下：*孙悟空到此一游。*

  "好了，"悟空说，"现在回去告诉佛祖我跳了多远。"

  悟空又是一个筋斗。过了很久，他重新落在佛祖的手掌上。

  "我回来了！"悟空说，"快叫玉皇大帝搬走吧。"

## ⑥

  佛祖皱起眉头。"你怎么说你已经回来了？你从来没有跳出过我的手心。"

  悟空糊涂了。"我明明跳出去了！我到了世界的尽头，那里有五根巨大的柱子，我还在上面写了字呢。你跟我去看看就知道了。"

  "不必去了，"佛祖说，"你低头看看吧。"

  悟空低头一看。佛祖的中指根部，赫然写着一行字：*孙悟空到此一游。*

## ⑦

  悟空跺了跺脚。"这是骗术！你使了什么魔法。"

  "不是骗术，"佛祖说，"你从来没有跳出过我的手掌。"

  "我跳了！我跳了！我明明跳了——"

  "你赌输了，孙悟空，"佛祖说，"现在你要受到惩罚了。"

  佛祖牢牢抓住悟空，向下落到人间。他的手变成一座大山，压在猴子身上。悟空咬紧牙关，使出浑身力气去推。大山开始松动上升。佛祖从袖中抽出一张小小的符纸，贴在大山顶上。大山轰然落下，重新把悟空压住。

  佛祖转身对玉皇大帝说。"孙悟空现在搬不动这座山了，"他说，"他要被压在山下，直到惩罚期满为止。"
