# Journey to the West · Chapter Eighty-Three: The Melon Field

## ①

> "Are you sure you can help?" Wukong asked the Buddha Maitreya. "Yellow Brows is powerful. He captured everyone with a magic cloth. He trapped me in a pair of cymbals."

> "I can defeat Yellow Brows," said Maitreya. "He used to be my student. The cloth and cymbals are precious Buddhist treasures that he stole from me."

> "Sorry, but I destroyed the cymbals," said Wukong.

## ②

  Maitreya smiled.

> "Don't worry about that. We must capture Yellow Brows. I'll tell you my plan."

  Wukong flew up to the monastery.

> "Yellow Brows!" he shouted. "Return my friends, or I'll smash your entire mountain!"

## ③

  The door flew open and Yellow Brows stepped out.

> "How dare you threaten me? Do you know how powerful I am?"

> "You're not powerful," said the monkey. "You're nothing, because you have that cloth. I bet you're too scared to fight me without it."

> "I'm not scared of you," said the demon.

## ④

  He went back inside and came back a moment later. He was holding a heavy wooden club.

  Wukong pulled out his iron bar as the demon rushed toward him. Wham! Their weapons smashed together again and again.

  As he fought the demon, Wukong took small steps backward. Slowly, he moved the fight away from the monastery. Soon they were fighting in the valley at the bottom of the mountain.

## ⑤

  Wukong took another swing at Yellow Brows and then pretended to be scared.

> "You are too powerful!" cried the monkey. "I can't fight you anymore!"

  He turned and ran away.

> "Hey! Get back here, child!" the demon ran after Wukong.

## ⑥

  The monkey ran through some woods and into a melon field. Yellow Brows chased him.

> "Where are you, Wukong?" he shouted.

  Then the demon noticed all the melons. He licked his lips.

  "Juicy," he said to himself.

  A farmer was working nearby. He had a big belly.

## ⑦

> "Hey, farmer," called Yellow Brows. "Give me one of your melons."

  The farmer put down his hoe and smiled. He walked over to the demon.

> "I'll find a nice ripe melon for you."

  The farmer searched the ground. Finally, he held up a ripe melon.

> "Here you go."

## ⑧

  Yellow Brows lifted the melon to take a bite. Suddenly, the melon shrank to the size of a berry. It leaped out of his hand and went right down his throat.

  Gold? The demon fell to his knees.

  "What was wrong with that melon?"

  Inside the demon, Wukong kicked and chopped.

## ⑨

  "Poof! Ho! Poof! Ho!" cried Yellow Brows.

  Then he noticed the farmer was gone. Instead, Maitreya stood over him. Maitreya glared at the demon.

> "Give me my magic cloth."

> "Yes, yes, Master."

  Yellow Brows pulled the cloth from his robe and handed it to Maitreya.

## ⑩

> "You may come out now, Wukong," said Maitreya.

  A tiny Wukong leaped from the demon's nose. Maitreya threw the cloth over Yellow Brows. It wrapped around the demon and became small. Maitreya put the bundle into a pocket.

  Wukong returned to his normal size. He and Maitreya went to the monastery.

## ⑪

  Maitreya recited a spell and the cymbals repaired themselves. He smiled at Wukong.

> "Good luck."

  Then he vanished.

  Wukong untied his companions and the other spirits. The dragon, the turtle and the serpent thanked the monkey and flew away.

## ⑫

> "Thank you, Wukong," said the Tang Monk. "I'm sorry for not believing you when you warned us about this place."

  Wukong laughed.

> "Maybe you'll believe me one of these days, Master."

  The travelers went outside and continued west.

# 🇨🇳 第八十三章：瓜田

## ①

> "你确定能帮上忙？"悟空问弥勒佛，"黄眉很厉害。他用一块魔布把所有人都抓住了，还用铙钹把我困住了。"

> "我能降服黄眉，"弥勒佛说，"他曾经是我的弟子。那块布和铙钹是他从我这里偷走的佛门宝物。"

> "对不起，我把铙钹砸坏了，"悟空说。

## ②

  弥勒佛微笑了。

> "别担心。我们必须抓住黄眉。我把计划告诉你。"

  悟空飞到寺院前。

> "黄眉！"他大叫，"还我朋友，不然我踏平你的山！"

## ③

  大门飞开，黄眉走了出来。

> "你敢威胁我？你知道我有多厉害吗？"

> "你没什么厉害的，"猴子说，"你全靠那块布。我敢说你没了布就不敢跟我打。"

> "我才不怕你，"妖怪说。

## ④

  他回身进屋，不一会儿拿着一根沉重的木棍出来。

  妖怪冲过来，悟空掏出铁棒迎战。当！两件兵器反复碰撞。

  悟空一边打一边慢慢后退。渐渐地，他把妖怪从寺院引到了山谷。不久他们在山脚下打了起来。

## ⑤

  悟空又朝黄眉打了一棒，然后装作害怕。

> "你太厉害了！"猴子叫苦，"我打不过你了！"

  他转身就跑。

> "站住！回来，小子！"妖怪追赶悟空。

## ⑥

  猴子穿过一片树林，跑进了一个瓜田。黄眉紧追不舍。

> "悟空你躲哪里去了？"他大喊。

  这时妖怪注意到了满地的瓜。他舔了舔嘴唇。

  "真甜啊，"他自言自语。

  旁边有个农夫在干活。他有个大肚子。

## ⑦

> "喂，农夫，"黄眉叫道，"给我一个瓜。"

  农夫放下锄头，微微一笑。他走到妖怪面前。

> "我给你找个熟的好瓜。"

  农夫在地里找了一会儿。终于，他举起一个熟透的瓜。

> "给你。"

## ⑧

  黄眉举起瓜正要咬。突然，瓜缩小到豆子那么大。它从他手里跳出来，直接钻进了他的喉咙。

  哎哟！妖怪跪倒在地。

  "这瓜怎么了？"

  在妖怪肚子里，悟空拳打脚踢。

## ⑨

  "哎哟！疼啊！"黄眉大叫。

  然后他发现农夫不见了。取而代之的是弥勒佛站在他面前。弥勒佛怒视着妖怪。

> "把我的魔布交出来。"

> "是，是，师父。"

  黄眉从袍子里掏出布，交给了弥勒佛。

## ⑩

> "悟空，你可以出来了，"弥勒佛说。

  一个小小的悟空从妖怪鼻子里跳了出来。弥勒佛把布往黄眉身上一扔，布裹住了妖怪，变得很小。弥勒佛把包裹放进口袋。

  悟空恢复了正常大小。他和弥勒佛回到了寺院。

## ⑪

  弥勒佛念了一句咒语，铙钹自动修复了。他朝悟空笑了笑。

> "祝你好运。"

  然后他消失了。

  悟空解开了同伴和其他精灵们。金龙、乌龟和蛇精谢过悟空，飞走了。

## ⑫

> "谢谢你，悟空，"唐僧说，"之前你不说这地方有危险，我不该不信你。"

  悟空笑了。

> "也许有一天你会相信我的，师父。"

  一行出了寺院，继续向西前行。
