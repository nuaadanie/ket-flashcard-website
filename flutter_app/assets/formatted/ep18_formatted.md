# Journey to the West · Chapter Eighteen: The Robe

## ①

  Wukong and Guanyin flew over the ocean and back to the wide river. From the sky, Guanyin called down, "Dragon, come out of the river at once. The Tang monk is here."

  The dragon rose from the river and bowed. "Bodhisattva," he said, "I'm sorry. I didn't realize the monk had arrived. I was trying to rest. A nasty monkey who was..."

  The dragon stopped talking when he saw Wukong next to Guanyin.

## ②

  "That's him!"

  "This is Sun Wukong," said Guanyin. "He is traveling with the Tang monk."

  "The monkey didn't tell me that," said the dragon. "I even asked him who he was and he didn't answer."

  "I know," said Guanyin. She shot Wukong a look. "From now on, you must tell people who you are. There are others waiting for you too."

  "Okay," said Wukong.

## ③

  Guanyin looked back at the dragon. "You will carry the Tang monk to the western paradise."

  Sparkling lights surrounded the dragon. When the lights faded, a horse stood where the dragon had been. It looked exactly like the Tang monk's horse.

  After Guanyin left, Wukong led the horse to the Tang monk.

  "You got the horse back!" said the monk.

  "No," said Wukong. "Guanyin was here. She turned the dragon into a horse. He's supposed to come with us."

  The Tang monk fell to his knees and bowed.

## ④

  Wukong laughed. "Stand up, Master. The Bodhisattva is off back on Mount Potalaka by now."

  "Do you need a ride?" called a voice.

  Wukong spun around. A boat had arrived next to the shore.

  "I will take you across the river," said the boatman.

  The Tang monk stepped onto the boat and Wukong followed with the horse. The boatman didn't speak as he rode them across the river.

## ⑤

  On the other side of the river, the monkey led the horse onto the shore. The Tang monk stepped out of the boat. When he turned to thank the boatman, the boat was gone.

  Wukong laughed when he saw how confused the monk looked. "He was the river's spirit," the monkey explained.

  The monk and the monkey continued their journey over hills and across plains.

  "How long will it take to reach the western paradise?" the Tang monk asked one afternoon.

## ⑥

  "A long, long time," said Wukong. "We barely started our journey."

  They walked some more until they reached a Buddhist monastery.

  "Let's stop here for the night," said Wukong. "We're Buddhists. The priests inside will gladly give us some food and a place to sleep."

  Later that evening, the Tang monk and Wukong ate a meal with the priests. When the head priest heard where the Tang monk was going, he was excited.

## ⑦

  "That is quite an honor," said the priest. "How did the Bodhisattva choose you?"

  "She said that only a true believer could make the journey," said the Tang monk. "I tried on a robe and saw that I was a true believer."

  "Do you have the robe with you?" asked the priest.

  "Yes," said the Tang monk. "It's a Buddhist treasure." He reached into his bag and pulled out the robe.

  The priest's eyes sparkled. "May I hold it?"

  The Tang monk handed the robe to the priest. The priest admired it for a moment.

## ⑧

  "May I borrow it just for tonight?" the priest asked.

  "I guess that would be okay," said the Tang monk, hesitating.

  Late that night, the priest was awake, gazing at the robe. "I can't return this robe," he whispered. "I must have it."

# 🇨🇳 第十八章：袈裟失窃


## ①

  悟空和观音飞越海面，回到了那条大河。观音从空中喊道："龙，马上从河里出来。唐僧到了。"

  龙从河中升起，拜道："菩萨，对不起，我不知道僧人已经到了。我本想休息一下。有一只讨厌的猴子他……"

  龙看见观音身旁的悟空，就停住了话头。

## ②

  "就是他！"

  "这是孙悟空，"观音说，"他和唐僧一起旅行。"

  "那猴子没跟我说过啊，"龙说，"我还问他是谁，他也不回答。"

  "我知道，"观音说。她瞪了悟空一眼，"从今以后，你必须告诉别人你是谁。还有别的人也在等着你呢。"

  "好吧，"悟空说。

## ③

  观音又看向龙，"你要驮唐僧去西方极乐世界。"

  闪闪发光的光芒笼罩了龙。光芒散去时，一匹马站在了龙原来的位置。它看起来和唐僧的马一模一样。

  观音走后，悟空牵着马来到了唐僧面前。

  "你把马要回来了！"唐僧说。

  "不是，"悟空说，"观音来了，她把龙变成了一匹马。他要跟我们一起去。"

  唐僧跪下来拜谢。

## ④

  悟空笑道："师父，起来吧。菩萨这会儿已经回到普陀山了。"

  "需要搭船吗？"一个声音喊道。

  悟空转过身来。一艘船已经靠在了岸边。

  "我送你们过河，"船夫说。

  唐僧上了船，悟空牵着马跟了上去。船夫一路沉默，渡他们过了河。

## ⑤

  到了河对岸，悟空牵着马上岸。唐僧从船上下来，转身想要感谢船夫，船却不见了。

  悟空见唐僧一脸茫然，不禁笑了，"他是河神，"悟空解释道。

  师徒二人继续赶路，翻山越岭。

  一天下午，唐僧问："我们要走多久才能到达西方极乐世界？"

## ⑥

  "很久很久，"悟空说，"我们的旅途才刚刚开始。"

  他们又走了一阵，来到了一座佛寺。

  "我们在这里住一晚吧，"悟空说，"我们是佛门弟子。里面的师父们一定会给我们食物和住宿的地方。"

  当天傍晚，唐僧和悟空和寺里的僧人们一起吃饭。主僧听说唐僧要去的地方，非常高兴。

## ⑦

  "真是莫大的荣誉，"主僧说，"菩萨是怎么选中你的？"

  "她说只有真正的信徒才能完成这趟旅途，"唐僧说，"我试穿了一件袈裟，证明了自己是一个真正的信徒。"

  "袈裟带在身上吗？"主僧问。

  "带了，"唐僧说，"它可是佛门至宝。"他从包里取出了袈裟。

  主僧两眼放光，"我能摸一下吗？"

  唐僧把袈裟递给了他。主僧欣赏了一会儿。

## ⑧

  "能借给我看一个晚上吗？"主僧问。

  "嗯，应该可以吧，"唐僧犹豫了一下说。

  深夜，主僧还没有睡，他凝视着那件袈裟，"我不能把这件袈裟还回去，"他低声说，"我一定要得到它。"
