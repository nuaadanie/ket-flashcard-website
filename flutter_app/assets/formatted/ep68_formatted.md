# Journey to the West · Chapter Sixty-Eight: The Woman in the Crowd

## ①

  Wukong stood at the cave door, shocked.

> "I don't believe it. One-Horned Buffalo stole our weapons again!"

> "What will we do now?" asked the Fire God.

> "This demon's powers are stronger than any I've seen before," said Wukong. "I know one person who can definitely defeat him. Buddha. I'll be back soon."

## ②

  The monkey leaped onto a cloud and soared toward the west. He arrived at the Thunder Clap Monastery. A guard at the gate stopped him.

> "I need to ask Buddha for help," Wukong told the guard. "It's about the Tang Monk's journey."

  The guard waved the monkey through.

## ③

  Wukong entered the monastery and bowed before Buddha.

> "Blessed Buddha," said the monkey. "I need..."

  Buddha held up a hand.

> "I know why you're here, Wukong. I also know who the demon is. You must go to Laozi for help."

> "Oh, okay," said the monkey, a bit surprised. "Thank you."

## ④

  Wukong flew toward Heaven.

> "Laozi doesn't like me much," thought the monkey. "He won't be happy to see me."

  When Wukong arrived at Laozi's house, the sage frowned.

> "What do you want, Wukong?" asked Laozi.

> "Buddha told me to come here," said Wukong. "The Tang Monk was captured by a demon called One-Horned Buffalo."

## ⑤

> "Oh dear," said Laozi.

> "Do you know him?" asked the monkey.

  Laozi nodded.

> "He's my green buffalo. I didn't realize he escaped."

> "Well, he did," said Wukong, getting annoyed. "And he's planning to eat the Tang Monk. Your buffalo also has a metal ring. Every time we try to fight him, the ring sucks in our weapons."

## ⑥

  Laozi held up his hands.

> "Okay, okay. I can capture the buffalo. Don't worry."

  The sage went into another room. When he came back, he was holding a fan.

> "Let's go."

## ⑦

  Wukong and Laozi returned to the mountain.

> "Buffalo!" shouted Laozi. "Get out here right now."

  The door flew open, and One-Horned Buffalo stepped outside. His face fell when he saw Laozi.

  Laozi raised the fan and brought it down fast. A fierce wind nearly knocked over One-Horned Buffalo.

## ⑧

  The demon pulled off the metal ring and flung it at Laozi. The sage caught it and brought the fan down once more.

  The wind knocked over the demon, and he turned into a large green buffalo. The buffalo stood up and snorted.

  Laozi tucked the fan and the ring into his robe. Then he climbed onto the buffalo and rode up into the sky.

## ⑨

  Wukong went into the cave, and the warrior and two gods followed. They soon found their weapons.

> "The gods and I will return to Heaven now," said the warrior. "Good luck with your journey, Wukong."

> "Thank you," said the monkey.

## ⑩

  Wukong found his companions deep in the cave.

> "What took you so long?" cried Bajie.

> "I was busy fighting demons," said Wukong, untying the Tang Monk.

> "Thank you, Wukong," said the monk.

> "You're welcome," said the monkey. "But next time I tell you to stay away from a place, please listen."

## ⑪

  A few weeks later, the travelers arrived at a busy city. The streets were filled with people selling vegetables, spices, and other goods. An old woman was stirring a large pot of soup. Other food sizzled over fires.

  Bajie breathed deep.

> "Ah! That smells wonderful!"

## ⑫

  Suddenly a young woman rushed out of the crowd.

> "The Tang Monk!" she cried.

  She threw her arms around the monk. A fierce wind swirled, and a second later, they were both gone.

# 🇨🇳 第六十八章：金兜山


## ①

  悟空站在洞门口，震惊不已。

> "真不敢相信，独角牛魔王又偷走了我们的兵器！"

> "我们现在怎么办？"火神问道。

> "这个妖怪的法力比我见过的任何妖怪都要强，"悟空说，"我知道有一个人一定能打败他——佛祖。我很快就回来。"

## ②

  猴子翻上云头，向西飞去。他赶到了雷音寺。门口的守卫拦住了他。

> "我需要请佛祖帮忙，"悟空对守卫说，"是关于唐僧取经的事。"

  守卫挥了挥手，放猴子进去了。

## ③

  悟空走进寺内，向佛祖行礼。

> "佛祖在上，"猴子说，"我需要……"

  佛祖举起一只手。

> "悟空，我知道你为什么来。我也知道那个妖怪是谁。你必须去找老君求助。"

> "哦，好的，"猴子有些吃惊，"谢谢佛祖。"

## ④

  悟空朝天上飞去。

> "老君本来就不喜欢我，"猴子想，"他看到我肯定不会高兴。"

  悟空到了老君的住处，老者皱起了眉头。

> "悟空，你有什么事？"老君问。

> "佛祖让我来找您的，"悟空说，"唐僧被一个叫独角牛魔王的妖怪抓走了。"

## ⑤

> "哎呀，"老君叹了口气。

> "您认识他吗？"猴子问。

  老君点了点头。

> "他是我的青牛精，没想到他逃跑了。"

> "他确实跑了，"悟空有些恼火，"而且他打算吃掉唐僧。您的青牛精手上还有一个金属圈，每次我们跟他打，那个圈就把我们的兵器吸走。"

## ⑥

  老君举起双手。

> "好了好了，我去收服那头牛。别担心。"

  老者走进另一个房间，出来的时候手里拿着一把扇子。

> "我们走吧。"

## ⑦

  悟空和老君回到了那座山。

> "孽畜！"老君大喊道，"快给我滚出来。"

  门猛地开了，独角牛魔王走了出来。当他看到老君时，脸色大变。

  老君举起扇子用力一扇，一阵狂风险些把独角牛魔王吹翻。

## ⑧

  妖怪扯下金属圈朝老君扔去。老者接住圈子后又扇了一扇子。

  狂风把妖怪吹倒在地，他现出了原形——一头巨大的青牛。青牛站起来，打了个响鼻。

  老君把扇子和金属圈收进袍子里，骑上青牛飞天而去。

## ⑨

  悟空走进洞里，天将和两位天神跟在后面。他们很快就找到了自己的兵器。

> "我和天神们现在就回天庭了，"天将说，"悟空，祝你取经一路顺利。"

> "谢谢你，"猴子说。

## ⑩

  悟空在洞深处找到了他的同伴们。

> "你怎么这么久才来？"八戒叫道。

> "我一直在收拾妖怪呢，"悟空一边给唐僧松绑一边说。

> "谢谢你，悟空，"唐僧说。

> "不客气，"猴子说，"不过下次我让你们离某个地方远一点的时候，请一定听我的。"

## ⑪

  几个星期后，一行人到了一座热闹的城市。街上挤满了卖蔬菜、香料和各种货物的人。一位老太太正在搅动一大锅汤。其他食物也在火上滋滋作响。

  八戒深吸了一口气。

> "啊，真香啊！"

## ⑫

  突然一个年轻女子从人群中冲了出来。

> "唐僧！"她哭喊道。

  她一把抱住了唐僧。一阵狂风卷起，眨眼之间，他们两人都不见了踪影。
