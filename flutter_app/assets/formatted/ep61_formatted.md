# Journey to the West · Chapter Sixty-One: The Demon Under the Ice

## ①

  Wukong leaped into the air. His three companions and the horse fell into the water with a splash. Bajie climbed back out, coughing. Wujing came up next, pulling the horse behind him.

> "Where's the Tang Monk?" cried Wukong.

  Below the ice, the Tang Monk struggled as the demon carried him deep underwater. The demon laughed.

> "You all fell for my trick. I froze the river and waited for you to cross it. Once you were above me, I cracked the ice open. As soon as his friends leave, I will eat him and live forever."

## ②

  They arrived at a palace. A sign over the door read: *Residence of the River Turtle*.

  The demon went inside and locked the monk in a stone box.

  Up on the ice, Wukong paced back and forth.

> "The ice didn't crack by accident," said the monkey. "I'm sure it was the demon. The demon probably caused this strange cold weather too. He made us freeze! I can't believe we fell for his trick. We must save the Tang Monk."

## ③

  Wukong stopped pacing and looked at his companions.

> "My powers aren't very strong underwater. You two must go down there and entice the demon to come back up."

  Wu Jing and Bajie nodded and dived into the river. They soon spotted a palace. Wujing read the sign over the door.

> "Residence of the River Turtle. I wonder if the demon is in here."

  Bajie knocked on the door. *Whop! Whop!* He shouted.

## ④

  The door opened and the demon stepped out. He was holding a large hammer.

> "What do you want?" he asked.

> "Did you kidnap our master?" asked Wujing.

> "Yes," said the demon. "I plan to eat him. Now go away."

  The demon tried to close the door, but Wujing stopped it with his spear. The demon moved toward him. Bajie's rake came crashing down.

  *Cling!*

  The demon blocked the rake with his hammer. The three fighters clashed.

## ⑤

  After a few minutes, Bajie pretended to be scared. He swam upward and Wujing soon followed.

> "Go after them!" called the demon, swimming toward the surface.

  The demon jumped out of the water and onto the ice. Wukong was waiting for him. The monkey swung his iron bar. The demon dodged. The bar hit the ice. The demon swung his hammer.

  *Bam!* More cracks formed in the ice. *Cling! Boom! Clash!*

  The spirit fought on the broken ice. The demon hit Bajie's legs.

> "Ow!" cried the pig.

  He slid across the ice and slammed into Wujing. The pig slipped and fell.

  The demon laughed and dived back under the ice.

## ⑥

  Bajie and Wujing stood back up.

> "The demon escaped," said the pig.

  "We must stop him before he eats the Tang Monk," said Wukong. "Go back down there and try to lead him up here again."

  Wujing and Bajie dived into the river. When they reached the palace, they found a huge pile of rocks blocking the door. Bajie struck the rocks with his rake. The rake bounced off. The rocks didn't move at all. Wujing stabbed at the rocks with his spear. Again, the rocks didn't move.

> "What kind of rocks are these?" cried the pig.

> "Maybe they're held in place with magic," said Wujing.

## ⑦

  They returned to the surface of the river and told Wukong about the rocks.

  The monkey sighed. "We need Guanyin's help."

# 🇨🇳 第六十一章：冰下妖怪


## ①

  悟空腾空而起。他的三个同伴和马匹扑通一声掉进了水里。八戒咳嗽着爬了出来。悟净紧随其后，拖着马。

> "唐僧呢？"悟空叫道。

  冰层下面，唐僧拼命挣扎，而妖怪正把他往水底深处拖。妖怪大笑起来。

> "你们都中了我的计。我冻住了河流，等你们走在上面，然后弄破冰面。等你们的朋友们一走，我就吃了他，永远不死。"

## ②

  他们来到了一座宫殿。门上挂着一块牌子："河鼋之宅"。

  妖怪走进宫里，把唐僧锁在了一个石匣里。

  冰面上，悟空来回踱步。

> "冰面裂开不是意外，"悟空说。"肯定是妖怪干的。这场怪寒大概也是他搞的。他让我们挨冻！真不敢相信我们上了他的当。我们必须去救唐僧。"

## ③

  悟空停下脚步，看着同伴们。

> "我的法力在水下不太强。你们俩得下去把那妖怪引上来。"

  悟净和八戒点了点头，潜入了河里。很快他们看到了一座宫殿。悟净念了念门上的牌子。

> "河鼋之宅。不知道妖怪在不在里面。"

  八戒敲了敲门。*咚咚咚！*他喊着。

## ④

  门开了，妖怪走了出来，手里拿着一把大锤子。

> "你们想干什么？"他问。

> "是不是你绑架了我们师父？"悟净问。

> "是，"妖怪说。"我打算吃他。现在快滚。"

  妖怪想关门，但悟净用矛挡住了门。妖怪向他逼近。八戒的钉耙猛砸下来。

  *叮！*

  妖怪用锤子挡住了钉耙。三个战士打作一团。

## ⑤

  打了一会儿，八戒假装害怕了，向水面游去，悟净也跟着游了上去。

> "追他们！"妖怪喊着，向水面游去。

  妖怪跃出水面，跳上了冰面。悟空正等着他。悟空抡起金箍棒。妖怪躲开了，棒子砸在冰上。妖怪挥起锤子。

  *啪！*冰面又裂开了几道口子。*叮！轰！哐！*

  妖怪在碎冰上厮杀，踢了八戒的腿一下。

> "哎哟！"八戒叫了起来。

  八戒在冰上滑了一大截，撞到了悟净。八戒摔倒了。

  妖怪大笑着跳回了水下。

## ⑥

  八戒和悟净重新站了起来。

> "妖怪跑了，"八戒说。

  "我们必须阻止他，不然他就要吃了唐僧，"悟空说。"再下去一趟，试着把他引上来。"

  悟净和八戒再次潜入河里。他们到宫殿前，发现一大堆石头堵住了门。八戒用钉耙猛砸石头，钉耙弹了回来，石头纹丝不动。悟净用矛猛刺石头，还是动不了。

> "这是什么石头？"八戒叫道。

> "大概是用法术固定的，"悟净说。

## ⑦

  他们回到水面，把石头的事告诉了悟空。

  悟空叹了口气。"我们需要观音来帮忙了。"
