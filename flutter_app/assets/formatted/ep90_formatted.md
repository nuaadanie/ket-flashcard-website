# Journey to the West · Chapter Ninety: The Lion Demon

## ①

  The elephant pointed at Wukong.

> "He's got a demon -- he's a monkey!"

> "He's not just a monkey," said the raptor. "He's a mou-kin, Jie'er."

  The lion pouted. Wukong shot out of the cave as fast as he could. He didn't stop running until he was far from the cave.

## ②

> "Woo!" the monkey said to himself. "That was close. I guess when I laughed, I broke my concentration. That must have made my disguise vanish."

  Wukong returned to his companions.

## ③

> "Well," said Wukong. "Is there an army up ahead? I scared away the entire army," Wukong boasted. "But there are still three powerful demons on the mountain. They planned to capture Master, and, well, eat him."

> "Oh!" cried the Tang monk, nearly falling over.

## ④

  Wukong caught him.

> "Don't worry, Master," said Wukong. "We won't let those demons harm you. But we do have to destroy them before we cross the mountain."

  The Tang monk nodded, wringing his hands nervously.

## ⑤

  Wukong turned to Bajie and Wujing.

> "Someone needs to help me get rid of the demons."

> "I'll go with you," said Bajie.

> "I'll stay here and look after the Tang monk," said Wujing.

## ⑥

  Wukong and Bajie went to the cave entrance. The monkey pulled out his iron bar. Bajie raised his rake.

> "I'll do all the talking," said Wukong.

> "What are you going to say?" asked Bajie.

> "I'll just ask them nicely to leave," said Wukong.

  He turned to the cave entrance.

## ⑦

> "Demons!" he shouted. "Leave this mountain now, or we'll knock it down and crush all three of you!"

  For a moment everything was quiet. Then the lion stepped out of the cave. He towered over the monkey and the pig.

> "Why are you out here yelling?" asked the lion calmly.

## ⑧

> "You with the elephant and the raptor must leave this mountain at once," said Wukong.

> "Why must we leave?" asked the lion. "We're not bothering you."

> "Villain!" shouted the monkey. "I know you're planning to capture the Tang monk!"

## ⑨

> "We haven't touched the Tang monk," said the lion. "But you, Sun Wukong, you scared away our entire army. You're the villain here."

> "I'm not a villain!" shouted Wukong.

  He raised his iron bar and charged toward the lion.

## ⑩

  The lion opened his mouth wide, revealing sharp fangs. Wukong swung his iron bar. The lion's mouth descended.

  Bajie's face went white with horror.

> "Yikes!" he cried. "That lion just swallowed Wukong!"

  The pig ran as fast as he could. He didn't stop running until he reached Wujing and the Tang monk.

## ⑪

> "What happened?" asked Wujing.

> "Wukong's gone. The journey is over," said the pig between breaths. "Wukong got ate!"

> "What?" cried the Tang monk. Bajie shook his head.

> "We'd all just go home."

## ⑫

  Meanwhile, the lion went back into the cave. His chest was puffed out and he wore a big smile.

> "What happened out there?" asked the elephant. "Did you scare away Sun Wukong?"

> "Not exactly," said the lion.

> "Where? Where is he?" asked the raptor.

## ⑬

  The lion patted his belly.

> "He's in here."

> "You ate him?" asked the elephant.

> "I did," said the lion.

  The raptor shook his head.

> "That was a bad idea."

## ⑭

> "Why?" asked the lion.

> "Sun Wukong is a manhole," said the raptor. "He's probably still alive inside you."

> "Yes, I am still alive!" Wukong suddenly shouted. "It's nice and warm in here!"

# 🇨🇳 第九十章：狮妖

## ①

  大象指着悟空。

> "他是个妖怪，是一只猴子！"

> "他可不只是一只猴子，"猛禽精说，"他是一只神猴啊。"

  狮子撅着嘴。悟空以最快的速度冲出洞穴。他一直跑了很远才停下来。

## ②

> "哇！"猴子自言自语道，"好险。我猜我一笑就破了法力，所以伪装消失了。"

  悟空回到了同伴们身边。

## ③

> "怎么样了？"悟空问，"前面还有军队吗？我把整个大军都吓跑了，"悟空吹嘘道，"但是山上还有三个厉害的妖怪。他们打算把师父抓了吃掉。"

> "哦！"唐僧大叫一声，差点摔倒。

## ④

  悟空扶住了他。

> "别担心，师父，"悟空说，"我们不会让那些妖怪伤害您。但在过山之前，我们得把他们除掉。"

  唐僧紧张地搓着手点了点头。

## ⑤

  悟空转向八戒和悟净。

> "得有人帮我对付那些妖怪。"

> "我跟你去，"八戒说。

> "我留下来照顾师父，"悟净说。

## ⑥

  悟空和八戒来到洞口。猴子掏出金箍棒，八戒举起钉耙。

> "由我来说话，"悟空说。

> "你打算说什么？"八戒问。

> "我就客气地请他们离开，"悟空说。

  他转身朝向洞口。

## ⑦

> "妖怪们！"他大喊，"马上离开这座山，不然我们就把它推倒，把你们三个都压死！"

  周围安静了一会儿。然后狮子走出了洞穴。他居高临下地看着猴子和猪。

> "你为什么在外面大喊大叫？"狮子平静地问。

## ⑧

> "你和你那大象、猛禽兄弟必须立刻离开这座山，"悟空说。

> "我们为什么要离开？"狮子问，"我们并没招惹你们。"

> "恶贼！"猴子大喊，"我知道你们打算抓唐僧！"

## ⑨

> "我们还没动唐僧，"狮子说，"但你孙悟空，你把我们整支大军都吓跑了，你才是恶贼。"

> "我不是恶贼！"悟空大喊。

  他举起金箍棒，朝狮子冲去。

## ⑩

  狮子张开大嘴，露出锋利的獠牙。悟空挥动金箍棒。狮子的嘴合了下来。

  八戒吓得脸色发白。

> "哎呀！"他叫了起来，"那只狮子把悟空吞了！"

  猪撒腿就跑，一直跑到了悟净和唐僧身边。

## ⑪

> "出什么事了？"悟净问。

> "悟空没了，取经之路到此为止了，"上气不接下气的八戒说，"悟空被吃了！"

> "什么？"唐僧大叫。八戒摇了摇头。

> "我们都回家算了。"

## ⑫

  与此同时，狮子回到了洞里。他挺着胸，脸上挂着得意的笑容。

> "外面到底发生了什么？"大象问，"你把孙悟空吓跑了吗？"

> "不完全是，"狮子说。

> "在哪？他在哪？"猛禽精问。

## ⑬

  狮子拍了拍肚子。

> "他在裡面呢。"

> "你把他吃了？"大象问。

> "没错，"狮子说。

  猛禽精摇了摇头。

> "这不是个好主意。"

## ⑭

> "为什么？"狮子问。

> "孙悟空浑身是神通的，"猛禽精说，"他多半还在你肚子里活着。"

> "没错，我还活着呢！"悟空突然大叫道，"里面暖洋洋的！"
