# Journey to the West · Chapter Fifty-One: Guanyin Becomes Angry

## ①

  Bajie didn't know Guanyin was really Red Boy in disguise.

> "The Tang Monk is in trouble," said the pig to the fake Guanyin. "A demon named Red Boy is planning to eat him. Can you help us?"

> "Of course," said the fake Guanyin. "I know Red Boy. He's very nice. I doubt he'll harm the Tang Monk. Let's go to Red Boy's cave and talk to him."

## ②

  Bajie and the fake Guanyin flew to Red Boy's cave. Bajie went in first. Red Boy returned to his normal appearance and then sneaked up behind Bajie. He threw a sack over the pig.

> "Hey, what's going on?" shouted Bajie.

> "You fool!" The demon laughed. "I'm Red Boy, and now you're trapped."

  The demon locked Bajie in a room.

## ③

> "Why isn't Bajie back yet?" asked Wujing.

  Wukong groaned.

> "He probably stopped to take a nap. That pig is so lazy."

  Wujing nodded.

> "Yes, he's very lazy. What should we do now?"

> "I'm going to sneak into Red Boy's cave," said Wukong. "I want to make sure the Tang Monk is still alive. Then I'll go to Guanyin myself."

## ④

  The monkey turned himself into a bee and flew up the mountain. He slipped through the cave door and flew down a long tunnel. Soon he heard the Tang Monk crying.

  *Master is scared,* thought Wukong. *But at least he's still alive.*

  The monkey went further and heard a shout. It was Bajie.

> "You filthy demon! How dare you make yourself look like Guanyin and trick me!"

  *Bajie didn't take a nap after all,* thought Wukong. *I must go to the real Guanyin right away.*

## ⑤

  The monkey flew out of the cave and told Wujing what he just learned. Then he jumped onto a cloud and headed south. He found Guanyin in her pagoda.

> "Wukong, why are you here?" asked Guanyin.

> "We have a problem, Bodhisattva," said the monkey. "The Tang Monk was caught by a demon named Red Boy. The demon breathes magic fire that water can't put out. Bajie was on his way here to ask for your help, but the demon made himself look like you and tricked the pig."

## ⑥

  Guanyin's face burned with anger.

> "How dare that demon make himself look like me!"

  The Bodhisattva grabbed a vase and stormed outside. She threw the vase down into the ocean, where it sank beneath the waves.

  Wukong gasped.

> "That vase was a Buddhist treasure. Why did you throw it away?"

  Guanyin didn't answer. A minute later, the water rumbled. The vase appeared on the back of a giant turtle. The turtle came onto the shore and bowed his head.

## ⑦

> "Go down and get the vase," said Guanyin.

  The monkey ran down the mountain and tried to pick up the vase. Wukong gasped and used all of his strength. But no matter how hard he tried, he couldn't lift the vase at all.

> "Bodhisattva!" he called. "It's just a small vase, but I can't lift it."

## ⑧

  Guanyin came down the mountain.

> "That vase just traveled around the world through all the oceans and rivers. It now holds an ocean's worth of water. Magic water. The water will put out Red Boy's fire."

  Guanyin picked up the vase with one hand. She held up her other hand and several swords appeared. She waved them and they turned into a large lotus seat.

> "I have what I need now," said Guanyin.

# 🇨🇳 第五十一章：观音的愤怒


## ①

  八戒不知道观音竟然是红孩儿假扮的。

> "唐僧有难了，"八戒对假观音说，"一个叫红孩儿的妖怪要吃他。你能帮帮我们吗？"

> "当然可以，"假观音说，"我认识红孩儿，他很善良，我不相信他会伤害唐僧。我们去他的洞里跟他谈谈吧。"

## ②

  八戒和假观音飞到了红孩儿的洞前。八戒先进了洞。红孩儿恢复了原形，悄悄从八戒身后靠近，把一个口袋套在了猪头上。

> "哎，怎么回事？"八戒大喊道。

> "你这个蠢货！"妖怪大笑起来，"我是红孩儿，你现在跑不了啦！"

  妖怪把八戒关进了一个房间。

## ③

> "八戒怎么还不回来？"悟净问。

  悟空哼了一声。

> "他准是半路停下睡觉去了。这头猪太懒了。"

  悟净点了点头。

> "是啊，他确实很懒。现在怎么办呢？"

> "我要偷偷潜入红孩儿的洞里，"悟空说，"我要确认师父还活着，然后我自己去找观音。"

## ④

  猴子变成了一只蜜蜂飞上山，从洞门缝隙钻了进去，沿着长长的通道飞去。不一会儿他就听到了唐僧的哭声。

  *师父吓坏了，*悟空想，*不过还好他还活着。*

  猴子继续往里飞，听到了一声喊叫，是八戒。

> "你这个脏妖怪！竟敢变成观音来骗我！"

  *八戒并没有偷懒睡觉，*悟空想，*我必须马上赶去找真正的观音。*

## ⑤

  猴子飞出洞外，把情况告诉了悟净，然后跳到一朵云上向南飞去。他在宝塔里找到了观音菩萨。

> "悟空，你怎么来了？"观音问。

> "菩萨，我们遇到麻烦了，"猴子说，"唐僧被一个叫红孩儿的妖怪抓了，那妖怪会喷三昧真火，水也灭不了。八戒本来要去请菩萨帮忙，结果被那妖怪变成菩萨给骗了。"

## ⑥

  观音气得脸色通红。

> "那妖怪竟敢假扮我！"

  菩萨抓起一只净瓶冲出外面，把瓶子扔进了大海，沉入波涛之中。

  悟空大吃一惊。

> "那净瓶可是佛门宝贝啊，菩萨为什么把它扔了？"

  观音没有回答。过了一会儿，水面翻涌起来，瓶子驮在一只大龟背上出现了。大龟爬上岸，低下头。

## ⑦

> "下去把瓶子拿上来，"观音说。

  猴子跑下山去试着捡起净瓶。悟空使出全力，不论怎么使劲，瓶子纹丝不动。

> "菩萨！"他喊道，"就这么一个小瓶子，我怎么拿不起来？"

## ⑧

  观音下了山。

> "那瓶子周游了全世界的海洋和江河，现在装了整整一海洋的水，那是神奇的甘露，能灭掉红孩儿的三昧真火。"

  观音一手拿起净瓶，另一只手一伸，几把宝剑出现。她一挥，宝剑变成了一座巨大的莲花台。

> "我需要的一切都已准备好了，"观音说。
