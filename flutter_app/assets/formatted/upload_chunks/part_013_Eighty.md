
# Journey to the West · Chapter Eighty: The Small Thunder-Clap Monastery

## ①

  One afternoon, Wukong and his companions were resting by a stream. They were eating peaches and berries.

> "We must be getting close to the Western Paradise," said the Tang Monk. "We've been traveling for years."

> "I feel like we've been traveling for thousands of years," said Bajie.

## ②

> "We still have a long way to go," said Wukong. "But don't worry. Eventually we'll arrive in the Western Paradise and climb up the Spirit Mountain."

  Buddha's home, the Thunderclap Monastery, he sits halfway up the mountain.

  The Tang Monk took a bite of his peach.

> "The Thunderclap Monastery must be wonderful to see."

## ③

> "The Monastery is spectacular," said the monkey. "Golden light shines from it."

  Bajie moaned.

> "So we're going to have to climb a mountain at the end of this journey? Just thinking about that makes me tired."

  Wujing stroked his chin.

> "I really hope the journey ends soon."

## ④

  The pig nodded.

> "You're tired of traveling, too?"

> "No," said Wujing with a serious look. "I'm just tired of hearing you complain."

  Everyone was quiet for a moment. Then Wujing's face broke into a smile, and everybody laughed. Even Bajie.

## ⑤

> "Very funny, Wujing," said Bajie.

  The Tang Monk stood up.

> "We should go. I'd like to find a village or a monastery before dark."

  A couple of hours later, they were traveling up a mountain road. The road curved around a bend. Ahead, they could see a small monastery.

## ⑥

  The Tang Monk gasped.

> "Is that the Thunderclap Monastery?"

  "Look at that," said Bajie. "Golden light is shining from it."

> "It's all along the mountain," Wujing added.

  Wukong laughed.

## ⑦

> "It's not the Thunderclap Monastery. We're not even in the Western Paradise yet."

  The monk appeared puzzled for a moment, and then frowned.

> "We must be careful. I see dark clouds over the monastery."

  The travelers continued walking along the road. Soon they were standing in front of the monastery. A sign over the door read: "Small Thunder-Clap Monastery."

## ⑧

> "Wow!" cried the Tang Monk. "This monastery must belong to Buddha."

  Wukong shook his head.

> "It doesn't belong to Buddha. Let's keep walking."

> "Hold on," said Bajie. "Golden light is shining from this monastery. It must be an important place."

## ⑨

> "It's not," said Wukong. "We should really—"

> "We're going in," the Tang Monk said. "This is a Buddhist monastery. We should visit."

  The monk opened the monastery's door and entered. Bajie and Wujing followed. Wukong sighed and went in after them.

## ⑩

  This place was definitely not what it seemed. The monkey pulled out his iron bar.

  The group entered the building's main hall. A large golden statue of Buddha sat before them.

> "Do you see that statue, Wukong?" said the Tang Monk. "This is a real Buddhist monastery."

## ⑪

  Suddenly a voice boomed.

> "Who dares to enter without bowing to Buddha?"

  The Tang Monk trembled with fear. Right away, he fell to his knees and bowed. Bajie and Wujing bowed. Wukong continued to stand, peering at the statue. It looked very suspicious.

## ⑫

> "Why don't you bow?" the voice asked.

  Wukong whispered to the Tang Monk.

> "Show some respect."

> "I will bow to—" shouted Wukong. "I can see that you're a fake!"

## ⑬

  Clang! Before the monkey could finish yelling, a large pair of cymbals fell from above. They slammed together, sealing Wukong inside.

> "Wukong!" cried the Tang Monk.

  Light flashed, and the statue turned into a demon with yellow eyebrows. The demon stood up and smiled.

  The Tang Monk!

# 🇨🇳 第八十章：小雷音寺

## ①

  一天下午，悟空和同伴们在小溪边休息。他们在吃桃子和浆果。

> "我们一定快到西天了，"唐僧说，"我们已经走了很多年了。"

> "我感觉我们走了好几千年了，"八戒说。

## ②

> "还有很长的路要走呢，"悟空说，"不过别担心。我们终究会到达西天，爬上灵山。"

  佛祖的居所——雷音寺，就在山的半山腰。

  唐僧咬了一口桃子。

> "雷音寺一定很壮观吧。"

## ③

> "那座寺院非常壮观，"猴子说，"金光闪闪。"

  八戒呻吟道。

> "所以到了旅途最后我们还要爬山？光是想想就累了。"

  悟净摸着下巴。

> "我真希望旅途早点结束。"

## ④

  猪八戒点了点头。

> "你也厌倦了赶路？"

> "不，"悟净一脸严肃地说，"我只是厌倦了听你抱怨。"

  大家沉默了一会儿。接着悟净的脸上露出了笑容，大家都笑了。连八戒也笑了。

## ⑤

> "真有意思，悟净，"八戒说。

  唐僧站了起来。

> "我们该出发了。我想在天黑前找个村庄或寺院。"

  几个小时后，他们在山路上行进着。道路转过一个弯道，前面出现了一座小寺院。

## ⑥

  唐僧倒吸一口凉气。

> "那就是雷音寺吗？"

  "你们看，"八戒说，"金光从那里射出来。"

> "整座山都是金色的，"悟净补充道。

  悟空笑了。

## ⑦

> "那不是雷音寺。我们还没到西天呢。"

  唐僧怔了一会儿，接着皱起了眉头。

> "我们要小心。我看到寺院上空有乌云。"

  旅行者们继续沿着道路往前走。很快他们就到了寺院门前。门上的牌匾写着"小雷音寺"。

## ⑧

> "哇！"唐僧叫道，"这座寺院一定是佛祖的。"

  悟空摇了摇头。

> "不属于佛祖。我们绕道走吧。"

> "等等，"八戒说，"这座寺院金光闪闪，一定是个重要的地方。"

## ⑨

> "不是的，"悟空说，"我们真的应该——"

> "我们要进去，"唐僧说，"这是座佛门寺院，我们理当拜访。"

  唐僧推开寺院的门走了进去。八戒和悟净跟在后面。悟空叹了口气，也跟了进去。

## ⑩

  这个地方绝不是表面看起来的那样。猴子掏出了铁棒。

  一行人走进大殿，一尊巨大的金色佛像端坐眼前。

> "悟空，你看到那尊佛像了吗？"唐僧说，"这是真正的佛门寺院。"

## ⑪

  忽然传来一声雷鸣般的喝问。

> "是谁敢见佛不拜？"

  唐僧吓得发抖，立刻跪地叩拜。八戒和悟净也拜了下去。悟空依然站着，审视着佛像。他心中生疑。

## ⑫

> "你为何不拜？"那声音问道。

  悟空低声对唐僧说。

> "小心有诈。"

> "我正要拜——"悟空大喊，"我发现你们是假的！"

## ⑬

  当啷！猴子还没喊完，一对巨大的铙从天而降。它们合在一起，将悟空困在里面。

> "悟空！"唐僧大叫。

  光芒一闪，佛像变成了一个黄眉妖怪。妖怪站起来，微微一笑。

  唐僧上当了！
