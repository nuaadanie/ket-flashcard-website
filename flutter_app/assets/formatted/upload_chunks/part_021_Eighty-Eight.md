
# Journey to the West · Chapter Eighty-Eight: The Many-Eyed Demon

## ①

> "Many eyes!" Wukong gasped as his companions began to look ill.

  The Tang Monk's head bobbed forward and his tea cup dropped from his hand. And then, thump. The monk toppled to the floor.

  A moment later, Wujing and Bajie had passed out too.

## ②

  Wukong jumped to his feet and pulled out his iron bar. The priest backed away. Just then, a door burst open and the three women ran into the room.

> "You again!" cried the monkey.

  He leaped out of the way as the women shot webbing at him. He jumped up, did a flip in the air, and landed directly behind the women.

## ③

  Furiously, he slammed his iron bar down. Wham! There was a puff of smoke. When it cleared, a dead spider lay where one of the women had been. The other two women stared in horror.

> "He destroyed our sister!" cried one.

> "He's a monster!" cried the other.

## ④

  Wukong charged toward them. He knocked blind webbing out of his way, and then--whack! Bam! Both women were gone. In their places, lay dead spiders.

> "Those spider spirits were my dear friends!" cried the priest. His eyes burned with fury.

## ⑤

> "You'll regret destroying them!"

  The priest opened his robe, revealing a body covered in eyes. Suddenly, blinding light shot from the many eyes.

> "The light is too bright!" cried the monkey.

  He covered his own eyes and ran all over the room.

## ⑥

  But he couldn't escape the blinding light. Finally, he slammed his iron bar into the abbey stone floor. A huge hole opened up. The monkey jumped in and turned into a mole. Using his claws, he tunnelled until he was sure he was far from the abbey.

## ⑦

  When Wukong popped up out of the ground, he turned back into himself. Bright light was shining from the abbey. Even from far away, the light burned the monkey's eyes. Wukong turned away.

> "What will I do? I have no way to save my friends. The demon's light is too powerful."

  He sobbed and sobbed until he heard a voice.

## ⑧

> "But don't cry, Wukong."

  The monkey looked up. A tall woman was standing over him.

> "I am the Bodhisattva Pilanpo," said the woman. "That priest is a centipede spirit called the Demon Lord of Many Eyes. I am the only one who can defeat him."

## ⑨

  Pilanpo pulled a small needle from her sleeve. Turning, she threw the needle into the beams of light. Crack! The sound thundered in the sky, and the beams of light vanished. Wukong and Pilanpo returned to the abbey.

  The spider spirit was shaking on the floor. All of his eyes were squeezed shut.

## ⑩

  Pilanpo pointed her finger at the priest, and he turned into a giant centipede. Pilanpo took a bottle out of her sleeve.

> "Here, Wukong," said the Bodhisattva. "These pills will cure your companions. I will take the centipede with me to be punished."

> "Thank you, Bodhisattva." Wukong bowed.

## ⑪

  The journey continued, and many weeks went by.

> "The journey has been easy lately," said the Tang Monk one day.

  Bajie snorted.

> "That's true," said Wujing. "It's been weeks since any demons tried to eat Master."

## ⑫

> "The Western Paradise is still far away," Wukong warned the others. "We'll face many more dangers before we get there."

  The Tang Monk smiled.

> "Relax, Wukong, and enjoy the beautiful day. There is no danger here."

## ⑬

  Just then, a man appeared up ahead. He was waving his arms frantically.

> "Don't come this way!" shouted the man. "There's danger ahead. Great danger!"

# 🇨🇳 第八十八章：百眼魔君

## ①

> "好多眼睛！"悟空看着同伴们一个个面露病态，不禁惊叫。

  唐僧的头猛然垂下，茶杯从手中滑落，然后身子一软，便倒在了地上。

  不一会儿，悟净和八戒也昏倒过去。

## ②

  悟空一跃而起，抽出铁棒。道士连连后退。就在这时，一扇门猛然打开，三个女子冲进了房间。

> "又是你们！"悟空叫道。

  他纵身一闪，躲开女子们射来的蛛网。他跳入空中翻了一个筋斗，落在女子们的背后。

## ③

  悟空奋力挥起铁棒打去。啪一声，一阵烟雾散去，原本一个女子的地方变成了一只死蜘蛛。另外两个女子吓得目瞪口呆。

> "他杀死了我们的姐姐！"一个叫道。

> "他是个妖怪！"另一个叫道。

## ④

  悟空向她们冲去。他挥棒打碎蛛网，然后--砰！咚！两个女子也消失了。她们各自化成了一只死蜘蛛。

> "那些蜘蛛精是我的好朋友！"道士叫道，他双眼怒火燃烧。

## ⑤

> "你杀死她们，必定会后悔！"

  道士敞开衣袍，露出了长满眼睛的身体。突然，无数只眼睛射出刺目的光芒。

> "光太亮了！"悟空大叫。

  他捂住双眼，在房间里四处乱跑。

## ⑥

  但是他怎么也逃不脱那刺目的光芒。最后他将铁棒狠狠击入道观石板地面。地面裂开一个大洞。悟空跳进洞中变成了一只地鼠，用双爪一路挖掘，直到确信自己已远离道观。

## ⑦

  悟空从地下钻出地面，变回了自己的模样。道观的远处仍有亮光闪烁，那光芒即使远远望去仍刺得悟空双眼生疼。悟空转过身去。

> "我该怎么办？我无法救出同伴。恶魔的光芒太厉害了。"

  他哭了又哭，直到听到一个声音。

## ⑧

> "悟空，别哭了。"

  悟空抬头一看，一个高大的女人站在他面前。

> "我是毗蓝婆菩萨，"那女人说，"那个道士是一只蜈蚣精，叫做百眼魔君。只有我能降服他。"

## ⑨

  毗蓝婆从袖中取出一根绣花针，对着那些光柱扔去。咔嚓！天空中响起一声巨响，那些光柱立刻消失了。悟空和毗蓝婆一起回到了道观。

  那蜈蚣精倒在地上浑身发抖，所有眼睛紧紧闭着。

## ⑩

  毗蓝婆伸出手指着道士，道士立刻变成了一只巨大的蜈蚣。毗蓝婆从袖中取出一个瓶子。

> "悟空，这些药丸可以治好你的同伴，"毗蓝婆说，"我会将这只蜈蚣带回去惩治。"

> "感谢菩萨。"悟空行了一个大礼。

## ⑪

  一行人继续前行，几个星期过去了。

> "这段日子可真太平啊，"唐僧有一天说。

  八戒哼了一声。

> "是啊，"悟净说，"已经好几个星期没有妖怪想吃师父了。"

## ⑫

> "灵山还远着呢，"悟空对大家说，"在到达之前，我们还会遇到更多的危险。"

  唐僧笑了一笑。

> "悟空，放松放松，享受这美好的一天吧。这里没有什么危险的。"

## ⑬

  就在这时，前方突然出现了一个人，他拼命地挥动着双臂。

> "别往这边走了！"那人大叫，"前面有危险，非常大的危险！"
