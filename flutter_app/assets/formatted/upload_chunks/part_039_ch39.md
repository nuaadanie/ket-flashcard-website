
# Journey to the West · Chapter One Hundred and Six: The Question

## ①

  Guanyin looked at the messenger in surprise.

> "What is the problem?" she asked.

> "It's about the Tang monk," said the messenger. "To become a Buddha, he needs to face a hundred challenges. I was just looking at the list of challenges he's faced. There are only ninety-nine challenges on the list. He must face one more challenge to become a Buddha."

## ②

> "We're moving so fast," said the Tang monk, smiling.

  The travelers were flying back east. The guard from the Thunderclap Monastery was using a spell to carry them.

> "I wish the whole journey had been this easy," said Bajie.

> "You couldn't, Master was still mortal before we reached the Western Paradise. He couldn't be carried then," said Wukong.

## ③

  Wukong looked to the horizon.

> "We'll reach the Tang Empire soon."

  Just then, a messenger caught up with them. The messenger whispered something in the guard's ear. The guard glanced quickly at the travelers, then he looked back at the messenger and nodded.

## ④

  Instead of continuing, the spell broke and the travelers fell from the sky. *Bam!* They slammed into the ground next to a wide river.

  Wukong stood up and saw the guard and messenger flying back west. The monkey shook his head.

> "What happened?"

  The Tang monk stood up and brushed mud from his robe.

> "Why did the guard drop us?"

## ⑤

> "I'll tell you why," said Bajie. "He's a villain. He thinks it's funny to drop people."

  The pig waved his fist at the guard, who was now far away.

  "Mild," Wukong turned slowly to the Tang monk.

> "I think I know why he dropped us, Master. You must face a hundred challenges in order to become a Buddha. He probably got to the Western Paradise before you'd faced all of them."

## ⑥

> "Oh no!" the monk trembled. "So other bad things must happen now?"

  Wukong nodded.

> "I hope there won't be too many more problems."

> "The first problem is crossing the river," said Wujing.

## ⑦

> "It looks familiar," said the monk. "We must have crossed it during the journey west."

  Suddenly a large form rose from the river. It was an enormous turtle.

> "Greetings," said the turtle. "Do you remember me?"

  The travelers all smiled.

> "Yes, we do," said the Tang monk. "You carried us over this river many years ago."

## ⑧

> "Yes," said the turtle. "And I will gladly carry you across again. Hop on to my back."

  The travelers climbed on and the turtle started across the river. Wujing and Bajie soon fell asleep while Wukong meditated. The Tang monk stayed awake too, sitting near the turtle's head.

## ⑨

> "Did you reach the Western Paradise?" asked the turtle.

> "Yes," said the Tang monk. "It is a wonderful place. We are now returning to the east so I can deliver the true scriptures."

> "Good," said the turtle. "Did you ask Buddha when I would be reborn as a human?"

## ⑩

  The monk bit his lip. He had forgotten to ask the turtle's question.

> "You are not answering," said the turtle.

> "I'm sorry," said the monk. "I..."

## ⑪

  The turtle suddenly dived under the water. It dumped everyone off his back. Wukong's eyes flew open as he hit the cold water. He leaped into the air. Bajie, Wujing, and the horse quickly flew across the river. But the Tang monk was flailing furiously in the rushing water.

> "Help!" yelped the monk.

## ⑫

  Wukong cast a spell and lifted the monk and the bow from the river. When everyone was on dry land, the monkey turned the bag upside down. Water poured out, followed by the scrolls. They were soaked.

> "The true scriptures!" cried the Tang monk. "They are wet!"

# 🇨🇳 第一百零六章：一问

## ①

  观音惊讶地看着信使。

> "出了什么问题？"她问道。

> "是关于唐僧的事，"信使说，"要成为佛，他必须经历百难。我刚才查了他经历的劫难清单。清单上只有九十九难。他还必须再经历一难才能成佛。"

## ②

> "我们飞得好快啊，"唐僧微笑着说。

  旅人们正飞回东土。雷音寺的护法施法术载着他们。

> "要是整段旅程都这么轻松就好了，"八戒说。

> "那可不行。没到西天之前师父还是凡胎，不能这样带着他飞的，"悟空说。

## ③

  悟空望向远方。

> "我们马上就到东土大唐了。"

  就在此时，一个信使追上了他们。信使在护法耳边低语了几句。护法快速瞥了一眼旅人们，又看向信使，点了点头。

## ④

  护法停了法术，旅人们从天上掉了下来。*砰！*他们摔在一条宽阔的河边。

  悟空站起来，看到护法和信使正往西天方向飞去。猴子摇了摇头。

> "怎么回事？"

  唐僧站起来，抖掉了袈裟上的泥土。

> "护法为什么把我们丢下来？"

## ⑤

> "我来告诉你为什么，"八戒说，"他是个坏蛋。他觉得把人从天上丢下来很好玩。"

  八戒朝已经飞远的护法的方向挥了挥拳头。

  悟空慢慢转向唐僧。

> "师父，我想我知道他为什么把我们扔下来了。要成佛您得历经百难。大概是护法发现您还差一难没经历。"

## ⑥

> "哎呀！"唐僧浑身发抖，"难道还要再出什么事吗？"

  悟空点了点头。

> "希望别再出太多问题了。"

> "第一个问题就是过河，"悟净说。

## ⑦

> "这河看起来很眼熟，"唐僧说，"我们西行的路上一定走过这里。"

  突然，河面上升起了一个巨大的身影。是一只巨大的乌龟。

> "诸位好，"乌龟说，"还记得我吗？"

  旅人们都笑了。

> "当然记得，"唐僧说，"多年前你驮我们过了这条河。"

## ⑧

> "不错，"乌龟说，"这次我还乐意载你们过去。请上背上来。"

  旅人们爬上龟背，乌龟开始过河。悟净和八戒很快就睡着了，悟空在一旁打坐。唐僧也醒着，坐在乌龟的头旁边。

## ⑨

> "你们到了西天吗？"乌龟问道。

> "到了，"唐僧说，"那是一个神奇的地方。我们现在回东土，我要把真经带回去。"

> "那太好了，"乌龟说，"你帮我问佛祖我没有？我什么时候才能再投胎做人？"

## ⑩

  唐僧咬了咬嘴唇。他已经忘了帮乌龟问了。

> "您怎么不回答？"乌龟说。

> "对不起，"唐僧说，"我……"

## ⑪

  乌龟突然往水下一沉，把背上的所有人都翻了下水。悟空被冷水一激，立刻睁开了眼睛。他纵身飞上了空中。八戒、悟净和白龙马都快速飞过了河。但是唐僧在湍急的河水中拼命挣扎。

> "救命！"唐僧叫道。

## ⑫

  悟空施法将唐僧和经包从河中捞起。众人上了岸，猴子把包袱倒了个底朝天。水哗啦啦流了出来，紧跟着涌出的是经卷。全都被泡透了。

> "真经！"唐僧叫了起来，"全都泡湿了！"
