
# Journey to the West · Chapter Ninety-Two: A Problem for the Lion Demon

## ①

  The elephant and the raptor stared at the lion.

> "The monkey is still alive inside you," said the raptor.

  The lion sat down on his throne.

> "I'm worried about what?"

> "Well," said the raptor, "the monkey is talking inside you. Doesn't that bother you?"

## ②

> "Let him talk," said the lion defiantly. "It doesn't bother me at all. What shall I talk about? Who wants to hear a story?"

  The elephant shook his head slowly.

> "What if the monkey never stops talking? You'll never get any sleep."

> "That would be bad," he thought for a moment. "I know what to do. I'll stop eating."

## ③

> "What will that do?" asked the elephant.

> "Yes," said Wukong from inside, "what will that do?"

> "If I don't eat, Wukong doesn't eat," said the lion. "I'll make him starve."

  Wukong laughed.

> "Nice try, lion. But I'll just turn one of my hairs into a stove. I'll cook a nice meal in here."

## ④

> "Oh dear," the raptor shook his head in defiance. "A stove? It will make you very hot. Plus, it will create lots of smoke. The smoke will fill your head, and you'll never stop coughing."

  At last, the lion was starting to look nervous.

> "That would be terrible," he said.

  I can call for Wukong.

## ⑤

  The lion went to the middle of the room. He bent over and coughed. Wukong laughed.

> "Ha ha! I'm still in here!"

  The lion coughed again. But still, Wukong stayed inside him.

> "I'm going to practice my fighting skills," announced the monkey.

  Wukong's punches and kicks landed inside the lion. The lion fell to his knees.

## ⑥

> "No!" he moaned. "Hey, it hurts, it hurts! Wukong, please stop! Let's make a deal."

> "I'll come out if you promise to let us cross the mountain safely," said Wukong.

  The lion looked up at the raptor and the elephant. They both nodded.

> "We promise," the lion moaned again.

## ⑦

> "Okay then," said Wukong. "I'm coming out."

  Wukong started the climb up the lion's throat. Just as he reached the lion's mouth, Wukong heard whispering.

> "Lion, I have a plan," whispered the elephant. "When Wukong tries to come out of your mouth, bite him."

## ⑧

> "I don't know," whispered the lion. "Wukong is very strong. My belly still hurts."

> "Jump in quickly before he can fight back," whispered the elephant.

> "Okay," said the lion.

  Those dumb demons, thought Wukong. Don't they realize I can hear them?

  He pulled out his iron bar.

## ⑨

> "Shall I come out now?" asked the monkey.

> "Um, yes," said the lion.

  Wukong heard the three demons snickering.

> "Okay," said Wukong. "I'm coming out."

  He held out the iron bar. The lion bit down hard on the bar.

## ⑩

  *Crack!* A tooth shattered into tiny pieces.

> "Ow, ow, ow!" The lion howled in pain. Tears streamed down his face. He rolled around on the floor, holding his paws over his mouth.

> "You villains!" Wukong shouted. "I heard you whispering!"

> "I'm sorry," the lion was still crying, "I will never try to bite you again. Please don't hurt me anymore."

## ⑪

  Wukong hesitated.

> "I'm coming out now. You better not try any more tricks."

> "We won't," said the elephant.

> "We promise," said the raptor.

  Wukong popped out of the lion's nose. He glared at the demons for a moment and then walked out of the cave.

## ⑫

  As soon as the monkey was gone, the elephant turned to the other two demons.

> "I have a new plan for capturing the Tang monk."

# 🇨🇳 第九十二章：狮妖的麻烦

## ①

  大象和猛禽精盯着狮子。

> "猴子还在你肚子里活着呢，"猛禽精说。

  狮子在他的王座上坐了下来。

> "我担心什么？"

> "嗯，"猛禽精说，"猴子在你肚子里不停地说话，你不难受吗？"

## ②

> "让他说吧，"狮子不服气地说，"我一点也不在乎。说什么？谁要听故事？"

  大象慢慢地摇了摇头。

> "万一猴子永远不停呢？那你就永远别想睡觉了。"

> "那确实不好，"他想了一会儿，"我知道该怎么办了，我不吃东西了。"

## ③

> "那有什么用？"大象问。

> "对啊，"里面的悟空说，"那有什么用？"

> "我不吃，悟空就没得吃，"狮子说，"我饿死他。"

  悟空笑了。

> "不错的主意，狮子。不过我只需拔一根毛变成炉子，我就能在里面做顿美餐。"

## ④

> "哎呀，"猛禽精摇着头说，"炉子？那可会让你非常热。而且，会产生很多烟。烟会充满你的脑袋，你会不停地咳嗽。"

  狮子终于开始露出紧张的神色了。

> "那可就糟了，"他说。

## ⑤

  狮子走到屋子中间，弯下腰咳嗽起来。悟空哈哈大笑。

> "哈哈！我还在里面呢！"

  狮子又咳了一次。可是悟空仍然待在他肚子里。

> "我要开始练武了，"猴子宣布道。

  悟空在狮子肚子里拳打脚踢。狮子跪倒在地。

## ⑥

> "不！"他呻吟着，"哎哟，好疼，好疼！悟空，求你停手吧！我们做个交易。"

> "你答应让我们安全过山，我就出来，"悟空说。

  狮子抬头看了看猛禽精和大象。他们都点了点头。

> "我们答应你，"狮子又呻吟了一声。

## ⑦

> "好吧，"悟空说，"我出来了。"

  悟空开始往狮子的喉咙上爬。就在他到了狮子嘴边时，悟空听到了窃窃私语。

> "狮子，我有个计划，"大象小声说，"悟空从你嘴里出来时，你就咬他。"

## ⑧

> "我不知道啊，"狮子小声说，"悟空很厉害的，我的肚子还疼呢。"

> "趁他還沒动手，赶紧咬下去，"大象小声说。

> "好，"狮子说。

  这些蠢货，悟空想，他们不知道我能听到吗？

  他掏出了金箍棒。

## ⑨

> "我现在可以出来了吗？"猴子问。

> "嗯，可以，"狮子说。

  悟空听到三个妖怪在偷笑。

> "好吧，"悟空说，"我出来了。"

  他举起了金箍棒。狮子用力咬了下去。

## ⑩

  咔嚓！一颗牙齿碎成了碎片。

> "哎呀呀！"狮子疼得大叫，眼泪哗哗地流下来。他在地上打滚，两只爪子捂着嘴巴。

> "你们这些坏蛋！"悟空大喊，"你们的悄悄话我都听见了！"

> "对不起，"狮子还在哭，"我再也不敢咬你了，求你别再打我了。"

## ⑪

  悟空犹豫了一下。

> "我现在出来了，你们最好别再耍花招。"

> "不会了，"大象说。

> "我们保证，"猛禽精说。

  悟空从狮子鼻子里蹦了出来。他瞪了妖怪们一眼，然后走出了洞穴。

## ⑫

  猴子一走，大象就转向另外两个妖怪。

> "我有个抓唐僧的新计划。"
