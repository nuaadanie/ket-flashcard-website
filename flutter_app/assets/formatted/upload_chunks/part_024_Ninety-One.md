
# Journey to the West · Chapter Ninety-One: The Demon Officers

## ①

  The demon general looked at Wukong, who was still in disguise.

> "The Tang monk is heading toward our mountain," the general said.

> "Yes!" said Wukong. "That is great news!"

> "That is great news!" said a demon captain.

  "Unfortunately, there's also some bad news," Wukong pretended to look worried.

## ②

  The general narrowed his eyes at Wukong.

> "And the third companion?"

> "The third companion is a monkey," said Wukong. "I believe he is Sun Wukong."

> "I was afraid that monkey was traveling with the monk," the general grumbled. "What does Sun Wukong look like?"

## ③

> "He's huge!" Wukong pretended to shiver. "He has fiery red eyes and sharp teeth!"

> "Oh dear," said a captain.

> "There's no need to worry about Sun's silly monkey," said the colonel. "I don't care what he did five hundred years ago. We have a powerful army!"

## ④

> "I heard the monkey talking," said Wukong. "He said he hopes there's an entire demon army on this mountain."

> "What?" The general looked confused. "He hopes there's an army?"

  Wukong nodded.

> "He said destroying demons is good exercise. He has a magic iron bar. He can make it big enough to crush our entire army with one strike. But he doesn't plan to do that."

## ⑤

> "No?" a captain gulped. "What does he plan to do?"

> "He pledged to crush one demon at a time," said Wukong. "That way he'll get more exercise."

  The demon's face went pale.

> "This is bad news," said the general.

  The other demons nodded in agreement.

## ⑥

> "You can go around if you want to," said Wukong. "But I'm leaving."

  A captain looked surprised.

> "What about eating the Tang monk? Don't you want to live forever?"

  Wukong shook his head.

> "I heard our three leaders talking privately. They don't plan to share the Tang monk with us. They're going to eat him themselves."

## ⑦

  The officers grew angry.

> "That's not fair," said the colonel.

> "It's terrible," said a captain.

  The general bit his lip.

> "If our leaders won't share the Tang monk with us, they can catch him themselves. Let's gather the troops and leave this place."

## ⑧

  Soon, Wukong could see the demon army marching away in the valley below. The monkey smiled.

  I'd get rid of the army. Now I'll deal with the three demon leaders.

  Still in disguise, Wukong entered the cave. He came to a large room. Three huge demons -- a lion, an elephant, and a raptor -- sat on thrones.

## ⑨

> "The Tang monk is heading toward the mountain," Wukong announced.

> "All right," said the lion. "Tell the troops to capture him."

> "The troops left," said Wukong.

> "Why?" shouted the raptor.

## ⑩

> "The Tang monk is travelling with Sun Wukong," said the monkey. "The troops were afraid of the monkey."

> "So it's true that Sun Wukong is with the monk," said the elephant. "That monkey is powerful. We should leave the Tang monk alone."

> "You're heard," the lion said. "We don't need an army to fight a monkey. We'll capture the Tang monk ourselves."

## ⑪

> "You're crazy," said the elephant. "Sun Wukong defeated Heaven's entire army."

> "Nobody's crazy," shouted the lion. "Don't shout at me!"

> "I don't shout at anyone I want to!" shouted the lion.

  As the two demons argued, Wukong began to laugh. Soon he was laughing hard.

## ⑫

  The demons stopped arguing and stared at him. The monkey looked down and saw that his disguise was gone.

  He gulped.

> "Oh."

# 🇨🇳 第九十一章：妖怪军官

## ①

  妖怪将军打量着仍然伪装着的悟空。

> "唐僧正朝我们这座山走来，"将军说。

> "没错！"悟空说，"这可真是个好消息！"

> "真是个好消息！"一个妖怪队长说。

  "不过，还有一个坏消息，"悟空假装露出担忧的神情。

## ②

  将军眯起了眼睛。

> "第三个同伴呢？"

> "第三个同伴是一只猴子，"悟空说，"我想他就是孙悟空。"

> "我早就担心那猴子跟唐僧在一起，"将军嘟囔着，"孙悟空长什么样？"

## ③

> "他巨大无比！"悟空假装打了个寒战，"他有一双火红的眼睛和尖牙利齿！"

> "哎呀，"一个队长说。

> "没必要担心那只傻猴子，"校尉说，"我不在乎他五百年前干了什么，我们有一支强大的大军！"

## ④

> "我听到那只猴子在说话，"悟空说，"他说他希望这座山上有一整支妖怪大军。"

> "什么？"将军一脸困惑，"他希望有军队？"

  悟空点了点头。

> "他说消灭妖怪是很好的锻炼。他有一根神奇的铁棒，可以变得足够大，一击就能压碎我们整支大军。不过他没打算那么干。"

## ⑤

> "哦？"一个队长吓了一跳，"他打算怎么干？"

> "他发誓一次只消灭一个妖怪，"悟空说，"这样他能得到更多锻炼。"

  妖怪们的脸色都白了。

> "这是个坏消息，"将军说。

  其他妖怪纷纷点头表示同意。

## ⑥

> "你们可以绕道走，"悟空说，"不过我先走了。"

  一个队长露出了惊讶的表情。

> "那唐僧呢？不想尝尝唐僧肉长生不老吗？"

  悟空摇了摇头。

> "我听到三个大王私下里在谈论。他们不打算把唐僧分给我们。他们要自己吃掉唐僧。"

## ⑦

  军官们愤怒了。

> "这太不公平了，"校尉说。

> "太可恶了，"一个队长说。

  将军咬了咬嘴唇。

> "既然大王们不愿意把唐僧分给我们，那就让他们自己去抓好了。我们集合队伍，离开这儿吧。"

## ⑧

  很快，悟空就看见山谷下面妖怪大军正在撤退。猴子微微一笑。

  大军赶跑了，现在该对付三个大王了。

  悟空仍然伪装着走进了妖怪洞。他来到了一个大房间。三个巨大的妖怪——狮子精、大象精和猛禽精——坐在王座上。

## ⑨

> "唐僧正朝这座山走来，"悟空宣布道。

> "很好，"狮子精说，"让弟兄们去抓他。"

> "弟兄们都走了，"悟空说。

> "为什么？"猛禽精吼道。

## ⑩

> "唐僧跟孙悟空一起，"猴子说，"弟兄们害怕孙悟空。"

> "原来孙悟空真的跟唐僧在一起，"大象精说，"那只猴子厉害得很，我们不该动唐僧。"

> "你听我说，"狮子精说，"我们不需要大军来对付一个猴子。我们自己就能抓到唐僧。"

## ⑪

> "你疯了，"大象精说，"孙悟空可是打败过天庭整支大军的人。"

> "谁疯了？"狮子精吼道，"你们冲我嚷什么！"

> "我没跟任何人嚷嚷！"狮子精大喊。

  两个妖怪吵了起来，悟空却开始笑了。很快他就笑得停不下来。

## ⑫

  妖怪们停止了争吵，全都盯着他。猴子低头一看，发现自己的伪装已经消失了。

  他咽了一口唾沫。

> "噢。"
