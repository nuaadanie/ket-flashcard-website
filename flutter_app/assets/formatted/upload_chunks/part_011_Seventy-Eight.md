
# Journey to the West · Chapter Seventy-Eight: Tricked Again and Again

## ①

  Wukong looked at the fan.

> "This fan doesn't work! The Iron Immortal must have tricked me!"

  The travelers made their way back to the village. The fire burned hotter.

> "What will we do?" asked the Tang monk. "We can't go through that fire."

  Bajie threw his hands up.

> "Let's just go home! We'll write a letter to Buddha explaining what happened."

> "Bajie, we can't do that," the Tang monk scolded.

## ②

  Wukong spoke up.

> "I used to be friends with the Iron Immortal's husband, the Bull Demon King. I have an idea."

  The Iron Immortal was in her cave when she heard a knock.

> "Oh no!" she said to herself. "That monkey is back."

  She walked quietly to the door and peeked outside. When she saw the Bull Demon King, she smiled and opened the door.

## ③

> "I'm glad you're back," said the Iron Immortal.

> "Sun Wukong was here," said the demon. "I used to be friends with him. It would be nice to see him again."

  His wife was confused.

> "What do you mean? You told me that you never really liked him."

> "Oh, really?" said the demon. "He was very rude."

  Said the woman.

> "He demanded I let him borrow my fan."

## ④

> "You didn't give it to him, did you?" asked the demon.

> "Of course not," said his wife. "I gave him a fake fan that looks just like the real one."

  The demon began pacing.

> "Wukong is very smart and very powerful. He'll realize you tricked him, and then he'll come back here. What should we do?" asked the Iron Immortal.

> "Give me the fan," said the demon. "I'll keep it safe."

## ⑤

  The Iron Immortal opened a chest and took out the fan. She handed it to her husband. Suddenly, light sparkled, and Wukong stood in front of her.

> "I tricked you!" the monkey laughed. He ran out of the cave.

  The Iron Immortal stamped her foot. She was still angry when her husband came home.

> "Hello, dear," said the Bull Demon King with a big smile.

  But then his smile faded.

> "What's wrong?"

> "Your friend Sun Wukong was here," she snapped.

## ⑥

> "He's not my friend," said the demon. "Even before he defeated Red Boy, I never really liked him."

> "Well, you'll like him even less now," said the Iron Immortal. "He stole my fan."

  The demon looked surprised.

> "My rival's wind magic is powerful. How did the monkey steal it?"

> "He tricked me into giving it to him," said his wife. "He made himself look like you!"

## ⑦

  The demon's face turned red.

> "How dare he do that?"

  He took a deep breath.

> "Don't worry. I will find that rotten monkey. I'll get your fan back."

  The Bull Demon King stomped out of the cave.

## ⑧

  Wukong walked along the road, carrying the plantain leaf fan. He was still laughing about how he tricked the Iron Immortal. Then he saw Bajie approaching.

> "Bajie!" cried Wukong. "What are you doing here?"

> "I was coming to help," said the pig.

> "Thanks, but I don't need help." Wukong held up the fan. "I tricked the Iron Immortal and got the real fan this time."

## ⑨

> "Good!" said Bajie. "I can carry it for you."

> "Thanks!" said Wukong as he handed the fan to Bajie.

  The monkey patted his friend on the shoulder.

> "Now let's get back to Master. We need to..."

  Suddenly there was a flash of light. A second later, the Bull Demon King stood next to Wukong. The demon laughed.

> "I tricked you, Wukong!"

  There was another flash, and the demon was gone.

# 🇨🇳 第七十八章：再遭欺骗

## ①

  悟空看着手中的扇子。

> "这扇子没用！铁扇仙又骗了我！"

  一行人回到了村里。火焰烧得更旺了。

> "我们怎么办？"唐僧问道，"那火是过不去的。"

  八戒把手一摊。

> "干脆回家算了！我们给佛祖写封信说明情况。"

> "八戒，那怎么行？"唐僧训斥道。

## ②

  悟空开口了。

> "我以前跟铁扇仙的丈夫牛魔王是朋友。我有一个主意。"

  铁扇仙在洞里听到敲门声。

> "坏了！"她心里一惊，"这猴子又回来了。"

  她悄悄走到门口，从门缝往外看。看到牛魔王时她笑了，打开门。

## ③

> "你回来啦。"铁扇仙说。

> "孙悟空来过了，"牛魔王说，"我以前跟他可是好兄弟。能再见到他也挺好。"

  妻子感到困惑。

> "你说什么？你不是说你一直讨厌他吗？"

> "哦，是吗？"牛魔王说。

  女人说。

> "他来找我借芭蕉扇。"

## ④

> "你没借给他吧？"牛魔王问。

> "当然没有，"妻子说，"我给了他把假扇子，跟真的一模一样。"

  牛魔王开始来回踱步。

> "孙悟空又聪明又厉害。他会发现你骗了他，到时候还会回来找麻烦的。怎么办？"铁扇仙问。

> "把扇子给我，"牛魔王说，"我来保管。"

## ⑤

  铁扇仙打开箱子，取出了芭蕉扇，交给了丈夫。突然光芒一闪，悟空出现在她面前。

> "我又骗到你了！"猴子大笑一声，跑出了洞。

  铁扇仙气得直跺脚。这时她的丈夫回来了。

> "夫人。"牛魔王满面笑容。

  但他很快笑不出来了。

> "怎么了？"

> "你那个朋友孙悟空来过！"铁扇仙怒道。

## ⑥

> "他可不是我的朋友，"牛魔王说，"就算在红孩儿事件之前，我也没多喜欢他。"

> "哼，你现在更该恨他了，"铁扇仙说，"他抢走了我的芭蕉扇。"

  牛魔王吃了一惊。

> "孙悟空的风魔法很厉害，他怎么抢到扇子的？"

> "他骗我交出来的，"妻子说，"他变成了你的模样！"

## ⑦

  牛魔王气得满脸通红。

> "岂有此理！"

  他深吸了一口气。

> "别担心，我去找那该死的猴子。一定把扇子夺回来。"

  牛魔王大步走出山洞。

## ⑧

  悟空走在路上，手里拿着芭蕉扇，还笑自己智取铁扇仙的事。这时八戒迎面走来。

> "八戒！"悟空叫道，"你怎么来了？"

> "我来帮你呀。"八戒说。

> "谢了，不过不需要。"悟空举起扇子，"这次我骗了铁扇仙，拿到真的了。"

## ⑨

> "太好了！"八戒说，"我来帮你拿吧。"

> "好！"悟空把扇子交给了八戒。

  猴子拍了拍朋友的肩膀。

> "走吧，我们回去找师父。我们要……"

  突然一道闪光。眨眼之间，牛魔王出现在悟空面前。

  那妖怪大笑。

> "孙悟空，我又骗到你了！"

  又是一道闪光，妖怪消失了。
