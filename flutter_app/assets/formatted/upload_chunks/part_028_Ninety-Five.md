
# Journey to the West · Chapter Ninety-Five: Bajie and the Monster

## ①

  Bajie took a step back as a dark mist surrounded the people on the road. When the mist cleared, he saw they were really a monster and several little demons. The monster grinned at him.

> "You are here for the feast, pig? We're going to eat you for lunch."

  Bajie raised his rake and lunged as the monster approached. The demons rushed toward the pig from all sides.

## ②

> "That brat monkey lied to me!" cried Bajie.

  He spun around and knocked over two demons with his rake. The monster threw a punch. Bajie blocked the punch with his rake.

> "Ow!" cried the monster, rubbing his hand.

## ③

  Bajie kept his eyes on demons sneaking up behind him.

> "That monkey is always causing trouble," the pig muttered.

  He slammed the rake down on another demon. Sometimes I wonder if I'd like to eat. Did he really have to trick me? The monster attacked again. Bajie brandished his rake, still complaining about Wukong.

> "Wukong is small. He doesn't need to eat as much as I do."

## ④

  The monster suddenly stopped fighting and looked at Bajie curiously.

> "You just said Wukong. Are you talking about Sun Wukong? The monkey who defeated Heaven's entire army five hundred years ago?"

> "Yes. Do you know him?"

> "I've heard he's travelling with the Tang monk," said the monster. "You must be travelling with the Tang monk too!"

## ⑤

> "Why are you interested in the monk?" asked Bajie suspiciously.

  The monster licked his lips.

> "I've heard that anyone who eats the Tang monk will live forever."

  Thinking quickly, Bajie ran from the demons and back to his companions. He pointed at Wukong.

## ⑥

> "You tricked me!"

  The monkey was laughing.

> "How was the feast, Bajie?"

  Bajie's eyes burned with fury. He turned to the Tang monk.

> "Wukong told me there was a feast up ahead, but instead it was a group of demons and a monster! They tried to eat me!"

## ⑦

  The Tang monk was angry.

> "Bajie, I thought you were going to check the mountain. Instead you went to look for a feast. You lied to me. You should be ashamed of yourself."

  Bajie hung his head. By now, Wukong was rolling on the ground with laughter.

> "And you, Wukong!" snapped the Tang monk. "I should recite the Tight Headband spell to punish you."

  The monkey stopped laughing. He jumped to his feet.

## ⑧

> "Please don't do that."

> "You knew there were demons and a monster ahead," said the monk, "and you didn't say anything?"

  Wujing shook his head.

> "You should have said something, Wukong."

> "I admit I knew about the demons and the monster," said Wukong. "But they're not dangerous."

## ⑨

> "Not dangerous?" Bajie scoffed. "They wanted to eat me. And now they'll eat Master!"

> "Oh no!" cried the Tang monk.

  Wukong held up his hands.

> "Relax, Master. You have nothing to worry about."

## ⑩

> "We must capture the Tang monk," the monster said to the demons. "But we need a plan. Sun Wukong is strong. Did you know that he defeated Heaven's entire army?"

> "Yes, we've heard," said one of the demons.

## ⑪

  Another demon hurried over.

> "I just spied on the travelers. The Tang monk has three spirit companions: Sun Wukong, the pig, and a purple fool."

> "Hmm," said the monster.

> "Really?" said the demon. "I have a plan to lure the spirits away from the monk."

  The demon told the monster his plan.

## ⑫

  When he was done speaking, the demon folded his arms.

> "What do you think?"

  A big smile spread across the monster's face.

> "I love it."

# 🇨🇳 第九十五章：八戒与怪物

## ①

  八戒后退了一步，一团黑雾笼罩了路上的人。等雾散去，他看到那其实是一只怪物和几个小妖怪。怪物冲他咧嘴一笑。

> "你是来赴宴的猪吗？我们要把你当午饭吃了！"

  八戒举起钉耙，朝冲来的怪物扑了上去。小妖怪们从四面八方朝猪扑来。

## ②

> "那只死猴子骗了我！"八戒大喊。

  他一个转身，用钉耙打翻了两个妖怪。怪物挥拳打来，八戒用钉耙挡住。

> "哎哟！"怪物揉着手叫了起来。

## ③

  八戒留意着身后偷偷摸上来的小妖怪。

> "那只猴子总是惹麻烦，"猪咕噜道。

  他一耙子又打翻了一个妖怪。有时候我真怀疑自己要不要吃东西，他为什么非要骗我呢？怪物又攻了上来。八戒挥舞钉耙，嘴里还在抱怨悟空。

> "悟空个子那么小，饭量肯定没我大。"

## ④

  怪物突然停止了打斗，好奇地看了看八戒。

> "你刚才说了悟空。你说的是孙悟空吗？那个五百年前打败天庭大军的猴子？"

> "对。你认识他？"

> "我听说他跟唐僧一起取经，"怪物说，"你一定也是跟唐僧在一起的！"

## ⑤

> "你为什么对唐僧感兴趣？"八戒怀疑地问。

  怪物舔了舔嘴唇。

> "我听说吃了唐僧就能长生不老。"

  八戒赶紧跑回了同伴身边。他指着悟空。

## ⑥

> "你骗我！"

  猴子正在笑。

> "八戒，宴席怎么样啊？"

  八戒怒火中烧。他转向唐僧。

> "悟空说前面有宴席，结果是一群妖怪和一个怪物！他们要吃了我！"

## ⑦

  唐僧很生气。

> "八戒，我以为你是去探路的。你却去找宴席吃，你还骗我。你太不像话了。"

  八戒低下了头。这时悟空已经笑得在地上打滚了。

> "还有你，悟空！"唐僧斥责道，"我该念紧箍咒罚你了。"

  猴子不笑了。他跳了起来。

## ⑧

> "师父别念。"

> "你明知道前面有妖怪和怪物，"唐僧说，"为什么不告诉我？"

  悟净摇了摇头。

> "你确实该说的，悟空。"

> "我承认我知道有妖怪和怪物，"悟空说，"但是他们不危险。"

## ⑨

> "不危险？"八戒讽刺道，"他们想吃我，接下来就要吃师父了！"

> "不好了！"唐僧大叫。

  悟空举起双手。

> "别慌，师父。没什么可怕的。"

## ⑩

> "我们必须抓住唐僧，"怪物对妖怪们说，"不过我们需要一个计划。孙悟空很强，你们知道他打败过天庭整支大军吗？"

> "听说过，"一个小妖怪说。

## ⑪

  另一个妖怪匆匆赶过来。

> "我刚去偷看了。唐僧有三个灵物同伴：孙悟空、猪还有一个紫面汉。"

> "嗯，"怪物沉吟。

> "是吗？"妖怪说，"我有个把他们从唐僧身边引开的计策。"

  妖怪把他的计策告诉了怪物。

## ⑫

  他说完之后，双手交叉抱在胸前。

> "你觉得怎么样？"

  怪物的脸上绽开了大大的笑容。

> "我喜欢这个计策。"
