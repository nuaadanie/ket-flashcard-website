
# Journey to the West · Chapter Seventy-Nine: The Mountain of Flames

## ①

  The mountain of flames. Wukong was furious.

> "The Bull Demon King tricked me!"

  The monkey rushed back to the cave and smashed the door. Crush! The Bull Demon King ran outside.

> "You villain!" he shouted. "You destroyed my door."

## ②

> "Give me that fan right now!" yelled Wukong. "Or I'll destroy the rest of your mountain!"

> "Never!" said the demon.

  Wukong charged toward the demon. Whoa! The demon turned into a crane and flew away.

## ③

  He's not the only one who can turn into other animals, thought the monkey.

  Wukong turned into a phoenix and shot after the crane. With his claws out, he swooped down at the crane.

  The crane dipped into the lower, and Wukong missed. When Wukong looked back, the crane was gone.

## ④

  Where did he go? thought Wukong.

  He looked down and saw an antelope grazing in a field.

  That's him!

  Wukong landed and turned into a tiger. He crept through the tall grass. When he got close to the antelope, he leaped.

## ⑤

  Light flashed, and the antelope turned into a huge white bull. The bull flew up into the sky, and Wukong followed.

  He had almost caught up to the bull when he heard a shout.

> "Step back, Wukong!"

  It was a soldier from heaven, sent by the Jade Emperor to help.

## ⑥

  More soldiers rushed toward the bull. With a snort, the bull spun around and flew the other way. But other soldiers appeared and blocked the bull's path.

  Wukong watched as soldiers surrounded the bull. They moved in closer, their swords and spears all pointed at the bull.

  The Bull Demon King returned to his demon form.

## ⑦

> "Please don't hurt the Iron Fan Princess," she cried, rushing toward the soldiers. "Don't harm my husband! He didn't mean to be evil."

  One soldier lowered his sword.

> "We won't harm you if you're truly sorry for being evil. But right now you're stopping the Tang Monk from continuing his journey."

> "Wukong will call for your fan."

  The Iron Fan Princess handed the fan to the soldier.

## ⑧

> "Here's the fan," she said. "We are truly sorry. My husband and I will be good Buddhists from now on. Just please don't hurt us!"

  The soldier looked at the Bull Demon King. The demon nodded.

> "We promise."

> "Very well," said the soldier. "You two may return to your cave. But you must avoid trouble from now on."

## ⑨

  The demon and his wife bowed and left. The soldier handed the fan to Wukong.

> "It's good that you'll be the one to put out the mountain flames, Wukong."

  Wukong was confused.

> "Why?"

> "You're the one who caused the fire," said the soldier. "It was when Laozi tried to burn you up in his pot. You leapt out of the pot, disturbing the fire in his workroom. The burning ember fell to earth that day. It started the fire on the mountain."

## ⑩

  Wukong looked at the fan.

> "I'll be happy to put out the fire."

  Wukong returned to the village. The Tang Monk and his companions were at the old man's house again. Once again they thanked the man and followed the road up the hill.

  At the top they looked at the mountain of flames. Wukong looked at the fan and waved it. Whoosh! Instantly the fire went out.

## ⑪

  The air cooled down, and Wukong saw the valley start to turn green. Leaves grew on trees and small flowers popped up here and there. Birds sang in the branches as other animals ran through the grass.

  Wukong smiled. He had done evil things a long time ago. Now he was fixing them.

# 🇨🇳 第七十九章：火焰山

## ①

  火焰山。悟空怒火中烧。

> "牛魔王骗了我！"

  猴子冲回洞中，砸碎了大门。轰隆！牛魔王从外面跑了出来。

> "你这恶棍！"他大叫道，"你毁了我的门！"

## ②

> "把扇子还给我！"悟空吼道，"不然我就把你的山全毁了！"

> "休想！"妖怪说。

  悟空向妖怪冲去。嚯！妖怪变成一只鹤飞走了。

## ③

  他会变身，我也会，猴子心想。

  悟空变成一只凤凰，朝着鹤飞去。他伸出利爪，向鹤猛扑过去。

  鹤俯冲而下，悟空扑了个空。当悟空回头时，鹤已经不见了。

## ④

  他去哪儿了？悟空心想。

  他低头看去，看到一只羚羊在田野里吃草。

  是他！

  悟空落到地上，变成一只老虎。他悄悄穿过高高的草丛。当他接近羚羊时，他纵身一跃。

## ⑤

  光芒一闪，羚羊变成了一头巨大的白牛。白牛飞上天空，悟空紧随其后。

  他差点就追上白牛，忽然听到一声喊。

> "退后，悟空！"

  是天兵，玉帝派他来助阵。

## ⑥

  更多天兵冲向白牛。白牛哼了一声，转身朝另一个方向飞去。但其他天兵出现，挡住了去路。

  悟空看着天兵包围了白牛。他们越靠越近，刀剑长矛全部指向白牛。

  牛魔王恢复了妖怪的真身。

## ⑦

> "请不要伤害我丈夫！"铁扇公主哭着冲向天兵。"他不是存心作恶的。"

  一个天兵放下了剑。

> "如果你们真心悔改，我们不会伤害你们。但现在你们阻断了唐僧西行的道路。"

> "悟空要借你的扇子。"

  铁扇公主把扇子交给了天兵。

## ⑧

> "扇子在这里，"她说，"我们真心知错了。我和丈夫从今以后一定做个好佛门弟子。请不要伤害我们！"

  天兵看着牛魔王。牛魔王点了点头。

> "我们发誓。"

> "很好，"天兵说，"你们可以回洞去了。但从今以后不得再惹事生非。"

## ⑨

  妖怪和妻子鞠躬离去。天兵把扇子交给了悟空。

> "你能来扑灭这山火，真是太好了，悟空。"

  悟空很困惑。

> "为什么这么说？"

> "火是你引起的，"天兵说，"当年太上老君把你扔进炼丹炉里烧你。你跳出丹炉时，弄翻了炉子里的火。燃烧的余烬掉落人间，点燃了这座山。"

## ⑩

  悟空看着手中的扇子。

> "我很乐意去灭火。"

  悟空回到村子。唐僧和同伴们正在老人家。他们再次谢过老人，沿着山路前行。

  到了山顶，他们望着火焰山。悟空看了一眼扇子，挥了一挥。呼！大火瞬间熄灭。

## ⑪

  空气凉爽下来，悟空看到山谷开始变得翠绿。树上长出绿叶，到处开着小花。鸟儿在枝头歌唱，动物在草地上奔跑。

  悟空微笑了。他很久以前做过恶事，现在他在弥补。
