
# Journey to the West · Chapter Ninety-Six: Three Fights

## ①

  Wukong and his companions walked on the mountain road. The monkey watched carefully for monsters and demons. Suddenly a demon charged toward the group.

> "Stay here, everyone!" shouted Bajie. "I'll fight this demon!"

  The pig spurred his steed onward. The demon stepped off the road and headed down the slope of the mountain. Bajie raced after the demon and was soon out of sight.

## ②

  Wukong heard grunts and clashing weapons, but he couldn't see the fight. A moment later, the demon climbed back onto the road.

> "Stay here, Wujing!" shouted Wukong. "Protect the Tang monk!"

  The monkey charged toward the demon. Their weapons clashed. Wukong fought with all his strength.

## ③

  Again, the demon left the road and headed down the mountain. Wukong chased him. The monkey and the demon disappeared down the slope of the mountain.

> "Do you think Wukong can defeat that demon?" asked the Tang monk nervously.

> "Definitely," said Wujing. "I wonder what happened to Bajie, though. I hope he's okay."

## ④

  Suddenly, the demon was back. Wujing was shocked.

> "How did you get past Wukong?"

  Without waiting for an answer, Wujing raised his spear and attacked the demon. The two fought, kicking up dust and rocks. Wujing struck the demon across the road. The demon tumbled down the mountainside. Wujing ran after him.

## ⑤

  The Tang monk stood alone in the road. For a moment, everything was quiet. Then, he heard footsteps behind him. The monk turned around and fell to the ground. A huge, fat monster was smiling wickedly at him.

  When Wukong returned to the road, he found Bajie alone. The pig looked upset.

> "Where are Master and Wujing?" asked the monkey.

  Bajie scratched his head.

## ⑥

> "I don't know. I defeated that demon and then came back here. Everyone was gone."

> "You didn't defeat the demon," said Wukong. "He came back here, and I had to fight him."

> "What?" shouted Bajie. "You're crazy. I was fighting with the demon until just a few moments ago."

  Wukong scoffed.

> "Do you really expect me to believe that?"

## ⑦

  Wukong returned to the road, annoyed.

> "How did that demon get past you, too? He wasn't that powerful?"

  Wukong was confused.

> "The demon didn't get past me. I was fighting with him until just a moment ago."

> "That's impossible," said Wukong. "After you chased him down the slope, he returned. Where's Master?"

## ⑧

  Wukong made a guess.

  How could we be so stupid? There wasn't just one demon. There were three. They lured us away one by one. Then another demon, probably that fat monster, grabbed Master while we were gone.

> "You're right!" said Bajie.

## ⑨

> "That monster must have a cave nearby," said Wujing. "Let's find it."

  The three companions searched the mountain. They soon found a cave. Its door was shut.

> "Do you think this is the monster's cave?" asked Wujing.

> "Let's find out," said Bajie.

  He raised his rake and knocked a hole in the door. Then he shouted into the hole.

## ⑩

> "Release the Tang monk!"

  A demon inside the cave shrieked and ran into a kitchen. The monster was grinding spices with a mortar and pestle.

> "That pig is here!" cried the demon.

  The monster kept grinding.

> "Who cares? I'm only afraid of Wukong."

## ⑪

> "I'm here too!" shouted Wukong through the hole.

  The monster dropped the mortar and pestle.

> "Wukong!"

  He froze for a moment. Then he cleared his throat and shouted.

> "You're too late! We already ate the Tang monk!"

# 🇨🇳 第九十六章：三场战斗

## ①

  悟空和同伴们走在山路上。猴子警惕地观察着妖怪的踪迹。突然一个妖怪朝队伍冲来。

> "大家别动！"八戒大喊，"我去对付这个妖怪！"

  妖怪下了路，朝山坡下跑去。八戒追了上去，很快就看不见了。

## ②

  悟空听到了打斗声和兵器相撞的声音，但看不见战斗的场面。过了一会儿，妖怪又回到了路上。

> "悟净，你在这里守着！"悟空大喊，"保护师父！"

  猴子朝妖怪冲去，两人兵器相撞。悟空使出全力战斗。

## ③

  妖怪又一次离开了大路，往山下跑去。悟空追了上去。猴子和妖怪一起消失在山坡下面。

> "悟空能打败那个妖怪吗？"唐僧不安地问。

> "肯定能，"悟净说，"不过不知道八戒怎么样了，希望他没事。"

## ④

  突然，妖怪又出现了。悟净大吃一惊。

> "他怎么躲过悟空的？"

  悟净不等回答，举起长矛就朝妖怪攻去。两人打得天昏地暗，尘土飞扬。悟净把妖怪打翻在路上，妖怪滚下了山坡。悟净追了上去。

## ⑤

  唐僧孤零零地站在路上。安静了一会儿，然后他听到身后传来脚步声。唐僧转过身，吓得跌倒在地。一个又高又胖的怪物正冲他邪恶地笑着。

  悟空回到路上时，发现只有八戒一个人。猪看起来很沮丧。

> "师父和悟净呢？"猴子问。

  八戒挠了挠头。

## ⑥

> "我不知道。我打败了那个妖怪就回来了，大家都不见了。"

> "你根本没打败妖怪，"悟空说，"他又回来了，我还跟他打了。"

> "什么？"八戒大喊，"你胡说，我刚才还在跟妖怪打呢。"

  悟空哼了一声。

> "你当真以为我会信？"

## ⑦

  悟空心烦意乱地走回路上。

> "怎么那妖怪也躲过你了？他没那么厉害啊？"

  悟空很困惑。

> "妖怪没躲过我，我刚才还在跟他打呢。"

> "这不可能，"悟空说，"你追下山以后，他又回来了。师父在哪？"

## ⑧

  悟空猜到了。

  我们怎么这么蠢？那不是只有一个妖怪，是三个！他们一个一个把我们引开。趁我们不在的时候，另一个妖怪——八成就是那个胖怪物——把师父抓走了。

> "你说得对！"八戒说。

## ⑨

> "那怪物的洞府肯定在附近，"悟净说，"我们去找找。"

  三个同伴在山上搜寻，很快就找到了一个洞穴。洞门关着。

> "你觉得这是怪物的洞府吗？"悟净问。

> "看看就知道了，"八戒说。

  他举起钉耙在门上砸了一个洞，然后对着洞里大喊。

## ⑩

> "放了唐僧！"

  洞里的一个小妖怪尖叫着跑进了厨房。怪物正在用石臼研磨香料。

> "那个猪来了！"妖怪大叫。

  怪物继续研磨着。

> "谁怕他？我只怕悟空。"

## ⑪

> "我也在这儿呢！"悟空从洞里大喊。

  怪物吓得掉了石臼和杵。

> "悟空！"

  他愣了一下，然后清了清嗓子大喊。

> "太迟了！我们已经把唐僧吃了！"
