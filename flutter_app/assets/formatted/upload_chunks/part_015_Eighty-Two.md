
# Journey to the West · Chapter Eighty-Two: The Magic Cloth

## ①

  "Oh no!" thought Wukong. "Yellow Brows is coming!"

  The monkey jumped back to his feet and ran fast out of the monastery. When he got outside, everyone was waiting for him.

> "Where are the bags?" asked Wujing.

  The monkey looked at his empty hands. He slapped his forehead.

## ②

> "I must have dropped them."

  He heard footsteps approaching from inside the monastery.

> "We can't worry about the bags now," whispered Wukong. "Yellow Brows is coming! We have to go quickly!"

  He helped the Tang Monk up onto the horse. The group hurried down the road.

## ③

> "Run faster, Bajie!" hissed Wujing.

  Bajie breathed heavily.

> "I... don't like running!"

  They hadn't gotten far before Yellow Brows flew over them.

## ④

> "You can't escape from me!" shouted the demon. "And you will not be going to the West. I am a true Buddhist, and I will be the one to get the true scriptures!"

  Wukong, Bajie and Wujing pulled out their weapons. The gold dragon bared its claws. Yellow Brows scoffed.

## ⑤

> "Do you really think you can fight me?"

  He reached into his robe and pulled out the cloth. Bajie lowered his rake and laughed.

> "Ha! We have powerful weapons, and all you have is a piece of cloth!"

> "That's not an ordinary cloth," warned the dragon.

  Yellow Brows threw the cloth towards the group.

## ⑥

> "Look out!" cried Wukong.

  The monkey leaped up to the side just before the cloth wrapped around his companions. Quickly, the demon grabbed the cloth with the group in it. He ran back into his monastery and slammed the door shut.

  Wukong sat down on a cloud to think.

## ⑦

  How could he rescue his companions?

  I can't ask the Jade Emperor for help, thought Wukong. He already sent the gold dragon, who was just captured again. Maybe I can ask someone else for help.

  As Wukong was wondering what to do, two spirits approached him. One was a turtle and the other was a serpent.

## ⑧

  The two spirits each carried a sword and a shield.

> "We're from Wu-Dang Mountain," said the turtle. "We've come to help you."

  The serpent nodded.

> "We heard the Tang Monk was captured by a demon called Yellow Brows."

> "Thank you for coming," said Wukong. He quickly told them what had happened.

## ⑨

> "Let's go to the monastery now," said the turtle. "Yellow Brows won't be able to fight all three of us."

> "We are much too powerful for him," added the serpent.

  Wukong led the two spirits down to the monastery.

## ⑩

> "Just be careful of his cloth," warned the monkey. "He'll try to capture us with it."

  Wukong banged on the monastery door.

> "Release my friends right now, Yellow Brows! If you don't, I'll knock down your monastery. Then I'll smash your entire mountain!"

  The door flew open. Yellow Brows stepped out and threw the cloth.

## ⑪

> "Look out!" cried Wukong.

  The monkey jumped out of the way, but the turtle and serpent weren't fast enough. The cloth wrapped around them. Laughing, the demon grabbed the bundle and went back into the monastery.

  Wukong flew back up into the sky. He sat on a cloud and moaned.

## ⑫

> "Now what will I do?"

  He was still thinking when he heard a voice.

> "Hello, Wukong."

  Wukong looked toward the voice and saw a man with a big belly. The man was smiling.

## ⑬

> "Do you know who I am?" asked the man.

  Wukong smiled.

> "You're the Buddha, Maitreya!"

> "Yes," said Maitreya. "And I've come to help you."

# 🇨🇳 第八十二章：魔布

## ①

  "坏了！"悟空心想，"黄眉来了！"

  猴子一跃而起，飞快逃出寺院。到了外面，大家都在等他。

> "行李呢？"悟净问。

  猴子看了看空空的双手，拍了拍额头。

## ②

> "我一定掉了。"

  他听到寺院内传来脚步声。

> "现在顾不上行李了，"悟空低声说，"黄眉来了！我们得赶快走！"

  他扶唐僧上了马，一行人匆匆上了路。

## ③

> "跑快点，八戒！"悟净催促道。

  八戒喘着粗气。

> "我……不喜欢跑步！"

  他们还没跑出多远，黄眉就从头顶飞了过来。

## ④

> "你们逃不出我的手掌心！"妖怪大叫，"你们也别想去西天了。我才是真正的佛门弟子，取经的非我莫属！"

  悟空、八戒和悟净掏出兵器。金龙亮出利爪。黄眉冷笑道。

## ⑤

> "你们真以为能打得过我？"

  他从袍子里掏出了那块布。八戒放下钉耙，哈哈大笑。

> "哈！我们有厉害的兵器，你只有一块破布！"

> "那可不是普通的布，"龙警告道。

  黄眉将布朝众人扔去。

## ⑥

> "小心！"悟空大叫。

  猴子向旁边一跃，布裹住了他的同伴。妖怪一把抓起布连带里面的人，跑回寺院，砰地关上了大门。

  悟空坐在云上思索对策。

## ⑦

  怎样才能救出同伴呢？

  我不能找玉帝帮忙，悟空心想。他已经派了金龙来，结果也被抓了。也许我可以找别人帮忙。

  悟空正在发愁，两个精灵朝他走来。一个是乌龟，一个是蛇精。

## ⑧

  两个精灵各持一面盾牌和一把剑。

> "我们来自武当山，"乌龟说，"我们来帮你。"

  蛇精点了点头。

> "我们听说唐僧被一个叫黄眉的妖怪抓了。"

> "谢谢你们前来相助，"悟空说，他迅速把事情经过告诉他们。

## ⑨

> "我们现在就去寺院吧，"乌龟说，"黄眉打不过我们三个。"

> "我们远比他厉害，"蛇精补充道。

  悟空领着两个精灵来到寺院。

## ⑩

> "小心他的布，"猴子提醒道，"他会用它来抓我们。"

  悟空猛敲寺院大门。

> "快放了我的朋友，黄眉！不然我就砸了你的寺院，再踏平你的整座山！"

  大门飞开。黄眉走出来，扔出了那块布。

## ⑪

> "小心！"悟空大叫。

  猴子闪身躲开，但乌龟和蛇精不够快。布把他们裹了起来。妖怪大笑着抓起包裹回到了寺院。

  悟空飞回天上。他坐在云上唉声叹气。

## ⑫

> "现在我该怎么办？"

  正在发愁时，他听到一个声音。

> "你好，悟空。"

  悟空循声望去，看见一个大肚子的男人，正微笑着。

## ⑬

> "你知道我是谁吗？"那人问。

  悟空笑了。

> "你是弥勒佛！"

> "不错，"弥勒佛说，"我来帮你。"
