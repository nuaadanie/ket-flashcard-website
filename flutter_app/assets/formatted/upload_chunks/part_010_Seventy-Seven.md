
# Journey to the West · Chapter Seventy-Seven: The Magic Fan

## ①

  For over a day, the wind from the fan carried Wukong. When he finally landed, he was far from the Iron Immortal's mountain. Wukong looked around and saw a mountain nearby. It looked familiar.

> "Mount Sumeru," said Wukong. "The Bodhisattva Lingji lives there. Maybe he can help me deal with the Iron Immortal. He was able to stop the Great King Yellow Wind's wind magic."

  The monkey went to Lingji's pagoda. The Bodhisattva was sitting in his garden.

## ②

> "Greetings, Wukong," said the Bodhisattva.

> "Hello, Lingji," said Wukong. "It's good to see you again. I was just about to have tea."

  Wukong and Lingji sat outside drinking tea. The monkey told the Bodhisattva everything that had happened with the Iron Immortal.

## ③

  The Bodhisattva handed a small stone to Wukong.

> "Keep this magic stone in your pocket. It will stop the wind from blowing you away."

> "Thank you, Bodhisattva," said the monkey.

> "Would you like to stay for lunch?" asked Lingji.

> "No, thank you," said Wukong. "I must get back to my companions. I need to get that fan from the Iron Immortal so we can continue west."

## ④

  Wukong returned to the Iron Immortal's cave door.

> "Open up!" he shouted. "I need your fan."

  The Iron Immortal came out and frowned.

> "I thought I got rid of you."

  She raised her fan.

> "I'll just have to wave harder this time."

  The woman waved the fan furiously. A terrible wind blew past Wukong, but the monkey didn't move.

## ⑤

  The Iron Immortal's eyes burned with anger.

> "The Bodhisattva Lingji gave me a magic stone. Your fan can't blow me away now."

  The monkey pulled out his iron bar.

> "Now give me that fan."

  The Iron Immortal turned and ran back into her cave. Before she could shut the door, Wukong turned into a cricket and slipped into the cave.

## ⑥

  Silently he followed the Iron Immortal along a tunnel. When the woman reached the room, she sat down. She tucked the fan into her robe.

> "Wukong is a horrible monkey," she said to herself. "He forced my poor baby Red Boy to become good. Then he demanded to borrow my precious fan."

## ⑦

  The Iron Immortal reached for a cup of water. As she took a drink, Wukong leaped into the cup.

  Inside the Iron Immortal's stomach, Wukong changed back into himself and pushed and kicked. The Iron Immortal fell to her knees.

> "What was in that water? I feel awful."

> "It was me," shouted Wukong. "I'm in your stomach now. And I won't stop punching and kicking until you give me that fan."

## ⑧

> "Please stop!" cried the Iron Immortal. "You can have the fan. Just don't hurt me anymore."

  Wukong leaped out of the woman's nose and returned to his normal size.

> "Give me the fan," he demanded.

## ⑨

  The Iron Immortal climbed back to her feet. She reached into her robe and pulled out a fan.

> "Here. Take it."

  Wukong found his companions in the village with the old man. The monkey explained how he tricked the Iron Immortal.

> "Wonderful," said the Tang monk. "Now we can continue our journey."

  They all thanked the old man and then headed west.

## ⑩

  They came to the top of the hill and saw the Mountain of Flames. Fire covered all the land around it.

> "Watch this," said Wukong.

  The monkey waved the fan hard. A strong wind blew toward the mountain.

  Wukong smiled. But in a moment, suddenly there was a roar and the flames grew bigger, filling the sky.

# 🇨🇳 第七十七章：芭蕉扇

## ①

  铁扇仙的扇子把悟空吹了一整天。等他终于落地时，已经到了离铁扇仙的山很远的地方。悟空环顾四周，看到附近有一座熟悉的山。

> "须弥山，"悟空说，"灵吉菩萨住在这里。也许他能帮我收拾铁扇仙，当初他就是用定力降服了黄风怪的风魔大法的。"

  猴子来到灵吉的宝塔前。菩萨正坐在花园中。

## ②

> "悟空，你好。"菩萨说。

> "灵吉菩萨，又见面了。"悟空说，"好巧，我刚想找你有事。"

  悟空和灵吉坐在外面喝茶。猴子把在铁扇仙那里的遭遇全说了一遍。

## ③

  菩萨递给悟空一颗小石子。

> "把这颗定风丹放在口袋里，她的扇子就吹不动你了。"

> "谢谢菩萨！"悟空道谢。

> "留下来吃午饭吗？"灵吉问。

> "不了，我得回去找同伴。我得从铁扇仙那里拿到扇子，才能继续往西走。"

## ④

  悟空回到铁扇仙的洞门前。

> "开门！"他大喊，"我要借你的扇子！"

  铁扇仙走出来了，眉头紧皱。

> "我以为再也见不到你了。"

  她举起扇子。

> "这次我得用力扇了。"

  女人用力扇动，一阵狂风掠过悟空面前，可猴子纹丝不动。

## ⑤

  铁扇仙怒火中烧。

> "灵吉菩萨给了我定风丹。你的扇子再也不能把我吹跑了。"

  猴子掏出铁棒。

> "快把扇子交出来！"

  铁扇仙转身就往洞里逃，还没关上门悟空就变成一只蟋蟀溜了进去。

## ⑥

  他悄悄跟着铁扇仙穿过隧道到了房间。女人坐下后把扇子藏进了衣袍里。

> "这猴子真是该死，"她自言自语道，"先把我可怜的红孩儿变成了观音的弟子，现在又来抢我的宝贝芭蕉扇。"

## ⑦

  铁扇仙端起一杯水来喝。趁她喝水的当口，悟空跳进了杯中。

  在铁扇仙的肚子里，悟空现出原形，又推又踢。铁扇仙扑通跪倒在地。

> "我喝的是什么？难受死了！"

> "是我！"悟空在里面大喊，"我在你肚子里呢。你不把扇子给我，我就一直踢个不停。"

## ⑧

> "求求你住手吧！"铁扇仙哭喊道，"扇子给你，别再折磨我了！"

  悟空从女人鼻孔里飞了出来，恢复原形。

> "把扇子给我。"他命令道。

## ⑨

  铁扇仙颤巍巍地站起来，从衣袍里掏出了一把扇子。

> "拿去，拿去吧。"

  悟空回到村里找到了同伴们，跟大家说了自己如何智取铁扇仙。

> "太好了，"唐僧说，"这下我们可以继续赶路了。"

  他们谢过老人，向西出发。

## ⑩

  他们来到小山顶上，看到了火焰山。烈火覆盖了四周所有的土地。

> "看好了，"悟空说。

  猴子用力扇动芭蕉扇。一阵猛烈的风朝火焰山吹去。

  悟空得意地笑了。谁知突然一声巨响，火焰非但没有熄灭，反而越烧越大，直冲云霄。
