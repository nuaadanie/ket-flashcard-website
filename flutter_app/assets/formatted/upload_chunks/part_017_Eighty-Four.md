
# Journey to the West · Chapter Eighty-Four: The Sad King

## ①

> "I wish I had money," Bajie moaned. "All this food looks so good!"

  The travelers were walking through a city in the Scarlet Kingdom. The streets were lined with people selling food and other goods.

> "Stop thinking about food, Bajie," said Wujing. "What's going on over there?"

  He pointed to a crowd of people reading a sign on a wall.

## ②

  The travelers walked over to the crowd. The sign read: "The King of the Scarlet Kingdom is seeking help. Anyone who can defeat demons must report to the royal palace."

  The Tang Monk sighed.

> "I don't want to delay our journey. But you three can defeat demons. We must report to the palace."

> "Don't worry, Master," said Wukong. "I'm great at defeating demons. This won't take long."

## ③

> "I hope there's food at the palace," said Bajie. "I'm starving."

  The travelers arrived at the palace gates. They spoke with a guard who then led them into the court. The king sat on his throne, looking very sad.

> "Your Majesty," said the guard, "the Tang Monk is here to see you. He says his companions can defeat demons."

  The king looked up but didn't say anything.

## ④

  The guard whispered to the travelers.

> "His Majesty is very upset. If you can help him, please do."

  Wukong walked up to the king.

> "Greetings, Your Majesty. I am Sun Wukong, King of the Fruit and Flower Mountain. You need someone who can defeat demons. Well, here I am."

## ⑤

  Suddenly the king covered his face with his hands and burst out crying.

> "Oh, it's terrible," cried the king. "I have no hope anymore."

  Wukong scratched his head.

> "What's wrong?"

> "The Queen, my wife," cried the king. "I'll never see her again!"

## ⑥

  Wukong frowned.

> "Your Majesty, stop crying."

> "Wukong," hissed the Tang Monk. "Show some respect."

  Wukong calmed himself and looked at the king.

> "I realize you're upset, but if you want my help, you must stay calm and explain everything to me."

## ⑦

  The king sniffed and sat up straight. He pulled out a handkerchief and blew his nose.

> "I will tell you what happened. Three years ago, the Demon of Power attacked the kingdom. The demon kidnapped the Queen. I sent soldiers to fight the demon and bring back my love. But the demon was too powerful."

> "Where is the demon?" asked Wukong.

## ⑧

  The king got up and walked to a window. He pointed to a mountain in the distance.

> "The demon lives on that mountain."

  Wukong slapped the king on the back.

> "You can stop being sad now, Your Majesty. I will rescue the Queen. In the meantime, please take care of my companions. I think Bajie is hungry."

## ⑨

  Wukong landed on the demon's mountain. Several small demons were standing outside a cave entrance.

> "Return the Queen, or I'll destroy all of you!" shouted the monkey.

  The small demons cried and ran into the cave.

  Wukong heard another voice inside the cave.

## ⑩

> "What's going on?" it said.

> "Master!" cried one demon. "There's a monkey outside. We must return the Queen, or he'll destroy all of us!"

  A large demon with broad shoulders came out of the cave. She wore three bells.

> "I am the Demon of Power!" shouted the demon. "Get off of my mountain!"

## ⑪

  Wukong was furious.

> "Do you know who I am? I'm the Great—"

  Whoosh! A blast shot all over. Smoke filled the air and blinded poor Wukong. The monkey coughed as sand got in his eyes and nose.

> "This is awful," cried Wukong.

  He covered his face and leapt off the mountain.

# 🇨🇳 第八十四章：悲伤的国王

## ①

> "我要是有钱就好了，"八戒哼哼道，"这些食物看起来真好吃！"

  旅行者们正在朱紫国的城中行走。街道两旁都是卖食物和各种货物的人。

> "别光想着吃的了，八戒，"悟净说，"那边是怎么回事？"

  他指着墙边的一群人。

## ②

  一行人走上前去。告示上写着："朱紫国国王求助。能降妖者速来王宫。"

  唐僧叹了口气。

> "我不想耽误行程，但你们三个能降妖。我们必须去王宫看看。"

> "别担心，师父，"悟空说，"降妖是我的拿手好戏，花不了多少时间。"

## ③

> "希望王宫里有吃的，"八戒说，"我饿坏了。"

  一行人来到王宫门前。他们和卫兵说了几句话，卫兵领他们进了大殿。国王坐在王座上，神情十分悲伤。

> "陛下，"卫兵说，"唐僧求见。他说他的徒弟能降妖除魔。"

  国王抬起头，但没有说话。

## ④

  卫兵低声对旅行者们说。

> "陛下非常伤心。如果你们能帮上忙，请一定帮忙。"

  悟空走到国王面前。

> "陛下您好。我是花果山美猴王孙悟空。您需要有人降妖，我不期而至。"

## ⑤

  国王突然双手捂面，放声大哭。

> "太惨了，"国王哭着说，"我已经没有希望了。"

  悟空挠了挠头。

> "怎么了？"

> "王后，我的妻子，"国王哭着说，"我再也见不到她了！"

## ⑥

  悟空皱起了眉头。

> "陛下，别哭了。"

> "悟空，"唐僧低声喝道，"注意礼节。"

  悟空定了定神，看着国王。

> "我知道您难过，但如果要让我帮忙，您必须冷静下来，把事情的来龙去脉说清楚。"

## ⑦

  国王抽了抽鼻子，坐直了身子。他掏出手帕擦了擦鼻涕。

> "我告诉你怎么回事。三年前，赛太岁妖怪来犯，掳走了王后。我派士兵去救她，但妖怪太厉害了。"

> "妖怪住在哪里？"悟空问。

## ⑧

  国王起身走到窗前，指着远处的一座山。

> "妖怪就住在那座山上。"

  悟空拍了拍国王的后背。

> "陛下别难过了，我去把王后救回来。这期间请照看好我的同伴们。八戒肯定饿了。"

## ⑨

  悟空降落在妖怪的山上。几个小妖怪正站在洞口。

> "交出王后，不然我把你们全部消灭！"猴子大喊。

  小妖怪们吓得哭喊着跑进洞里。

  悟空听到里面传来另一个声音。

## ⑩

> "怎么回事？"那声音说。

> "大王！"一个小妖怪叫道，"外面来了个猴子。我们得把王后还给他，不然他要毁了我们的！"

  一个膀大腰圆的大妖怪从洞里走了出来。妖怪脖子上挂着三个铃铛。

> "我是赛太岁！"妖怪大叫，"滚出我的山！"

## ⑪

  悟空怒火中烧。

> "你知道我是谁吗？我是齐天大圣——"

  呼！一股黄沙扑面而来，烟雾弥漫，悟空睁不开眼睛。猴子咳嗽不止，沙土灌进他的眼睛和鼻子。

> "太可恶了！"悟空大喊。

  他捂住脸，跳下了山。
