
# Journey to the West · Chapter Seventy: The Star Lord

## ①

  Guanyin shook her head.

> "You cannot go back to that cave," said Guanyin.

> "But we must go back," said Wukong. "That woman kidnapped the Tang monk."

> "That woman is a scorpion spirit. She is very dangerous."

## ②

> "She sure is!" cried Bajie. "What did she stab us with, anyway?"

> "It was her stinger that stabbed you," said Guanyin.

  Wukong thought about the pain he'd felt.

> "I won't go near her."

## ③

> "Even Buddha is afraid of her," said Guanyin. "He was teaching his students one day and saw that scorpion creeping toward him. He tried to move it away, but it stung him. The pain was horrible. He told his guards to capture the scorpion, but it escaped to Earth."

## ④

  Wukong paced anxiously.

> "What are we going to do, then?"

> "We must save the Tang monk. Go to Heaven and ask the Star Lord for help," said Guanyin.

  Guanyin disappeared in a flash of light.

## ⑤

  A little while later, Wukong stood at the southern gate of Heaven.

> "Greetings, Wukong," said the guard.

> "I must speak with the Star Lord," said the monkey.

  The guard told Wukong the way to the Star Lord's palace. A few minutes later, the monkey knocked on the palace door.

## ⑥

> "Wukong," said the Star Lord. "What can I do for you?"

> "I need your help," said Wukong. "I'm traveling to the Western Paradise with the Tang monk, but he was captured by a demon. Guanyin said you can help me."

  The Star Lord looked confused.

> "I don't know why you need my help. I've heard you're quite strong. What kind of demon is it?"

## ⑦

> "It's a scorpion spirit," said the monkey.

> "Ah," said the Star Lord. "I understand now. Lead the way. We must hurry."

## ⑧

  Wukong led the Star Lord out of Heaven and down to Earth. They landed on the mountain where Bajie was still rubbing his nose.

> "The Star Lord is here to help us," said Wukong.

  The Star Lord looked at Bajie's nose.

> "I can heal that for you."

  Bajie walked over to the Star Lord, who then blew on the pig's nose. The injury healed instantly.

## ⑨

> "Thank you," said the pig, smiling.

> "I was stabbed too," said Wukong. "But the stinger didn't leave a mark on me." He grinned at Bajie. "I'm much more powerful than the pig."

  Bajie frowned.

## ⑩

> "I have special magic that I'll use to defeat the Scorpion," said the Star Lord. "You just need to get her to come outside."

  Everyone went up to the cave. Bajie and Wujing hid behind some rocks. Wukong stood in front of the cave entrance and shouted.

> "Come out, demon! Release my master!"

  Then he ran as fast as he could away from the cave.

## ⑪

  The woman rushed out of the cave, spinning wildly. Suddenly the Star Lord turned into an enormous rooster. The rooster crowed, and the sound echoed through the mountains. The woman screamed and held her hands over her ears. The rooster crowed again, and the woman disappeared in a puff of smoke. In her place was a dead scorpion.

## ⑫

  Wukong ran into the cave. He found the Tang monk sitting at a table. The monk's eyes lit up.

> "Wukong! Thank you for saving me!"

  The monkey smiled.

> "As long as I'm protecting you, no demon will ever harm you."

## ⑬

  The Tang monk shook his head.

> "That woman didn't want to harm me. She wanted to marry me."

  The monk sighed.

> "But I told her that I'm a Buddhist monk and I will never marry. My only goal is to reach the Western Paradise."

# 🇨🇳 第七十章：昴日星官

## ①

  观音摇了摇头。

> "你们不能再回那个洞去了，"观音说。

> "可是我们必须回去，"悟空说，"那女人掳走了唐僧。"

> "那女人是蝎子精，非常危险。"

## ②

> "她确实危险！"八戒叫道，"她到底用什么刺我们的？"

> "是她的尾刺刺了你们，"观音说。

  悟空想起那阵疼痛，心有余悸。

> "我再也不靠近她了。"

## ③

> "连佛祖都怕她，"观音说，"有一天佛祖正在讲经，看见这只蝎子悄悄向他爬来。他想把她赶走，却被她的尾刺蛰了一下。那疼痛难以忍受。他叫护法金刚把她捉住，但她却逃到了人间。"

## ④

  悟空焦急地来回踱步。

> "那我们怎么办？我们得救唐僧啊。"

> "去天上请昴日星官帮忙。"观音说。

  观音说完便在一道金光中消失了。

## ⑤

  过了一会儿，悟空来到了南天门外。

> "大圣，你好。"守卫说。

> "我要见昴日星官。"悟空说。

  守卫给悟空指了去昴日星官宫殿的路。几分钟后，悟空敲响了宫殿的门。

## ⑥

> "悟空，"昴日星官说，"你有什么事？"

> "我需要你的帮助，"悟空说，"我和唐僧正在去西天取经的路上，但他被一个妖怪抓走了。观音菩萨说你能帮我。"

  昴日星官一脸困惑。

> "我不明白你为什么需要我的帮助。听说你很厉害，是什么妖怪？"

## ⑦

> "是一只蝎子精。"悟空说。

> "噢，我明白了，"昴日星官说，"带路吧，我们得赶快。"

## ⑧

  悟空领着昴日星官离开天庭来到人间。他们降落在山上，八戒正在那里揉鼻子。

> "昴日星官来帮我们了。"悟空说。

  昴日星官看了看八戒的鼻子。

> "我来帮你治好。"

  八戒走到昴日星官面前，星官朝他的鼻子吹了一口气，伤口瞬间就愈合了。

## ⑨

> "谢谢！"八戒笑着说。

> "我也被刺了，"悟空说，"不过尾刺没在我身上留下痕迹。"他朝八戒咧嘴一笑，"我可比这猪强多了。"

  八戒皱了皱眉。

## ⑩

> "我有一套特别的法术可以降服蝎子精，"昴日星官说，"你们只需要把她引出洞来。"

  大家一起上了山。八戒和悟净躲在石头后面，悟空站在洞口大喊。

> "蝎子精！快出来见我师父！"

  然后他飞快逃离洞口。

## ⑪

  那女人冲出洞来，疯狂地旋转着。突然之间，昴日星官变成了一只巨大的公鸡。公鸡高声啼鸣，声音在山间回荡。那女人惨叫一声，双手捂住耳朵。公鸡又啼叫了一声，那女人在"砰"的一声中化作了一团青烟。原地留下一只死了的蝎子。

## ⑫

  悟空跑进洞里，发现唐僧正坐在桌旁。唐僧眼睛一亮。

> "悟空，谢谢你救我！"

  悟空笑道。

> "只要有我保护你，没有任何妖怪能伤害你。"

## ⑬

  唐僧摇了摇头。

> "那女人并非想害我，她想跟我成亲。"

  唐僧叹了口气。

> "但我告诉她我是出家人，绝不会娶亲。我唯一的目标就是到达西天取经。"
