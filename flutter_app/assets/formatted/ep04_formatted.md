# Journey to the West · Chapter Four: Secret Formulas

## ①

  That night, after the other students went to bed, Wukong stayed awake. At three o'clock in the morning, he crept quietly through the dark temple and entered Subhuti's room.
  > "Wukong, what are you doing here?" asked the sage.
  > "I understood your secret message," said Wukong. "You hit me on the head three times. That meant you wanted me to visit you at three o'clock. Now, will you teach me how to live forever?"
  Subhuti smiled faintly.
  > "I knew you would understand. Tell the other students to leave."
  Subhuti whispered into Wukong's ear. The sage whispered prayers and secret formulas about the universe and nature. As the monkey listened to his teacher's words, powerful light filled the room. Now Wukong knew how to live forever.

## ②

  For the next few years, Wukong meditated and practiced the secret formulas. While the other students slept at night, Wukong stayed up. Soon his mind and body became powerful; he could change shape, transform into other animals, and soar through the sky on clouds.

## ③

  One day, all the students were sitting outside.
  > "Wukong," said one student, "you've been busy lately."
  > "That's true," said another student, "what have you been doing?"
  > "I've been meditating and practicing secret formulas," answered Wukong.
  > "Secret formulas?" asked the first student.
  Everyone looked very interested and leaned in closer to Wukong.
  > "Yes," said the monkey. "Our master taught them to me."
  > "He did?" said a student. "You must be special. He doesn't teach those formulas to everyone. What did he teach you?"
  Wukong smiled.
  > "He taught me how to live forever. He also taught me how to change into other things."
  > "Show us what you can do," said the students.

## ④

  Wukong stood up. He muttered a spell and made a sign with his hands. Suddenly—POOF!—the monkey was gone. In his place stood an enormous pine tree.
  > "What do you think?" came a curious voice from the tree.
  The students clapped and cheered.
  > "That's amazing!" said one. "The monkey is truly powerful," said another.
  The noise disturbed Subhuti. He ran outside and saw the enormous pine tree.
  > "What's going on out here?" he shouted.
  Everyone stopped cheering. Wukong quickly turned back into himself. Subhuti's eyes flashed with anger.
  > "Everyone but Wukong, go inside!" he yelled.

## ⑤

  Subhuti waited for the other students to leave.
  > "I taught you the secret formulas so you could live forever," said the sage, "not so you could show off."
  > "I'm sorry," said Wukong.
  Subhuti continued.
  > "Now all the students have seen what you can do. They will want to know how to do it too. If you don't tell them, they might harm you. It's not safe for you to stay here any longer. You must go."
  Wukong started to cry.
  > "But Master, where will I go?"
  > "Go back to the Mountain of Flowers and Fruit," said Subhuti.
  > "But you taught me so much," said Wukong. "I must repay you first."
  > "You don't need to repay me," said Subhuti. "Just try to stay out of trouble."
  The sage looked at Wukong for a moment.
  > "I can see you will do evil things someday. Don't tell anyone you are my student. Now go."
  Wukong hung his head.
  > "Goodbye, Master."
  He leaped onto a cloud and left.

## ⑥

  After a short journey, Wukong arrived at the Mountain of Flowers and Fruit.
  > "I have returned!" he called.

# 🇨🇳 第四章：秘传法术

## ①

  那晚其他弟子睡下后，悟空仍醒着。凌晨三点，他悄悄潜入黑暗的寺庙，进入须菩提的房间。
  > "悟空，你在这里做什么？"仙人问道。
  > "我明白您的暗号，"悟空说，"您在我头上打了三下，意思是让我三点钟来找您。现在，您愿意教我长生不老之法吗？"
  须菩提微微一笑。
  > "我早知道你领会了。让这些弟子都离开。"
  须菩提凑近悟空耳边，传授关于宇宙与自然的咒语和秘法。当猴子聆听师父教导时，强大的光芒充满房间。此刻悟空已明了长生之道。

## ②

  接下来的几年里，悟空打坐修炼秘法。其他弟子夜间安睡时，悟空却总在修行。不久他的身心变得强大，能够变化形态，还可化身为各类动物，驾云在天空翱翔。

## ③

  一天，所有弟子正在户外坐着。
  > "悟空，"一名弟子说，"你最近很忙啊。"
  > "确实，"另一名弟子说，"你都在做什么？"
  > "我一直在打坐、修炼秘法，"悟空回答。
  > "秘法？"第一名弟子问。
  众人显得非常感兴趣，纷纷凑近悟空。
  > "是的，"猴子说，"师父把这些教给了我。"
  > "真的吗？"有弟子说，"你一定特别受宠。师父可不会把这些秘法教给任何人。他都教你什么了？"
  悟空微微一笑。
  > "他教我如何长生不老，还教我如何变化成其他事物。"
  > "给我们展示一下你的本领吧，"弟子们说。

## ④

  悟空站起身，念动咒语，双手结印。忽然——啪！猴子消失无踪，取而代之的是一棵高大的松树。
  > "怎么样？"树中传来好奇的声音。
  弟子们纷纷喝彩。
  > "太神奇了！"一名弟子说。
  > "这只猴子真是力大无穷，"另一名说道。
  喧闹声惊动了须菩提。他跑出来看到那棵巨大的松树。
  > "外面怎么回事？"他喝道。
  众人停止欢呼。悟空迅速变回原形。须菩提眼中怒火闪动。
  > "除了悟空，其他人统统进去！"他吼道。

## ⑤

  须菩提等其他弟子离开。
  > "我教你秘法是为了让你长生不老，"仙人说，"不是为了让你炫耀。"
  > "对不起，"悟空说道。
  须菩提继续说道。
  > "如今所有弟子都看到了你的本事。他们也会想知道如何做到。若你不告诉他们，他们可能会加害于你。你再留在这里不安全了。你必须离开。"
  悟空开始哭泣。
  > "可是师父，我该去哪里？"
  > "回你的花果山去吧，"须菩提说。
  > "可您教会了我这么多，"猴子说，"我必须先报答您。"
  > "你不必报答我，"须菩提道，"只要今后少惹麻烦就行。"
  仙人注视悟空片刻。
  > "我能看出你将来会做恶事。不要告诉任何人你是我的弟子。去吧。"
  悟空低下头。
  > "弟子告辞，师父。"
  他纵身跳上云朵，离去。

## ⑥

  经过一段短途飞行，悟空回到了花果山。
  > "我回来啦！"他高声喊道。
