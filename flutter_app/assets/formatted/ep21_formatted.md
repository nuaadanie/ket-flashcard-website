# Journey to the West · Chapter Twenty-One: The Monster's Friend

## ①

  Wukong stood before Guanyin. He was telling the Bodhisattva everything that had happened at the monastery.

  "Then a bear spirit called the Black Wind King stole the robe," the monkey went on. "I demanded he give it back. We fought until the bear spirit said it was lunchtime and hid in his cave. I kicked the door and yelled insults at him for more than an hour. But he wouldn't come out. You must help me."

## ②

  Guanyin sighed. "The Tang monk should not have shown that robe to the priest. The robe is a Buddhist treasure. When other people see it, they will want it. And as that head priest tried to do, they might harm the Tang monk."

  "I agree," said Wukong. "I've told the Tang monk many times not to do dumb things. But still he —"

  "And you should have put out that fire," Guanyin said, interrupting Wukong. "What you did was terrible."

## ③

  Wukong looked at the ground. He kicked a pebble. "I know," he mumbled.

  "I'll help you get back the robe," said Guanyin. "Let's go."

  Wukong and Guanyin soared over the ocean. Soon they landed on the mountain.

  "Where is the Black Wind King's cave?" asked Guanyin.

  "It's close," said Wukong. "We should sneak up to it."

## ④

  They heard footsteps coming closer. Wukong and Guanyin hid as a Taoist priest walked along the path. The priest was holding a tray. A bright purple pill sat on the tray.

  The priest was talking to himself. "This is an exciting day," he said. "The Black Wind King has a new robe that is a Buddhist treasure. And I hope he likes the pill I made for him. It will make him live forever."

## ⑤

  "Who comes here?" Wukong stared at the priest using his magic sight. "That's not a priest," he realized. "It's a wolf spirit dressed as a priest."

  The monkey's eyes twinkled. "I have an idea, Bodhisattva."

  A little while later, the priest stood before the Black Wind King's cave door. He knocked, and the door flew open.

  "Are you still here, little monkey?" shouted the Black Wind King. Then he saw the priest and smiled. "Oh, it's you, my old friend. Sorry for yelling. A pesky monkey spirit was here a little while ago."

## ⑥

  He looked around. "I guess he's gone."

  "A monkey spirit was here?" asked the priest.

  "Yes," said the bear. "He kept banging on my door and yelling at me. He was very rude." The Black Wind King patted the priest on the shoulder. "But let's not talk about him. It's good to see you. How have you been?"

  "I'm well," said the priest. "I brought you a gift to celebrate your new Buddhist treasure." He held up the tray with the pill.

## ⑦

  The Black Wind King looked interested. "What kind of pill is that?"

  "It is a very special pill," said the priest with a smile. "I made it just for you. It will make you live forever."

  The Black Wind King stroked his chin. "You know, I was just thinking this morning that I would like to live forever."

## ⑧

  The bear picked up the pill and opened his mouth wide. As the priest brought the pill up to his mouth, the pill wiggled.

  "Oh, did the pill just —"

  But before the Black Wind King could finish speaking, the pill shot out of his hand. It shot into his mouth and slid down his throat.

# 🇨🇳 第二十一章：妖怪的朋友


## ①

  悟空站在观音面前，把寺院里发生的一切都告诉了菩萨。

  "然后一个叫黑风大王的熊精偷走了袈裟，"悟空接着说，"我让他还回来，我们打了起来，后来那熊精说该吃午饭了，就躲进了洞里。我踢门骂了他一个多时辰，他就是不出来。你一定要帮帮我。"

## ②

  观音叹了口气，"唐僧不该把那件袈裟给那个僧人看。袈裟是佛门至宝，别人看到了就会动贪念。就像那个主僧一样，他们可能会伤害唐僧。"

  "我同意，"悟空说，"我已经跟唐僧说过很多次不要做傻事，可是他——"

  "还有那场火你也不该那样做，"观音打断了悟空，"你做的太过分了。"

## ③

  悟空低头看了看地面，踢开一块小石子，"我知道，"他咕哝道。

  "我帮你拿回袈裟，"观音说，"走吧。"

  悟空和观音飞越海面，很快落到山上。

  "黑风大王的洞在哪里？"观音问。

  "不远，"悟空说，"我们悄悄靠近。"

## ④

  他们听到脚步声越来越近。悟空和观音隐藏起来，一个道士沿着小路走来。道士手里端着一个托盘，托盘上放着一颗鲜亮的紫色丹药。

  道士自言自语地说："今天真让人兴奋。黑风大王得了一件佛门至宝袈裟。我希望他会喜欢我为他炼制的丹药。这丹药能让他长生不老。"

## ⑤

"谁来了？"悟空用他神通的双眼盯着道士，"那不是道士，"他心想，"是一个狼精假扮的道士。"

  悟空眼睛一亮，"菩萨，我有个主意。"

  过了一会儿，那道士来到黑风大王的洞门前。他敲了敲门，门一下子开了。

  "你这小猴子还在吗？"黑风大王喊道。然后他看到了道士，微微一笑，"哦，是你啊，老朋友。原谅我刚才的吆喝。有一只讨厌的猴子刚才来过。"

## ⑥

  他四处张望了一下，"我猜他走了。"

  "猴子来过？"道士问。

  "是啊，"熊说，"他一直敲门骂我，非常无礼。"黑风大王拍了拍道士的肩膀，"先不说他了。见到你真好。最近怎么样？"

  "一切都好，"道士说，"我给你带了件礼物，庆祝你得到那件佛门至宝。"他举起托盘上的丹药。

## ⑦

  黑风大王有了兴致，"这是什么丹药？"

  "这是一颗非常特别的丹药，"道士笑着说，"专为陛下炼制的。服下可以长生不老。"

  黑风大王摸了摸下巴，"你还别说，我今天早上正好在想，我要是能长生不老就好了。"

## ⑧

  熊拿起丹药，张大了嘴。道士把丹药送到他嘴边的那一刻，丹药突然扭动了一下。

  "咦，那丹药刚才是不是——"

  黑风大王话还没说完，丹药就从他手里弹了起来，射进嘴里，顺着喉咙滑了下去。
