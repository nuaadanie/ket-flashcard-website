# Journey to the West · Chapter Sixty-Seven: The Master of Faith

## ①

> "What are we going to do now?" cried Wukong. "One-Horned Buffalo stole all our weapons."

  The warrior rubbed his chin. He looked very serious.

> "We can't go back to the Jade Emperor. He'll be angry if he finds out we couldn't arrest the demon."

## ②

> "You're right," said the Fire God. "We need a different plan. It's getting dark out, almost nighttime. Maybe one of us can sneak into the cave and steal that metal ring."

## ③

  The Thunder God smiled.

> "I know who's perfect for that job."

  The others all looked at the god.

> "One of us is a master thief," the Thunder God continued. "He once stole all the peaches from the Royal Peach Garden."

  Now everyone looked at Wukong.

## ④

  The monkey pretended to laugh.

> "Ha ha ha. Yes, it's true. I used to steal things, but I know now that stealing is wrong. However, I will steal that metal ring because I need to save the Tang Monk. You all wait here."

## ⑤

  The monkey turned himself into a tiny bug and flew into the cave. He went down a long dark tunnel followed by another tunnel. Then he heard One-Horned Buffalo.

  Wukong flew toward the voice. The demon was sitting on the edge of a bed, talking to himself. The metal ring was still on his arm.

## ⑥

> "Ah, it's time for bed," said the demon with a yawn. "I hope I can eat the Tang Monk tomorrow. I couldn't eat him today because of that annoying monkey and his friends from Heaven."

  The demon lay down and was soon asleep.

## ⑦

  Wukong flew over and landed on the demon's arm.

> "I'll bite his arm," thought the monkey. "He'll get itchy and have to take off the metal ring."

  Wukong bit the demon's arm again and again.

  The demon rolled over and mumbled in his sleep. He scratched his arm.

  Wukong bit him some more. The demon scratched and scratched, but he didn't take off the metal ring.

## ⑧

> "This isn't working," thought Wukong.

  He was about to leave the room when he spotted something in the corner. He gasped. It was the weapons!

  Quietly, he changed back into himself. He made his iron bar small and put it in his ear. Then he grabbed the flaming spear, the thunderbolt, and the sword.

## ⑨

  Outside the cave, the others were waiting.

  The warrior made a fist.

> "I hope Wukong steals that metal ring. I'm so angry at that demon."

> "Shh," said the Fire God. "I can't believe he stole my fire spear."

## ⑩

  The Thunder God held up a hand.

> "The door is opening."

  The three of them held their breath. When they saw Wukong peek out, they were relieved.

> "Did you get the ring?" asked the Thunder God.

> "No, but look."

  The monkey held up the weapons.

## ⑪

> "Well done, Wukong," said the Fire God.

  The Thunder God cracked the monkey on the back.

> "Thank you, Wukong."

  The warrior held up his sword.

> "We're all armed. We can easily defeat the demon now."

> "You're right," said Wukong. He looked around. "Is everyone ready?"

> "Yes," said the warrior.

> "Yes," said the Fire God.

> "Yes," said the Thunder God.

## ⑫

  The monkey banged on the cave door.

> "Wake up, One-Horned Buffalo! Get out here right now or we'll destroy your entire mountain."

  The demon stepped outside yawning. Then he saw the weapons.

> "How dare you steal from me!"

  He held up the metal ring. Before anyone could react, the weapons were sucked into it, once again.

# 🇨🇳 第六十七章：偷天换日


## ①

> "我们现在怎么办？"悟空叫道，"独角牛魔王偷走了我们所有的兵器。"

  天将摸着下巴，表情十分严肃。

> "我们不能回去见玉帝了。让他知道我们没能捉住妖怪，他定会发怒。"

## ②

> "说得对，"火神说，"我们需要换个计划。天色渐渐暗下来了，马上就是晚上了。也许我们中间有哪个人能溜进山洞，把那个金属圈偷出来。"

## ③

  雷神微微一笑。

> "我知道谁最适合干这个差事。"

  大家都看向了雷神。

> "我们中间有一个神偷，"雷神继续说，"他曾把蟠桃园里的仙桃全都偷了。"

  众人齐刷刷地看向悟空。

## ④

  猴子装出一副大笑的样子。

> "哈哈哈，没错，我从前确实偷过东西，不过现在我知道偷东西是不对的。不过，为了救唐僧，我愿意去偷那个金属圈。你们都在这里等着。"

## ⑤

  猴子变成了一只小虫子，飞进了山洞。他穿过一条漫长的黑暗地道，接着又穿过另一条。然后他听到了独角牛魔王的声音。

  悟空朝声音的方向飞去。妖怪正坐在床边自言自语，金属圈仍然戴在他胳膊上。

## ⑥

> "啊，该睡觉了，"妖怪打着哈欠说，"希望明天能吃上唐僧肉。今天没吃成，都是那只讨厌的猴子和他那帮天庭的朋友碍事。"

  妖怪躺下来，很快就睡着了。

## ⑦

  悟空飞过去落在妖怪的胳膊上。

> "我就咬他的胳膊，"猴子心想，"他一定会觉得痒，不得不把金属圈取下来。"

  悟空咬了咬妖怪的胳膊，一口又一口。

  妖怪翻了个身，在睡梦中嘟囔着。他抓了抓胳膊。

  悟空又咬了几口。妖怪挠了又挠，可就是没有取下那个金属圈。

## ⑧

> "这招不管用，"悟空心想。

  他正准备离开房间，突然看到了角落里有什么东西。他倒吸一口凉气，是那些兵器！

  他悄悄地变回了自己原来的模样。把金箍棒变小放进耳朵里，然后一把抓起烈焰长矛、雷电之剑和宝剑。

## ⑨

  洞外，其他人在等着。

  天将握紧了拳头。

> "希望悟空能把那个金属圈偷出来，我对那个妖怪真是气极了。"

> "嘘，"火神说，"真不敢相信他偷走了我的火矛。"

## ⑩

  雷神举起一只手。

> "门开了。"

  三人都屏住了呼吸，看见悟空探出头来，都松了口气。

> "你拿到金属圈了吗？"雷神问。

> "没有，不过你们看。"

  猴子举起了那些兵器。

## ⑪

> "干得好，悟空，"火神说。

  雷神拍了一下猴子的后背。

> "谢谢你啊，悟空。"

  天将举起了自己的宝剑。

> "我们都有兵器了，现在可以轻松打败那个妖怪了。"

> "没错，"悟空说。他环顾四周，"大家都准备好了吗？"

> "准备好了，"天将说。

> "准备好了，"火神说。

> "准备好了，"雷神说。

## ⑫

  猴子用力敲打着洞门。

> "独角牛魔王，快滚出来！再不出来我们就毁了整座山。"

  妖怪打着哈欠走了出来。然后他看到了那些兵器。

> "竟敢偷我的东西！"

  他举起金属圈。大家还没来得及做出反应，所有的兵器再一次被吸进了圈里。
