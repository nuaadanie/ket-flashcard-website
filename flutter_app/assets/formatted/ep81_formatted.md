# Journey to the West · Chapter Eighty-One: Help From a Dragon

## ①

  Bajie and Wujing swung their weapons at the demon.

  Ah! The demon laughed.

> "I'm much too powerful for you!"

  From inside the cymbals, Wukong heard the fight. He heard grunts and the sounds of weapons clashing.

> "No!" the Tang Monk cried.

## ②

  Then there was silence.

> "What happened out there?" shouted Wukong.

  There was no answer. The monkey punched and kicked the inside of the cymbals for hours. But the two cymbals would not come apart.

> "This is terrible," he said to himself. "How will I get out of here?"

## ③

  The monkey was about to strike the cymbals again when he heard a voice.

> "Wukong, are you in there?"

> "Who is that?" Wukong asked.

> "I'm the gold dragon," said the voice. "The Jade Emperor sent me to help you. I'll wedge my horn between the cymbals and pry them apart."

## ④

> "Please try," said Wukong.

  Slowly the dragon's horn slipped through. But the cymbals were still tightly clasped together. Wukong groaned.

> "I can't escape."

> "Maybe we could find another way to pry the cymbals apart," said the dragon.

## ⑤

> "Actually, I have a different idea," said the monkey.

  Wukong turned his iron bar into a drill. The monkey drilled a hole in the dragon's horn. Then he made himself tiny and climbed into the hole.

> "Pull your horn out now!" said Wukong.

## ⑥

  The dragon pulled and pulled and finally got his horn out. The monkey jumped out of the hole and returned to his normal size.

> "Thank you," said Wukong. "Now we must free my friends!"

> "We have to be quiet," said the dragon. "The demon is sleeping."

  Wukong nodded.

## ⑦

  Then he turned toward the cymbals and raised his iron bar. The cymbals shattered.

> "You did that?" cried the dragon.

  Wukong shrugged.

> "I was angry."

  The demon stormed into the room.

> "Sun Wukong! How did you escape? Who are you?"

## ⑧

> "How do you know my name?" demanded Wukong.

> "My name is Yellow Brows," said the demon. "I've been waiting for you and the Tang Monk to pass by my mountain."

  Wukong narrowed his eyes.

> "Why?"

## ⑨

> "I'm a Buddhist," said the demon. "I plan to go to the Western Paradise and get the true scriptures myself."

> "You can't do that!" shouted Wukong.

  The monkey and the dragon charged toward the demon. The demon pulled a cloth from his robe and threw it. The cloth wrapped around Wukong and the dragon. And then it became very small.

## ⑩

  Soon they were tied up in a room. The Tang Monk, Bajie and Wujing were also in the room. Their bags were lying in a corner.

> "I'm sorry for not believing you, Wukong," said the Tang Monk. "You suspected this place was dangerous."

> "Don't worry about that, Master," said the monkey.

## ⑪

> "We must escape as soon as the demon goes back to sleep. He plans to get the true scriptures himself."

  The group waited for a while. Finally, Wukong made himself tiny and slipped out from his ropes. He untied his companions and the dragon.

> "Let's go," the monkey whispered. "Bajie, get the bags."

## ⑫

> "Everyone, be quiet," the dragon warned.

  They tiptoed toward the monastery door and were soon outside. Wukong looked at Bajie and frowned.

> "Where are the bags?"

  The pig moaned.

## ⑬

> "Ah, I forgot them."

> "Master's special robes are in one of those bags," Wukong sighed. "Wait here. I'll go back and get them."

  The monkey quietly hurried back to the room and grabbed the bags. But as he was leaving, he tripped and knocked over a statue.

## ⑭

  Then, from another room, Yellow Brows shouted.

> "What was that?"

# 🇨🇳 第八十一章：龙王相助

## ①

  八戒和悟净挥舞着武器向妖怪打去。

  哈！妖怪大笑。

> "你们根本不是我的对手！"

  在铙钹里面，悟空听到了打斗声。他听到喘息声和兵器碰撞的声音。

> "不！"唐僧喊道。

## ②

  随后一片寂静。

> "外面发生什么了？"悟空大喊。

  没有回应。猴子拼命捶打踹踢铙钹内壁好几个时辰，但那对铙钹纹丝不动。

> "太糟糕了，"他自言自语，"我怎么才能出去呢？"

## ③

  猴子正要再次猛击铙钹，忽然听到一个声音。

> "悟空，你在里面吗？"

> "你是谁？"悟空问。

> "我是金龙，"那声音说，"玉帝派我来帮你。我把角塞进铙钹缝里，把它们撬开。"

## ④

> "试试吧，"悟空说。

  慢慢地，龙角钻了进来。但铙钹仍然紧紧合拢。悟空呻吟了一声。

> "我出不去。"

> "也许我们还有别的办法把铙钹撬开，"龙说。

## ⑤

> "其实我有个别的主意，"猴子说。

  悟空把铁棒变成了一把钻。猴子在龙角上钻了一个洞。然后他变得极小，爬进了洞里。

> "现在把角抽出来！"悟空说。

## ⑥

  龙用力拉拽，终于把角抽了出来。猴子跳出洞口，恢复了正常大小。

> "谢谢你，"悟空说，"现在我们必须救出朋友们！"

> "我们要轻一点，"龙说，"妖怪睡着了。"

  悟空点了点头。

## ⑦

  然后他转向铙钹，举起了铁棒。铙钹 shattered了。

> "是你干的？"龙惊叫。

  悟空耸了耸肩。

> "我很生气。"

  妖怪冲进了房间。

> "孙悟空！你怎么逃出来的？你是谁？"

## ⑧

> "你怎么知道我的名字？"悟空质问道。

> "我叫黄眉，"妖怪说，"我一直在等你和唐僧路过我的山。"

  悟空眯起了眼睛。

> "为什么？"

## ⑨

> "我是佛门弟子，"妖怪说，"我打算自己去西天取经。"

> "你休想！"悟空大喊。

  猴子和龙向妖怪冲去。妖怪从袍子里掏出一块布扔了出去。布裹住了悟空和龙，然后变得极小。

## ⑩

  很快他们被绑在一间屋里。唐僧、八戒和悟净也在屋里。行李包扔在角落里。

> "对不起，悟空，我不该不相信你，"唐僧说，"你早就怀疑这地方不对劲。"

> "别放在心上，师父，"猴子说。

## ⑪

> "等妖怪睡着我们就必须逃出去。他打算自己去取经。"

  大家等了好一会儿。终于，悟空把自己变得极小，从绳索中溜了出来。他解开了同伴们和龙的绳子。

> "走吧，"猴子低声说，"八戒，拿上行李。"

## ⑫

> "大家别出声，"龙警告道。

  他们蹑手蹑脚走向寺院大门，很快到了外面。悟空看着八戒，皱起了眉头。

> "行李呢？"

  八戒哼哼了一声。

## ⑬

> "哎，我忘拿了。"

> "师父的锦襕袈裟就在其中一个包里，"悟空叹道，"你们等着，我回去拿。"

  猴子悄悄跑回房间，抓起行李包。但就在他离开的时候，他绊了一跤，碰倒了一尊雕像。

## ⑭

  然后，从另一个房间里传来黄眉的大喊。

> "那是什么声音？"
