# Journey to the West · Chapter Eighty-Five: Magic Bells

## ①

  Outside the cave, Wukong shouted. He shouted all kinds of insults about the Demon of Power.

  Inside the cave, the guard woke up. He ran to the demon's bedroom.

> "Master! The monkey is back!" cried the guard.

> "It's the middle of the night," said the demon. "What does he want?"

## ②

> "He said we must return the Queen!" said the guard. "If we don't, he'll smash our entire mountain! He also yelled some terrible things about you!"

  Ah! The Demon of Power climbed out of bed and grabbed three bells.

> "I'll blow that monkey away with my fire, smoke, and sand again!"

  She tied the bells around her neck and went outside.

## ③

  The demon glared at Wukong.

> "Go away! I'm sleeping!"

> "Return the Queen!" shouted the monkey.

> "Never!" said the demon. She reached for the bells around her neck.

  Wukong pretended to be surprised.

> "Oh! I have bells just like those!"

## ④

  The demon squinted at the monkey.

> "I doubt your bells are like mine!"

> "Let me see them!"

  Wukong held up the bells. The demon peered at them for a moment.

> "Those bells do look like mine! But my bells are magic!"

> "So am I," said Wukong. "My bells can make fire, smoke, and sand!"

## ⑤

  The demon smiled.

> "That's interesting!"

  Wukong smiled.

> "My bells can do that too."

> "Really?" The Demon of Power looked surprised. "Let's compare bells to see whose work better."

> "Okay," said Wukong. "You go first."

## ⑥

> "Ah! I was planning to!" said the demon.

  He shook his bells. Nothing happened.

  He shook the bells again. Again, nothing happened.

> "That's strange," the demon mumbled. He looked closely at the bells. "These bells work fine yesterday!"

  Wukong tried not to laugh.

> "I'm sorry. Let's see if my bells work," he said.

## ⑦

  The monkey shook the bells. Whoosh! Fire, smoke, and sand rushed towards the demon.

> "Ah!" cried the demon. He covered his face and fell to the ground.

  Wukong pulled out his iron bar and got ready to strike.

> "Wukong, stop!" shouted a voice.

## ⑧

  The monkey looked up and saw Guanyin coming down from the sky. Wukong bowed.

> "Greetings, Bodhisattva. I was just going to destroy this demon."

> "You must not harm him," said Guanyin. "He's my pet wolf. He escaped from heaven."

## ⑨

  The Bodhisattva looked at the demon.

> "Return to your true form."

  Light sparkled, and the demon turned into a huge wolf. Guanyin climbed onto the wolf's back. She looked at the wolf's neck and then at Wukong.

> "Give me the bells, Wukong."

## ⑩

> "What bells?" Wukong held the bells behind his back.

> "My wolf wears a set of three bells," said Guanyin. "I know you have them. You could not have defeated the wolf without them."

  Wukong shrugged.

> "I have no idea what you're—"

> "Hand them over," said Guanyin. "Or I'll recite the tight-fillet spell."

## ⑪

> "There's no need for that," cried the monkey. "I was just joking."

  Smiling sheepishly, he handed over the bells. Guanyin tied the bells around the wolf's neck and then rode up into the sky.

  Back at the palace, the King and Queen were reunited.

## ⑫

> "Thank you for rescuing the Queen, Sun Wukong," said the King.

> "Tonight, we will hold a feast to thank you and your companions," said the Queen.

  That night, the royal banquet hall was filled with people. Servants carried trays filled with rice, vegetables, mushrooms, noodles, tofu, dumplings, and other food.

## ⑬

  Bajie ate everything in front of him.

> "Would you like more steamed buns?" asked a servant.

> "You don't need to ask," said the pig. "Just put food in front of me and I'll eat it!"

# 🇨🇳 第八十五章：魔铃

## ①

  悟空在洞外大声叫骂，说了许多关于赛太岁的难听话。

  洞里面，守卫醒了过来。他跑到妖怪的卧室。

> "大王！那猴子又回来了！"守卫叫道。

> "深更半夜的，"妖怪说，"他想干什么？"

## ②

> "他说我们必须交出王后！"守卫说，"不然他就踏平我们的山！他还说了许多关于您的难听话！"

  赛太岁从床上爬起来，抓起三个铃铛。

> "我再用烟火沙把那猴子吹跑！"

  她把铃铛系在脖子上，走了出去。

## ③

  妖怪怒视着悟空。

> "滚开！我在睡觉！"

> "交出王后！"猴子大喊。

> "休想！"妖怪说。她伸手去摸脖子上的铃铛。

  悟空装作很惊讶。

> "哦！我的铃铛跟你的很像嘛！"

## ④

  妖怪斜眼看着猴子。

> "我怀疑你的铃铛跟我的不一样！"

> "让我看看！"

  悟空举起铃铛。妖怪仔细看了看。

> "看起来确实跟我的很像！但我的铃铛是魔法的！"

> "我的也是，"悟空说，"我的铃铛能放火、冒烟、起沙！"

## ⑤

  妖怪得意地笑了。

> "有意思！"

  悟空也笑了。

> "我的铃铛也能做到。"

> "真的？"赛太岁露出惊讶的表情，"那我们比比看谁的铃铛更厉害。"

> "好，"悟空说，"你先来。"

## ⑥

> "哼！我正有此意！"妖怪说。

  她摇动铃铛。什么也没发生。

  她又摇了摇铃铛。还是什么都没有。

> "奇怪了，"妖怪嘟囔着，仔细查看铃铛，"昨天还好好的呢！"

  悟空忍住不笑。

> "不好意思，让我来试试我的铃铛。"

## ⑦

  猴子摇了摇铃铛。呼！火焰、浓烟和黄沙朝妖怪扑去。

> "啊！"妖怪大叫。她捂住脸倒在地上。

  悟空掏出铁棒，准备痛打。

> "悟空，住手！"一个声音喊道。

## ⑧

  猴子抬头一看，观音菩萨从天而降。悟空鞠躬行礼。

> "菩萨您好。我正要消灭这个妖怪。"

> "你休伤他性命，"观音说，"他是我的坐骑金毛犼，从天上逃下来的。"

## ⑨

  菩萨看着妖怪。

> "现出原形吧。"

  光芒一闪，妖怪变成了一头巨大的狼形犼。观音骑上犼背，看了看犼的脖子，又看向悟空。

> "把铃铛给我，悟空。"

## ⑩

> "什么铃铛？"悟空把铃铛藏在背后。

> "我的犼脖子上戴着一串三个铃铛，"观音说，"我知道在你那里。没有铃铛，你不可能打败他。"

  悟空耸了耸肩。

> "我不知道您说什么——"

> "交出来，"观音说，"不然我就念紧箍咒。"

## ⑪

> "别念别念！"猴子大叫，"我开玩笑的。"

  悟空不好意思地交出铃铛。观音把铃铛系回犼的脖子上，然后腾云而去。

  王宫中，国王和王后终于团聚了。

## ⑫

> "谢谢你救回王后，孙悟空，"国王说。

> "今晚我们设宴款待你和你的同伴们，"王后说。

  当晚，王宫宴会厅坐满了人。侍者们端着托盘，上面放满了米饭、蔬菜、蘑菇、面条、豆腐、饺子等美食。

## ⑬

  八戒狼吞虎咽地吃着眼前的一切。

> "客官，还要来点包子吗？"一个侍者问。

> "这还用问？"八戒说，"把吃的尽管端上来就行！"
