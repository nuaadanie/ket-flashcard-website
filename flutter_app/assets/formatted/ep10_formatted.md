# Journey to the West · Chapter Ten: The Powerful Sage

## ①

  The story begins in the heavenly court. The Jade Emperor sat upon his throne, furious.

> "How will we defeat Sun Wukong?" he asked.

  A general stepped forward.

> "Your Majesty," said the general, "I sent our entire army and they couldn't arrest him."

  Guanyin spoke up from among the immortals.

> "Perhaps we can ask the Sage of Plum Mountain to capture him," said Guanyin.

> "Yes," said the Jade Emperor. "Erlang is very powerful."

  He turned to the messenger.

> "Go to Plum Mountain. Ask Erlang if he will help us."

## ②

  Wukong stood in front of the waterfall with his cheering subjects.

> "My king, you have just defeated an entire army from heaven!" said one of the monkeys.

> "I hope the Jade Emperor has learned his lesson," said Wukong.

> "He and his army are no match for me!"

  A deep voice boomed from above. Wukong looked up. A large man was coming down from the sky. Some of the other monkeys ran and hid.

> "I am Erlang Shen," said the man. "The Jade Emperor sent me to arrest you."

  Wukong laughed.

> "The Jade Emperor's entire army couldn't defeat me. What do you think you're going to do?"

> "I am going to take you back to heaven," said Erlang. "And you will be punished."

  Wukong raised his iron bar and leaped into the air.

> "Come on!"

  Erlang blocked the bar with his sword and then struck back.

## ③

  Wukong jumped onto a cloud to get out of the way. Erlang leaped into the air. He shook his body and suddenly grew much bigger.

  Wukong laughed.

> "I can do that too!"

  He shook his body and grew just as big as Erlang. Clouds and wind blew as the two giants fought in the sky. Their weapons banged together through the night. They were still fighting when the sun rose the next morning.

  This fight could last forever, thought Wukong. I should try a different plan.

  Wukong struck the ground one last time, and then jumped fast down to earth. He turned himself into a sparrow and perched on a branch.

  Erlang will never find me now, thought Wukong.

  He looked up and saw a hawk.

  Danger! thought Wukong. Better run!

  Wukong dived into a stream. He turned himself into a fish and swam in the water. But then a crane flew over the stream. The crane swooped down at the water, its mouth wide open.

  Wukong shot out of the stream. He changed back into himself and ran down a hill.

> "I'll turn myself into a temple," he thought. "Then, if Erlang tries to enter, I'll bite him."

  Wukong's eyes became windows and his mouth became a door. He turned his tail into a flagpole.

## ④

  A moment later, Erlang came down the hill. When he saw the temple, he scratched his head.

> "Hmm," said Erlang. "I never saw a temple with a flagpole before. This must be Wukong. I'll kick in that door!"

  Oh no! thought Wukong. You'll break all my teeth!

  Wukong changed back into himself and ran. Erlang was right behind him.

## ⑤

  Up in heaven, the Jade Emperor, the Queen Mother, and Guanyin watched the chase. The Sage Laozi came over to them.

> "What's going on?" he asked, looking down.

> "That monkey, Sun Wukong, ate all the peaches from the garden," said the Jade Emperor. "Then he defeated our entire army. Erlang is trying to catch him now."

> "I can help," said Laozi.

  He took a metal ring off his arm. He aimed carefully and hurled it down to earth.

> "Go!"

  The ring hit Wukong on the head. The monkey felt dizzy for a moment and then fell to the ground. The powerful Sage had been captured.

# 🇨🇳 第十章：神通广大的神仙


## ①

  故事从天庭开始。玉皇大帝坐在宝座上，勃然大怒。

> "我们怎样才能打败孙悟空？"他问道。

  一位天将走上前来。

> "陛下，"天将说，"我派了整个天兵天将，都没能捉住他。"

  观音菩萨在众神仙中开口了。

> "也许我们可以请灌江口的二郎神来捉他，"观音说。

> "好，"玉皇大帝说，"二郎神法力高强。"

  他转向信使。

> "去灌江口，请二郎神来帮我们。"

## ②

  悟空站在瀑布前，猴儿们正在为他欢呼。

> "大王，您刚刚打败了天庭的整个军队！"一只猴子说。

> "希望玉皇大帝已经吸取了教训，"悟空说。

> "他和他的军队根本不是我的对手！"

  天空中传来一个洪亮的声音。悟空抬头一看，一个高大的人正从天而降。一些猴子吓得跑开躲了起来。

> "我是二郎神，"那人说，"玉皇大帝派我来捉拿你。"

  悟空大笑。

> "玉皇大帝的整个军队都打不过我。你以为你能怎么样？"

> "我要把你带回天庭，"二郎神说，"你将受到惩罚。"

  悟空举起金箍棒，纵身一跃。

> "来吧！"

  二郎神用三尖刀挡住了铁棒，随即反击。

## ③

  悟空跳上云头躲避。二郎神腾空而起，摇身一变，突然变得巨大无比。

  悟空大笑。

> "我也会这招！"

  他也摇身一变，变得和二郎神一样高大。云雾翻腾，狂风大作，两个巨人在天空中大战。兵器叮叮当当地碰撞，整整打了一夜。到了第二天太阳升起的时候，他们还在打。

  这样打下去永远没完没了，悟空想。我得换个法子。

  悟空朝地上猛击一棒，然后飞快地跳到下界。他摇身一变，变成一只麻雀，停在树枝上。

  二郎神这次肯定找不到我了，悟空想。

  他抬头一看，却看见一只老鹰。

  不好！悟空想。快跑！

  悟空一头扎进溪流里，变成一条鱼在水中游。可是，一只仙鹤飞到了溪流上空。仙鹤俯冲向水面，嘴张得大大的。

  悟空从溪水里窜出来，现出原形，朝山下跑去。

> "我变成一座土地庙吧，"他想，"等二郎神想进去的时候，我就一口咬住他。"

  悟空的眼睛变成了窗户，嘴巴变成了门，尾巴变成了一根旗杆。

## ④

  不一会儿，二郎神来到了山下。他看见这座庙，挠了挠头。

> "嗯？"二郎神说，"我还从没见过庙旗杆竖在后面的呢，这一定是孙悟空变的。看我砸烂他的门！"

  哎呀！悟空想。你会把我牙齿都打掉的！

  悟空现出原形就跑，二郎神紧紧追在后面。

## ⑤

  在天庭上，玉皇大帝、王母娘娘和观音正看着这场追逐。太上老君走了过来。

> "怎么回事？"他低头问道。

> "那只猴子孙悟空，吃光了蟠桃园里所有的桃子，"玉皇大帝说，"然后又打败了我们整个军队。现在二郎神正在捉他。"

> "我来帮忙，"老君说。

  他从手臂上取下一个金钢琢，瞄准了，朝下界扔了下去。

> "去吧！"

  金钢琢正中悟空的脑袋。猴子一阵头晕目眩，摔倒在地上。神通广大的大圣，终于被捉住了。
