# Journey to the West · Chapter Seventy-Five: Battle of the Wukongs

## ①

  Curious Ears spoke. The two Wukongs stared at it.

> "I know which of you is the fake Sun Wukong," said Curious Ears. "But I won't say who it is."

## ②

> "The fake Sun Wukong has all the same powers as the real one. If I point him out, he'll get angry. He could destroy the entire palace!"

> "It's true," said one Wukong. "I could destroy this whole palace if I wanted to."

> "Do you mean I could destroy it?" said the other Wukong.

> "No!" yelled the other. "I could!"

## ③

  The two Wukongs began swinging their iron bars at each other.

> "Please, stop!" cried the judge of the dead. "You'll destroy the whole palace!"

  The monkeys stopped.

> "But I can help you," said Curious Ears. "The Buddha will be able to see which of you is the real Wukong. And he's powerful enough to destroy the fake one."

## ④

> "Of course!" cried one Wukong. "Let's go see Buddha."

> "Oh, um, sure," said the other Wukong. He looked worried.

> "You're nervous," said the first Wukong. "That's because you know Buddha is going to see who you really are."

> "No!" shouted the other Wukong. He swung his iron bar furiously.

> "Get out!" cried the judge of the dead. "Take your battle somewhere else."

## ⑤

  The two Wukongs left the Land of Darkness. Clouds swirled and thundered as the two mighty spirits battled across the sky. They soon arrived in the Western Paradise. A guard rushed out of the Thunderclap Monastery.

> "Stop fighting immediately!" he called up to them. "Tell me who you are."

## ⑥

  The two Wukongs landed in front of the guard. The guard looked surprised.

> "Wukong! There are two of you."

> "Yes," said one of the Wukongs. "I must speak with Buddha right away."

  The other Wukong scratched his head.

> "If Buddha is busy, we can just come back another time."

  The first Wukong shot a look at the other.

> "No, we can't."

> "You may enter," said the guard. "But you must behave yourselves while inside."

## ⑦

  As the first Wukong started to enter the monastery, the other one backed away.

> "You are coming with me!" said the first Wukong.

  He grabbed the other monkey by the arm and dragged him into the monastery. In front of Buddha, the first Wukong bowed. The other Wukong hesitated a moment and then bowed also.

## ⑧

> "Greetings, Buddha," said the first Wukong. "I must ask for your help."

> "I know why you're here, Wukong," said Buddha. He looked at the other monkey. "And I know that you are a fake."

  The other Wukong gasped and then vanished.

## ⑨

  Buddha held a bowl up in the air and then placed it upside down on a table.

> "I've trapped the fake Wukong under this bowl," said Buddha.

  When Buddha lifted the bowl, a tiny monkey spirit lay on the table.

## ⑩

  Wukong leaned closer to see.

> "He was a monkey who became an evil spirit," said Buddha. "He wanted to be you because you're so powerful."

  Wukong laughed.

> "Many spirits want to be me."

## ⑪

  The monkey thanked Buddha and then flew back to Mount Potalaka. Guanyin decided it was time for Wukong to rejoin his companions on the journey. She and the monkey found the Tang monk and the others along the road to the west. The Bodhisattva told the Tang monk that it wasn't Wukong who attacked him.

> "You must keep Wukong with you," explained Guanyin.

> "Yes, Bodhisattva," said the Tang monk, bowing.

## ⑫

> "Thank you, Bodhisattva," said Wukong. "I'll make sure the Tang monk reaches the Western Paradise."

# 🇨🇳 第七十五章：真假猴王大决战

## ①

  谛听开口了。两个悟空盯着它。

> "我知道谁是假孙悟空，"谛听说，"可我不敢说。"

## ②

> "假孙悟空跟真的本事一样大。我要是当面指出来，他会发怒，说不定把整个宫殿都毁了！"

> "说得对，"一个悟空说，"我要想拆了这宫殿易如反掌。"

> "你说谁能拆？"另一个悟空说。

> "我！"另一个大喊。

## ③

  两个悟空又挥起铁棒打了起来。

> "别打了，求你们！"阎王大喊道，"会把整个地府全毁的！"

  猴子们停下手。

> "不过我可以帮你们，"谛听说，"佛祖能分辨你们谁是真悟空，而且他法力无边，足以降服假的。"

## ④

> "当然！"一个悟空叫道，"我们找佛祖去！"

> "行、好吧，"另一个悟空说，脸上露出一丝担忧。

> "你心虚了，"第一个悟空说，"因为你知道到了佛祖面前你就原形毕露了。"

> "才不是！"另一个悟空大喊，挥棒就打。

> "滚出去！"阎王大怒，"你们去别处打！"

## ⑤

  两个悟空离开了地府。两团云雾翻腾，电闪雷鸣，两个神通广大的妖精在空中激战，很快到了西天。一个守卫从雷音寺冲出来。

> "立刻住手！"他朝上大喊，"报上名来。"

## ⑥

  两个悟空落在守卫面前。守卫大吃一惊。

> "悟空！怎么有两个你？"

> "是的，"一个悟空说，"我要立刻见佛祖。"

  另一个悟空挠了挠头。

> "佛祖要是忙的话，我们改天再来也行。"

  第一个悟空瞪了他一眼。

> "不行，今天必须见。"

> "你们可以进去，"守卫说，"但在里面不许动手。"

## ⑦

  第一个悟空正要进寺，另一个想逃。

> "你跟我一起进去！"第一个悟空一把抓住他的胳膊，拖着进了雷音寺。在佛祖面前，第一个悟空行礼。另一个犹豫了一下，也施了礼。

## ⑧

> "佛祖在上，"第一个悟空说，"我来求您帮忙。"

> "悟空，我知道你为什么来，"佛祖说，目光转向另一个猴子，"我也知道你是个假货。"

  另一个悟空倒吸一口凉气，随即消失了。

## ⑨

  佛祖举起一个钵盂，然后倒扣在桌上。

> "我把假悟空扣在这个钵下面了，"佛祖说。

  佛祖揭开钵盂，桌上一只小猴妖趴在那里。

## ⑩

  悟空凑近来看。

> "他是一只修炼成精的猴子，"佛祖说，"因为你法力高强，他想取而代之。"

  悟空大笑。

> "想取代我的妖精还真不少。"

## ⑪

  悟空谢过佛祖，飞回了普陀山。观音觉得是时候让悟空回到取经队伍中了。她和猴子在西行路上找到了唐僧和众人。菩萨告诉唐僧，之前打他的那个并非真悟空。

> "你千万要留住悟空，"观音吩咐道。

> "是，菩萨。"唐僧恭敬地行礼。

## ⑫

> "谢谢菩萨，"悟空说，"我发誓一定保师父到达西天。"
