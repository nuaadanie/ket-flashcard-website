# Journey to the West · Chapter Two: The Water Curtain Cave

## ①

  The monkey splashed through the waterfall and landed in a large cave. His eyes opened wide. The cave was filled with bamboo, trees, and beautiful flowers. Moss covered the walls. A stream flowed through the cave, and glowing light and mist filled the air. The monkey found a long table with bowls, cups, and plates, and a fireplace against one wall. Deeper in the cave, the monkey found bedrooms with cozy beds.
  Outside, the other monkeys and apes were looking at the waterfall.
  > "Do you think he made it through?" asked one monkey.

## ②

  > "No, I don't think so," said another monkey. "He probably got washed away down the river."
  Suddenly, with a splash, the monkey shot out of the waterfall and landed in front of the group.
  > "Your lives!" said the monkey, laughing. "Yes, and we are very lucky! I found a perfect home for all of us. Follow me!"
  The monkey jumped back through the water. Some of the monkeys scratched their heads.
  > "It still seems dangerous," said one monkey.
  > "Let's go!"
  He leaped through the waterfall. After that, another monkey jumped through. Then more followed. Soon all of the monkeys and apes were inside the cave. Everyone ran around, marveling at all the wonderful things.
  > "This cave is beautiful!" said one of the monkeys.
  Some of the monkeys and apes began fighting and arguing. They grabbed plates and bowls from one another. Others fought over the bedrooms.
  > "I saw this bed first!" yelled one monkey.
  > "I did!" yelled another.
  Monkey watched his friends for a moment and then folded his arms.
  > "Stop!" he called. All the monkeys and apes stopped what they were doing and looked at him.
  > "I found this cave for us," said Monkey. "That means I am now your king. Why aren't you bowing to me?"
  > "He's right!" they bowed to Monkey and said, "All hail the Monkey King!" Everyone else bowed. "Hail the Monkey King!" they shouted.

## ③

  They cheered. Many years went by. During the day, the monkeys and apes played in the forest and the stream. Every night they slept safely in their cave. One night the monkeys and apes held a huge feast.

## ④

  Fruit and nuts covered the table. The king's subjects danced and sang while music played. Monkey sat on his throne and smiled. But then his smile faded. Soon he was frowning.

## ⑤

  One of the monkeys rushed over.
  > "Your Majesty," he said, "you look sad. What's wrong?"

## ⑥

  Monkey sighed. "Our life here is perfect."
  > "Yes, that's true," said the monkey. "So why are you sad?"

## ⑦

  Monkey was quiet for a moment. Then he said, "Someday our lives will end. I wish this perfect life could last forever."
  The monkey's eyes lit up.

## ⑧

  > "Oh," said the monkey. "You want to live forever?"
  > "I do," said Monkey.

## ⑨

  The monkey rubbed his chin.
  > "Then you must study with a sage," he said. "Sages are very wise people. They can transform themselves into other animals. They can fly through the sky."

## ⑩

  > "And they know how to live forever," he added.
  Monkey smiled again.
  > "Where can I find a sage?" he asked.
  > "You must find a land with people," said the monkey. "That's where you'll find a sage."

## ⑪

  The next morning, Monkey built a raft and loaded food onto it. He pushed the raft into the stream and jumped on. As the raft began to float away, Monkey waved to his friends.

## ⑫

  > "Don't worry," he called. "After I learn how to live forever, I will return."

# 🇨🇳 第二章：水帘洞天

## ①

  猴子溅着水穿过瀑布，落进一个巨大的洞穴，双眼圆睁。洞穴里长满了竹子、树木和美丽的花朵。苔藓覆盖着岩壁。一条溪流穿洞而过，光芒与雾气弥漫空中。猴子发现一张长桌，摆着碗、杯、盘子，壁炉靠墙。洞穴深处，猴子发现了带有舒适床铺的卧室。

## ②

  外面，众猴正望着瀑布。
  > "你觉得他成功了吗？"一只猴子问。
  > "不，不会吧，"另一只猴子说，"他可能被河水冲走了。"
  突然，噗通一声，猴子从瀑布中射出，落在群猴面前。
  > "各位，"猴子笑着说道，"是的，我们非常幸运！我为全体同胞找到了一个完美的家园。跟我来！"
  猴子跳回水中。有些猴子挠了挠头。
  > "还是很危险，"一只猴子说。
  > "我们走！"
  于是，又有猴子跳了进去。接着，更多猴子跟上。很快，所有猴子、猿猴都进了洞穴。大家四处奔跑，欣赏着奇妙的一切。
  > "这个洞穴真美，"某只猴子说。
  一些猴子、猿猴开始争吵打闹，互相抢夺盘碗；另一些则为卧室争执。
  > "这张床是我先发现的，"一只猴子嚷道。
  > "明明是我，"另一只喊道。
  > "是我的！"
  猴子观察朋友们一会儿，然后双臂交叉，大喝一声。所有猴子、猿猴停下动作，望着他。
  > "这个洞穴是我为你们找到的，"猴子说，"从今往后，我就是你们的大王。还不行礼吗？"
  > "他说得对！"他们向猴子鞠躬，喊道："全体参见猴王！"其他人也跟着行礼："参见大王！"

## ③

  他们齐声欢呼。许多年过去了。白天，猴子们在森林和溪流中嬉戏，每晚都安全地睡在洞穴里。一天晚上，猴子们举行了一场盛大的宴会。

## ④

  水果和坚果铺满了桌子。猴王的臣子们随着音乐跳舞歌唱。猴王坐在宝座上微笑着，但笑容渐渐消失，很快皱起了眉头。

## ⑤

  某只猴子急忙上前。
  > "陛下，"他说，"您看起来不开心。怎么了？"

## ⑥

  猴王叹了口气。
  > "我们的生活在这里很完美。"
  > "确实如此，"那只猴子说，"那您为什么伤心？"

## ⑦

  猴王沉默片刻，然后说："总有一天我们的生命会终结。我希望这完美的生活能永远持续。"那只猴子的眼睛亮了起来。
  > "哦，"他说，"你想永生吗？"
  > "是的，"猴王说。

## ⑧

  那只猴子摸了摸下巴。
  > "那你必须去拜师学艺。仙人非常有智慧，能变化形态，还能在空中飞翔。"

## ⑨

  > "他们还懂得如何获得永生。"
  猴王再次微笑。
  > "我在哪里能找到仙人？"他问。
  > "你必须去有人烟的地方，"那只猴子说。"那才是你能找到仙人的地方。"

## ⑩

## ⑪

  第二天一早，猴王扎了一个木筏，装满食物。他把木筏推入溪流，跳了上去。木筏漂离时，猴王向朋友们挥手告别。

## ⑫

  > "别担心，"他喊道，"等我学会永生之术，我一定会回来。"
