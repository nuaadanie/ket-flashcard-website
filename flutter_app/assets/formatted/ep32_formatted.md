# Journey to the West · Chapter Thirty-Two: Lady Whitebone

## ①

  Lady Whitebone sat by the road and sniffed the air again.

> "The Tang monk has some powerful companions," she said. "I need a disguise."

  Bajie dropped the bags.

> "I'm tired and hungry. Let's stop for lunch."

  Using his magic sight, Wukong saw a peach orchard thousands of miles away.

> "I'll be back in a minute," he said.

  Soon after the monkey had left, a young woman came down the road. She held out a bowl.

> "Are you the Tang monk?" she asked. "I have food for you."

  Bajie reached for the bowl.

> "Thank you!"

## ②

  "Journey to the West." Lady Whitebone sat by the road. Wukong dropped all the peaches and pulled out his iron bar. *Whack!* He struck at the young woman. But before he hit her, she disappeared.

> "Why did you do that?" cried the Tang monk.

> "She's not human, Master," said Wukong. "You couldn't see that the woman was really a spirit who wanted to harm you. Unfortunately, she got away."

> "Why are you so cruel? She was giving us food!"

> "It's not food," said the monkey. "See?"

  He held up the bowl, which was filled with worms.

## ③

> "Don't believe Wukong," said Bajie. "He used magic to turn the food into worms."

  The pig looked at the ground where the woman had been and shook his head. Wukong hit her so hard, there wasn't anything left of her.

> "This is ridiculous!" said Wukong.

  The monkey rolled on the ground as the Tang monk recited the Tight Headband Spell.

> "I didn't know you could do that, Master!" Bajie laughed.

## ④

  Just then, an old woman came down the road. The monk stopped reciting the spell and Wukong stood up.

> "I'm looking for my daughter," said the woman. "She was bringing food to the Tang monk."

> "I'm so sorry," said the Tang monk.

  Bajie stepped forward.

> "You should ask this monkey what happened to your daughter."

  Wukong looked at the old woman closely for a moment. Then he took out his iron bar and swung. But again he missed.

> "She got away again!" cried Wukong.

> "He killed another innocent person!" cried the pig. "Master, recite the spell!"

## ⑤

  The Tang monk recited the spell again. Wukong fell to the ground in pain.

> "Please stop!" cried the monkey.

  The Tang monk stopped.

> "Don't hurt any more people," he said. "If you do, I'll recite the spell until your head explodes."

  Just then, an old man came down the road.

> "I'm looking for my wife and daughter," he said.

> "That's the evil spirit again," thought Wukong. "If I don't destroy her, she'll harm the Tang monk. But if I do hurt her, the monk will make my head explode."

## ⑥

  Wukong sighed.

> "I must protect the Tang monk no matter what," he thought.

  Wukong took out his iron bar and swung. There was a puff of smoke. When the smoke cleared, a skeleton lay on the ground.

> "I got her!" cried Wukong. "See? She was an evil skeleton spirit."

## ⑦

  The Tang monk frowned. He recited the spell again.

> "Master, I want you to leave Wukong," said the Tang monk.

> "Master?" said the monkey. "I just saved your life."

> "I don't believe you," said the monk. "Now leave, or I'll recite the spell again."

  Wukong became angry.

> "If you really want me to leave, I'll leave. But don't come looking for me when you need help."

  He leaped into the air and vanished.

# 🇨🇳 第三十二章：白骨夫人


## ①

  白骨精坐在路边，又嗅了嗅空气。

> "唐僧有几个厉害的同伴，"她说，"我得变身伪装一下。"

  八戒把行李丢在地上。

> "我又累又饿，咱们停下来吃午饭吧。"

  悟空用法眼一看，看到千里之外有一片桃林。

> "我马上回来，"他说。

  猴子走后不久，一个年轻女子从路上走来。她端着一只碗。

> "您是唐僧吗？"她问，"我给您带了些吃的。"

  八戒伸手去接碗。

> "谢谢！"

## ②

  悟空丢下所有桃子，掏出铁棒，抡了过去。砰！他朝年轻女子打去。可还没打着，她就消失了。

> "你为什么这样做？"唐僧叫道。

> "她不是人，师父，"悟空说，"您看不出来这个女人其实是个要害您的妖怪。可惜让她跑了。"

> "你怎么这么狠心？人家是给我们送饭来的！"

> "那才不是什么饭，"猴子说，"你看？"

  他举起碗，里面装满了蛆虫。

## ③

> "别信悟空的，"八戒说，"他是用法术把食物变成了虫子。"

  八戒瞅了瞅那女人刚才站立的地方，摇了摇头。悟空下手太重，地上什么都没留下。

> "太不像话了！"悟空说道。

  唐僧念起了紧箍咒，悟空在地上打起滚来。

> "师父，没想到您还有这一手！"八戒大笑。

## ④

  正在这时，一个老太太从路上走来。唐僧停了咒语，悟空站了起来。

> "我在找我女儿，"老太太说，"她给唐僧送饭去了。"

> "实在抱歉，"唐僧说。

  八戒走上前来。

> "你该问问这猴子，他把你女儿怎么样了。"

  悟空仔细端详了老太太一会儿，掏出铁棒打了过去。可是又一次打空了。

> "她又跑了！"悟空叫道。

> "他又杀了一个无辜的人！"八戒叫道，"师父，念咒！"

## ⑤

  唐僧又念起了咒语。悟空疼得趴在了地上。

> "求您别念了！"猴子叫道。

  唐僧停了下来。

> "不许再伤害人了，"他说，"再这样，我就念咒念到你脑袋炸开为止。"

  正在这时，一个老头从路上走来。

> "我在找我老伴和女儿，"他说。

> "又是那个妖怪！"悟空心想，"我不除了她，她就会害师父。可我要是打她，师父就会念咒念得我头痛欲裂。"

## ⑥

  悟空叹了口气。

> "无论如何我得保护师父，"他想。

  悟空取出铁棒打过去。升起了一股烟雾。烟雾散去后，地上躺着一具白骨。

> "终于打败她了！"悟空叫道，"看到了吧？她是个白骨精！"

## ⑦

  唐僧皱起了眉头。他又念起了咒语。

> "悟空，我要你走，"唐僧说。

> "师父？"猴子说，"我刚刚救了您的命啊。"

> "我不信你，"唐僧说，"马上走人，不然我又要念咒了。"

  悟空大怒。

> "既然你真要赶我走，那我就走。不过以后你们遇到麻烦可别来找我。"

  他纵身一跃，消失在天际。
